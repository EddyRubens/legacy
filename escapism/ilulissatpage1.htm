There was no Stig Nielsen on board of this vessel. But I did meet a Dutch guy called Jaco. He grew up in the same village as I did! His best friend turned out to be the son of my parent's neighbours. What a coincidence!  I talked with Jaco about our place of birth, the local schools we attended and about all the other places and people we remembered. It felt so strange. There I was, in the country of my dreams, talking about a childhood that seemed to be as far away as possible! It didn’t feel real. If I had talked with a Chinese farmer about my childhood in the rice fields, I would have believed that to be true as well. But it didn’t frighten me. On the contrary. This non-reality, this state of dreaming felt very close to euphoria.
