Magdalena’s niece (or is she a granddaughter?) gave me her newspaper when she finished reading it. Björk is on the cover. She is here in Greenland as well! She gathered girls from all over Greenland to compile a choir that will accompany her on her world tour. That will certainly become the experience of a lifetime for these girls, who have rarely left their village.

I asked the niece if she likes Björk. She had never heard of Björk before. But she knows almost every other girl on the picture: Arnannguaq, Nuka and Regine from Sisimiut, Pilu from Aasiaat and Amalie, Maannguaq, Ida, Najaaraq and that girl on the right from Nuuk.
