In June 2001 I exposed Journey to the North - adventures of an Avatar in Las Palmas in Rotterdam. This exhibition was part of my M.F.A.-exams. 

A room of 6 by 6 meters was fitted up in the tradition of a museum of natural history with a bit of fake snow on the floor. 
I papered the walls with wooden design wallpaper. Several attributes and relics were shown inside wooden showcases and a beamer projected the Internet site on a wall. Many visitors took a seat behind a wooden table to visit the site and some of them mailed a response. With this installation I managed to expose my dream world outside the computer as well. That way people who were too computer phobic to view the site were still able to tell the project was about a journey in a cold area. And sometimes they even doubted the genuineness.
