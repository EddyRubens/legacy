I met him at the youth hostel. He is here to see the ice, like everyone. I told him that I wanted to leave Ilulissat as soon as possible. In all probability I will leave tomorrow morning for a hike along the ice fjord. It will be a couple of days walking. According to my map, it must be a very interesting hike.

That evening we made some pasta together and went out for a drink. Ulrik said he would love to 
hike the same route but didn’t dare to do it alone. “You can always join me.” I answered spontaneously.
