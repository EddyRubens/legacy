It has been a turbulent year, the year of my Journey to the North. I never thought the impact of this project could be so extensive!

Living a second life took a lot of time and energy and it interfered with my first life more than I could have imagined. Sometimes I wasn’t so sure about my identity during Adventures of an Avatar. Were the emotions I felt my own or my avatar's? Why did I take some e-mail messages so personally when they were addressed to my avatar?

I was very glad I could finally travel to Greenland in reality. A dream I had for a long time finally came true. Several people warned me against a disappointment, but I got more then I dared to hope for.

Back in the Netherlands, I started The real escapism as soon as possible. Now I was reporting about my real journey in the same way as my avatar's. After being back home for a month , my memories from Greenland seemed to feel as real or unreal as my avatar's adventures. Maybe because I handled them the same way as I handled my fantasies.
