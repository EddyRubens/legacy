A less pleasant happening are the funerals. There have been four funerals this week. The first was for a sixteen-year-old girl who committed suicide. She shot herself through her hart with her father's hunting-rifle. Then there was another suicide. And a homicide the day after that. The murderer tried to kill himself afterwards, but failed. He is still in the intensive care. And there was also a natural death. All in one week! I am glad to hear this is not normal. This is the average for a whole year. These funerals cause a strange atmosphere. Who will be next?
