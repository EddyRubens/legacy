There is no Björk in Ilulissat. She left two days ago. Perhaps that’s better than meeting her.

My disappointment is not because of Björk. Ilulissat just isn’t as nice as Sisimiut. The scenery is absolutely beautiful and the weather is also very nice. But the atmosphere doesn't compare to a town like Sisimiut, where the people are so friendly.

The enormous icebergs here attract a lot of people. Most of them are tourists. Even the Greenlanders spend their holiday here. I don’t think I will find a Magdalena or a Gaba here.
