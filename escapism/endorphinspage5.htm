Never thought that Greenland could break me so easily. I’m still a bit shocked by my near death experience. For the first time since I’ve been here I feel lonely. And vulnerable.

A huge disaster could be waiting for me on every hillside. The musk oxen can be very angry if you come too close. What if there is a gigantic bull on the other side of this hill? Maybe he smells me already, but I probably wouldn't see him before it would be too late. Last summer musk oxen killed one Canadian and two Germans.

And then there are those annoying mosquitoes! They are the real hunters! They’re black and they are with so many of them that they drive you insane. They bite wherever they can, right through your trousers and one millimeter beside the DEET. I’ve been told oxen and reindeers can get so roused that they just give in. They’ll lie down and wait for the mosquitoes to drink them to death.
