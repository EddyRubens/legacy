I can imagine how red this bay was coloured whenever my ancestors skinned a whale. No wonder they named this settlement Rodebay. I would love to travel back in time to see how they lived. Were they happy to be here or were they only into it for the money? How much Dutch blood is mixed with the Greenlanders'?

I am afraid those whalers weren’t very gracious all the time, but the inhabitants from nowadays are very friendly. Even when they learn I’m Dutch.
