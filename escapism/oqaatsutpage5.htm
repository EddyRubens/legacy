Suddenly I almost believed in miracles. I was lying on some big rocks near the water, wondering what I could do these last moments in Greenland. Images I once saw on television of women cleaning sealskins were back in my mind. Those images
developed my interest in Greenland many years ago. How I would love to clean one of those skins myself! I fell asleep for some minutes and when I woke up a piece of sealskin had washed ashore! Whoever made this dream come true: thank you! I sprinted to the local shop to buy an ulo. Cleaning the skin turned out to be much harder than I had seen on television.
