Unfortunately, all things come to an end. And so did my journey. A cargo boat brought me back to Ilullisat and from there I took a plane to Copenhagen. I walked around the city till five o’clock in the morning. I just couldn’t sleep. Here it gets dark during the night and here there are trees. Trees are absolutely beautiful but in Copenhagen they made me weep. They told me it was over now. I’m home, but a part of me will always be in the far north.
