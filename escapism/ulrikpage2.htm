So I’m going to spend several days with a person I don’t know at all! We will be totally alone and entirely dependent upon each other. What if this man is not so friendly as he seems to be? What if he turns out to be very annoying and irritating? I’m getting doubts about my invitation. It will be my first time to walk with someone else and I’m not sure I’ll be good company. I know I can be very grumbly when I’m tired! Perhaps he’ll walk so fast I can’t keep up. Or maybe he doesn’t like to rest as often as I do. It might be wise to talk this over with Ulrik before we depart. And reserve the possibility to split up during the trip if one of us wants to.

My doubts are increasing now that he’s not here on time. We had planned to depart at eleven o’clock and there is still no sign of Ulrik at noon! Why isn’t he here? In Greenland even two o’clock will be on time, so I hope he didn’t adopt this slackness! Finally he shows up. He was too late because he went to the hairdresser...
