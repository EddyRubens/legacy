We had been climbing a whole day. The altitude wasn’t spectacular, but the views were still fabulous! From here we could see the whole ice fjord and the inland ice. Nothing could spoil our sensation, except those damned mosquitoes. 
Ulrik had a wonderful time as well and therefore he asked me to go on a helicopter trip. That way we would have the best view and we would even land on the inland ice! It was nice of him to ask me, but I couldn’t accept it. Those trips are very expensive, although he said it was no problem for him to pay for two.  I was a bit afraid he might be hoping for a romance so I refused. I went for a sailing trip at the ice fjord instead.
