It is difficult to experience the ancient Greenland culture because western influences are everywhere. Sometimes the two worlds unite perfectly and bring improvement, but mostly their combination is afflicted. Some colours just don’t mix.

On trying to come closer to Magdalena and her history, I sew skins. I can sit in my tent for hours, doing this needlework. It makes me feel calm and harmonious. Of course my tent isn’t an igloo and of course I don’t sew to survive, but I think I do feel some of the old spirits. After having made three small bags, the canvas is covered with white hair. It looks as if a polar bear has paid me a visit.
