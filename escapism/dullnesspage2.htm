Most of the women here are absolutely gorgeous. It doesn’t matter if they have Inuit or Danish ancestors; they all have such pretty faces! Perhaps living among nature makes you look like this. Or maybe it is the daily diet of fresh fish. Whatever their secret is, it works out differently for the Greenland men. They look rather funny and they behave like children. At the airport  there are several television sets. The screens are suspended from the ceiling to provide flight information, but the men prefer to watch television. Once I saw two men changing the channel back and forth, because they both wanted to see a different programme. One of them had just turned his programme on again and went back to his seat. Before his butt touched the chair, the other man had already changed it back to his favorite channel. They didn’t get angry or irritated or anything; they just kept changing the channel back and forth over a hundred times!
