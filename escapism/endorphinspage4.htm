Perhaps it wasn’t a good idea to continue walking  immediately after this holistic experience. I thought I could handle everything and felt one with nature. The opposite turned out to be the truth.
With the glacier less than 300 meters behind me, I almost killed myself. I was following a small, vague track that led into a very steep incline and I realized this too late.

My backpack was so heavy that I would definitely fall down if I tried to turn back. Going further was no option either, because there, it was getting even steeper than it already was. Going up was the only way to get out of this miserable situation. I was very afraid and even thought about dropping my backpack, because the heavy weight of it would surely make me drown if I would happen to fall into the water. Was it a smart idea to unbind the backpack? Or would this only get me out of balance? After taking some deep breaths I decided to trust my abilities and climb up calmly. I felt so relieved when I reached a safe spot! All I wanted now was to return to civilization as soon as possible.
