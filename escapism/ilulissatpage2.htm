I was a bit impatient for the ship to depart because I love to sail, especially in the northern regions. But I also felt a bit sorry to leave Sisimiut.

Sailing is a good way to clear your mind and to prepare for new encounters. You are literally between two situations and you can recharge your batteries.

It didn’t take long before I saw the first iceberg. A gigantic white castle on it's way to the south. It took several hours before I saw more. But at four o’clock in the morning there were so many icebergs the ship had to maneuver it's way around them. Fantastic, how silently the ship finds it's way and how the breaking of ice against the hull every now and then interrupts this silence! Almost magical. I felt like the queen in a fairy tale.
