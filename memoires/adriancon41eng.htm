I saw a woman waiting but didn’t recognize her at first. I walked over to her and asked: “Are you Sonja?”. She turned and looked at me. “Adrian. Man, you look a mess!” I wasn’t feeling too good and I told her I was scared to death. “Some plan here to help us get out of the country!” Sonja told me how it all came about. How she got stuck in all this. A little while later Paul came back with Rosanne. We were ready to go.

When we arrived at the apartment building I looked up. It was terribly high. “I’m not up for this, but I’ll have to do it.” Paul took the stuff he needed from a locker in the hall and we got into an elevator. There was a strong wind on the roof. I put the parachute pack on my back and secured the straps around my legs as tight as I could. I wanted to be very sure that everything was in place. Rosanne noticed I was scared and said I didn’t have to do this, but I knew better. “We are in this together” was the only thing I could blurt out. And I purposely concealed the fact that I was jumping for Kodjiro’s sake. When all the preparations were made and everything was checked again we walked to the edge of the roof and grabbed Rosanne’s hand. I was scared to death. Paul and Sonja took the elevator back to the ground floor.

When we saw them standing there I looked at Rosanne (for the last time maybe). We nodded at each other and without saying a word we made the jump. The first pull wasn’t bad, but it just didn’t stop. I kept on falling and wished I hadn’t done it now. I wanted to go back to the safety of the roof. The ground moved towards me at a terrible speed. The wind tore at my limbs and clothes. “This has to stop, I’m falling to my death. The parachute isn’t working. This is the end.”
