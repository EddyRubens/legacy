I really enjoyed searching for the sender of my Valentine’s present. Sonja and I were behaving like two hilarious teenagers and that was sure a relief. Our idea with Robbert’s card was exactly the way we wanted it to happen. We did not find a handwriting resembling the poem. But that did not spoil our pleasure. It was Robbert himself who queered somebody’s pitch.

Just like Dr. Hamilton told me already a few days ago Robbert returned to do some more tests. Happy and cheerful he walked into the communion-room where we were watching TV with roughly eight people at the time. Felt deadly ashamed! Everybody could see that he was in high spirits far from depressive. Steve investigated about it and so he replied that he had not felt better ever before. He and his wife have had discussions during some nights and together they realized that it was much too early to get separated. By recalling somebody’s memories Robbert felt he could easily imagine other people’s feelings. He explained that he understands his wife much better now. Even plans had been made to make their honeymoon-trip over again within two months. Together for just one week in Egypt.

While Robbert told us this, many glances pointed my direction. I did not know how to look back. Sonja was in session at that moment so I was desperately left on my own. There was no excuse anymore then to get the card. Denying the whole matter just like that would be ridiculous. So I collected the giga-card. “Good to hear that you are doing fine in the meantime!”  I said while I was ready to give him the card. “We all have written you something wishing you all the best for the future. I realize now that it is not necessary anymore. Glad for you. I will give it to you anyway. On behalf of all test subjects of Braintec.” It was very clear that Robbert did not understand what to do with this card. However he thanked us kindly and his eyes rushed over the texts quickly. Then it was time for him to do some tests. I accompanied him up to the laboratory like he could not manage without any escort. It seemed to me the easiest way to avoid possible questions of other test subjects.
