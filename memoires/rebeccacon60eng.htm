"How?..." was all I could manage to say. One of the Agents, who was sitting on the left end of the 'U' shaped booth, stood up and stood behind me and Carmen. He put one of his hands on each of our backs and quietly said, "Now don't make a scene or people will look. If they look, ... they are likely to remember us if anyone were to ask. Just sit down and we'll talk."

Carmen slid into the booth first then me. After I slid in next to Carmen the Agent who was standing, sat down next to me. He spoke again, "Now let's get introductions out of the way. My name is Agent David Andrews, that man over there (he pointed at the other Agent) is my good friend and partner of thirteen years, Agent Marcus Jacobs."  Agent Jacobs nodded his head once as a 'Hello.' I returned the nod. Then Agent Andrews continued, "And I am sure you are familiar with these two people."

I could hold it in no longer. "Oh, I am so happy to see you!" The two unexpected guests were Josh and Diego. I wanted to slide under the table and come back up next to Josh, but I knew that would cause a scene.  Carmen was sitting next to Diego and they were already hugging. Agent Jacobs stood up and said, "Gentlemen..." Josh gave me a smile and mouthed the words 'I love you.' Then he and Diego slid out of the booth and went to the bar with Agent Jacobs. Agent Andrews, who seemed to be the talker of the two agents said, "We felt an obligation to contact your spouses because of the seriousness of this matter. We have only told them minor details of what has happened." He shared with us that when they contacted Josh and Diego, they told them that there were 'Internal Government Issues' happening at Braintec. They know that there is a 'criminal at large', (Dr. Tachikawa) but they don't know who it is or what it relates to. Apparently, All they know is that Carmen and I are in some sort of trouble but they don't know why or how.

"I feel it is up to the two of you to let them know what is going on." Agent Andrew said. "You can tell them as little or as much as you feel comfortable telling them. They have both agreed to stay in our custody, not as criminals, but as... (He paused as though he was trying to think of a good term) ...'Citizen Agents.' We are going to allow you to have half an hour to talk with them alone. Then they will leave with another Agent waiting at a disclosed location. He will arrive, pick them up and return our safehouse. Then Agent Jacobs and I will discuss a plan we have set out for the two of you."
