The Agents arranged for two private tables. Josh and I were at one of them and Carmen and Diego were at the other. Josh reached his hands across the table and held my hands. I wanted to start crying because I felt like I had let him down somehow. All I could manage to say at first was, "Josh... I'm so sorry!"

Then I did start to cry. He squeezed my hands and said, "Honey, what's done is done. Let's just try to fix whatever is happening. I want you to come home." I knew then that I could tell Josh everything that has happened and he would do whatever he could to help make it right. I did my best to fit in the past few months into a half hour conversation. I told him everything about Dr. Hamilton, Sheila, Carmen, Megan, Kaleb, Kasey, and the mean Dr. Tachikawa. I told him about the uploads the extraction, Kathy freaking out, how I think they messed up on Davel; and all the crazy things he has done, Dr. Hamilton's diamond bracelet, and everything else I could think of. I could tell by the look on his face that he was overwhelmed with information. He said that he wished that I had emailed him when all this was happening. "I could have helped you so much sooner!" He said. "Josh,..." I said. "You don't understand. I was worried that you would be angry with me for getting involved in something like this. Then, when I decided I would write you about this place, bad things happened. I was afraid that if I involved you, something bad might happen to you. You don't know what Dr. Tachikawa is like. You don't know what he is capable of! I would never forgive myself if something bad happened to you Josh, never!" "We're going to make things right." Josh said.  "I'm going to do whatever it takes to catch this bastard and make sure he's locked up forever. Your my girl, and no one messes with my girl!" He was starting to get mad. I told him that getting angry was not going to help things. He agreed and told me I was right, but I could see he was still boiling inside.

Agent Andrews came over to the table and told us to wrap things up. We both stood up and hugged. I didn't want him to let go. I felt so safe in his arms, but it had to end. Before he walked away, I made him promise not to tell Diego anything I told him. "I don't know how much Carmen is going to tell him." I said. "He needs to find things out from her when she's ready...okay?" He smiled and said, "Anything for you." He gave me short but sweet kiss and left with Diego and the third Agent.

As I stood watching them walk out, Carmen came up and put her hand on my shoulder. She said, "I have a feeling that things are going to be OK." I looked at her and smiled. I knew there was a long road still ahead, I just wasn't sure if it would be smooth or bumpy.
