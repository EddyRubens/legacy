For years and years I have dreamt, like every boy I think, that one day I would step into the middle of an exciting adventure. And now it is, possibly, that far. What do I do? Do I leave Braintec, after Sonja’s warning, or do I stay and step into that probably dangerous adventure? I know I can stay calm and controlled under most circumstances, but who knows this module in my head will change that.

The second hypnosis session with Rosanne didn’t make me much wiser. This time I brought her into a deeper trance and with a detour, I made her a movie director making a documentary of her youth I’ve got the truth out of it. When I let her speak after the session the disbelief on her face was enormous. Was that all? It is amazing how deep a person can slip away in unaccepted belief. How a small detail can influence someone’s life. That naughty girl with the braids on the second row in class can follow some people for their whole life. Or the remarks of the teacher. Or like in Rosanne’s case, the lost of a silver spoon. Her Grandmother told about it all the time, how beautiful it was and when she was allowed to take it with her to school it must have felt out of her bag. Who knows who found it.....
