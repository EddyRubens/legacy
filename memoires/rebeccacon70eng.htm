A little over an hour had passed when I called the phone number again. I was eager to tell Agent Andrews all the information that Carmen had found out. (I have to be honest, the fact that the CIA couldn't get a simple phone to work had me a little on edge. These were the men I was putting my trust in, the men who were going to get us out of this huge mess and capture Dr. Tachikawa?) I pulled out my laptop and once again emailed the CIA address that I had used before. I was automatically connected with Agent Andrews. Since the phone was not working he must have been waiting online to speak with me.

Miss Reynolds,
Is there a place you know of where we can meet?  I can arrange one if you'd like. Would you feel comfortable letting me choose a location this time?
Agent D.A.

I responded back;
Agent Andrews, I would feel safe at any location you choose as long as Josh, Diego and Carmen are there as well. Please notify me as to where and when you would like to meet. Just to make things clear, Carmen and I will not enter any location until Josh and Diego are present. I am sorry if this may be any sort of an insult to you. We have been through too much and have a hard time trusting people. I hope you will understand.
Rebecca

Miss Reynolds,
Agent Jacobs and myself take no offense to your request. We are pleased to know you are taking all precautions you feel necessary. We have set up a meeting location. Mr. Conners (Josh) and Mr. Montero (Diego) will meet you and Miss Castillo (Carmen) in the front lobby. From there they will bring you to our suite, where we will discuss further plans. Even though Dr. Tachikawa is gone, the rules are the same around here. They usually don't like us to be out too late in the evenings. Would 6:30pm be okay with you?
Rebecca
