The return trip seems to be less tiring than the journey out. Maybe because I travel along with time or something like that. I’ve got a transfer in New York soon, so I’m not there yet. I’ve traveled the longest part already. Sleep is not giving me a hard time. Instead of the joy which kept me awake two weeks ago, I became tired to my satisfactory. I experienced many impressions in Holland. Many good things to be remembered but also something good to look forward to. Soon Kodjiro will pick me up from the airport! I can hardly wait until that moment and I feel nervous when I think of him. Again more tensions. I hope he likes the souvenir of Spottie. Shall we kiss again?

During this long flight I started a conversation with the man next to me. He has been in Germany for business and is heading home just like me. I told him about my promotional work for Braintec and attracted his attention. He wanted to know everything. I have never spoken to somebody who questioned me so persistently. It almost resembled a police investigation. However, I have answered his questions as friendly and patiently as possible. I have given him a brochure and a pen when we arrived in New York.
