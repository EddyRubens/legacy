It's horrible here, and what is worse, it is  not supposed to be.  Something went wrong in the surgery, I think, like, maybe they left a special canister or capsule of hallucinogenic medicine in my system?   I think that what is happening is that there is something left over in my system that was not supposed to be there, something that is penetrating my conscience and my consciousness, and making me vulnerable to things I should not see. 

The world should be a happy interface, where I am able to confront that which pleases me.  Others seem to be pleased at their lives.  I envy Rosanna, who is often wooed by the doctors, or by other subjects, because of her happy, yet pensively objective, subjectivity. 

Today I had a nice meal, one of the first meals I have been able to cook myself without becoming distracted by images and visions.  Maybe I am the healer here, the medicine man who can see into the beyond? I don't know, nor do I care right now.  I am able over the past eighteen hours to enjoy my tofu, mybeans, some brocolli and ketchup and h ot sauce.  I love to pour condiments on everything. 

When will I find out what has happened?  Will someone, a doctor, email me to suggest what is the discrepancy? I want a normal life without this post traumatic stress, this anxiety about my future.  The ironic thing is that this haunting, fragmented past is not mine.  Could this be the instigation of a belief in primogeniture or re-incarnation? 

One day i would like to go to India.  I was in love with a Sikh girl once, I remember her, Taschda.  She was unique in so many ways. 

I will finish my meal and then take a nap.
