I am Rosanne van Klaveren and I am a test subject for Braintec, an American technology company in Portland that does a research on the recall of memories. To that effect I have received a brain implant inside my left temporal lobe.
     
  With this implant I can relive the memories of other test subjects as if I had really experienced them. Great, isn't it? I am the first female subject with an implant that's also capable of uploading memories to a storage device outside the brain. This enables other test subjects to relive what I remembered. And I can for example re-experience my sixth birthday.

This is all so cool that I wish to recommend Braintec to everyone. You can apply here at my site to become a test subject, but you can also do it the official way at the Braintec company site. 

At this site you will find a collection of experiences and memories about my residency, but also about the stay of other test subjects at Braintec. This site will grow every week. If you want to, you can send me your e-mail address so I can let you know when there's something new to see.
