I just woke up when Kodjiro was sitting at the foot of my bed. That man really got up early! The clock just struck eight when he appeared in his costume. Still tired I rubbed my eyes and sat up straight in bed. “Time for an extraction,” Kodjiro said. I looked at him surprisingly. “I want to register your memory from yesterday spent with the seagulls. In exchange, you will get another upload this afternoon.”

I was awake in just a second. Of course I wanted to get such an upload! I prefer it here, now and fast, instead of waiting until this afternoon. Although I didn’t fancy an extraction at all. I just woke up! It felt as if I was in Portland again. Responsibilities are coming back in return.

I couldn’t think about it any longer. Kodjiro was ready with his equipment. Also the size was much smaller than the last time. Not as small as the portable upload-set, but it could easily fit into a briefcase. He used a small laptop to measure my brain-wavelength. I was permitted to lie down trying to relax. It wasn’t difficult to recall the splendid afternoon we shared yesterday. Kodjiro was satisfied. According to myself the extraction was completed successfully. He packed all equipment and told me he would pick me up around one o’clock this afternoon.

Until that time I stayed in bed. Slept for a long time, apparently I was still very tired. However, I also have been thinking about everything so far. Again a boat-trip. As far as I know, it is already the third registered boat-trip on M-Store. Adventures in a rubber boat on the Atlantic Ocean, Steward’s trip on the river with the pinup-girls which had to be destroyed, and now this one of myself. Obviously, I’m not the only one who loves to sail. On water you can relax. No restraints and you are what you really feel.

Suddenly I stood up. That bastard! That arrant Japanese didn’t choose an extraction of a boat-trip just like that! And the timing of the extraction, no doubt, didn’t appear out of the blue! Without making me suspicious, he had extracted my feelings for him! How weird. He must have noticed that something else was on my mind, apart from the pleasure of the moment itself. He could have asked for it, why not? I’m sure he thinks this was more exciting. And who is experiencing this memory of mine? Maybe my feelings for Kodjiro are sold for incredible high amounts, right at this moment!   

I felt being misused. Kodjiro had taken something from me which was too precious to share with somebody else. The jerk! But strangely enough it also felt pleasant. When the shot of adrenaline slowly worked out, I was curling around the pillow thinking of Kodjiro. That man can do anything he likes with me and funny enough, I even like the idea…
