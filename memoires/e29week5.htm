We took a taxi to the mentioned address. It was a temple. Rather pretty looking actually, very Japanese. But we haven’t come here acting like tourists. We were hiding behind a stand selling incense sticks and amulets with Japanese characters. From here we had a reasonable overview. What now? We hadn’t the slightest idea what was going on here. Maybe we were located on the wrong spot.

Then Adrian recognized them. He touched me and was pointing into the direction of two tourists walking out of the temple. “Hans and Heather,” he whispered. The two seemed to enjoy themselves. They were talking to each other. Then Hans nodded and they walked together with the boy. We had no idea what was going on, but we felt in our bones that Hans and Heather were in great danger. “We will follow them, alright?” Adrian asked and I nodded. It was exciting and also a bit scaring. There were many people near the temple and that’s why nobody had noticed us. Now we were walking though small alleys. We remained at a distance although we didn’t want to loose them out of sight. Then all of a sudden they entered a building.

It was a small eating-place, like Tokyo has thousands of them. So what should we do? Going inside. “If we don’t do anything maybe those people will get murdered,” I whispered. “We’ll have to!” Adrian nodded and after a meaningful look we stormed inside. The small restaurant was empty. “See that door over there!” Adrian pointed and we rushed to it. It all went so fast. We entered a small room where we saw, apart from Hans and Heather, about six or seven Japanese. I recognized Kodjiro. “Quick, you have to get out of here!” Adrian was screaming. “They are going to kill you!” Heather passed us running to the door, but Hans had no chance to escape. The Japanese boy held him very tight. Adrian jumped on top of them and pulled away the boy. In that way also Hans could ran away. Two men I recognized from the meeting at the blood-bank were running behind him. The other men were heading towards Adrian. They were shouting something in Japanese language. Three of them were holding him tight. Kodjiro came to me and grabbed my arm.

Adrian screamed. Suddenly I got furious. I seemed to grow, well, that’s how it felt. Never before I felt so strong. I jumped onto the fighting men and was biting and pulling any spot I could manage. I had become a wild animal! Blind anger was going on and on. Special effect for Adrian managed to escape. Then somebody grabbed my hair and pushed a needle into my neck. It was Kodjiro. He snarled something in Japanese and after that they were all chasing Adrian. Suddenly I got very tired and fell on the ground. Kodjiro pulled me on a chair and wiped off the clammy sweat from my forehead. “It won’t take long,” he said. “Within a few minutes you will be yourself again.” Indeed dizziness seemed to disappear.

“I want you to bite me,” Kodjiro said. I looked at him fully surprised. Was this a dream? Did he give me a poisonous injection and was I already on the other side? ”I want you to bite me here, here in my underarm.” Kodjiro was saying it clear. He had rolled up his shirt. I looked at him wondering. “Bite me as hard as you can, then I will let go. That’s all I can do for you.” He was sweet, I was sure of that. All my doubts disappeared. Kodjiro has always been kind to me but he’s in a wasp’s nest, just like me. “Just do it Rosanne, do it now for we have not much time left.” I had bitten him as hard as I could. “Harder, harder,” Kodjiro hissed and he pinched me in my side. That hurt a lot. Through that pain I could bite even harder. I tasted blood and withdrew. Kodjiro took me in his arms and kissed me passionately. “Next time I’ll probably have to kill you,” he said. “Goodbye dear Otemba, now you have to run!” For a second I looked at his bleeding arm and rushed outside. Although the street was empty I was running as fast as I could.
