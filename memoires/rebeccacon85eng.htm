The impossible didn't seem so far off now that I know Carmen is here. But, as I sit and type I wonder what is going on in the minds of Josh and Diego. My feelings of betrayal have subsided and now I am actually worried for Josh. I have known him for so many years and I know without a doubt, that he would never put me in harms way. "Josh,

Rebecca"
