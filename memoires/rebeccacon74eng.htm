When we reached the third level, the 'Excutive-Club Floor,' Carmen had to lean against the wall for a moment. She said she was feeling winded and a little lightheaded. Diego was very concerned and asked her to sit down on the leathery cushioned bench. She insisted that she was fine. "It's just because I'm tired and hungry." She said. As we walked down the hall, Diego never took his arms from Carmen's waist. It was obvious that he had so much love for her. I gave Josh's hand a little squeeze. He gripped my hand just a little bit tighter. I knew it was a small jester for him as a way to say 'I love you.'

We finally reached the room, Suite 103. It was very near the end of the hall. Josh pointed to the door at the end of the hall which read Suite 105. "That is the room Diego and I are in. And you see the door right there, (he pointed at Suite 101) that is the room where the agents sleep. They arranged to have this middle room as sort of a conference and group planning area." Josh said. Diego took out a card key and handed it to Josh. I think he didn't want to take his arms from Carmen. Josh opened the door and Diego and Carmen entered first. I stopped Josh before we entered and whispered to him, "Does Diego know about the baby?" He looked at me with raised eyebrows and said, "I swear Rebecca,... It wasn't me. It was Agent Andrews. He was talking about this surgeon who could remove these things in your heads. Then he said something about Carmen being a sensitive patient because of the baby and the circumstance that go with it."
"What did Diego say?" I asked. "He was in shock but he's not mad. He just wants it all to go back to normal, like me." Josh said. Then he nudged me so that I would enter the room.

Diego already had Carmen sitting down with a glass of ice water in her hands. He was reading her some of the food items from the guest store. I couldn't help but notice all the electronic equipment when I entered the room. I had no idea what the majority of it was used for. It was on all the tables with colorful wires connecting them together. There were maps and photos taped to the framed artworks hanging on the walls. It didn't even look like a hotel suite any longer. More like a mini headquarters of some kind. "Well, being that you ladies have arrived early, I think it would be wise for you to take advantage of this time and order some dinner. Then I will inform you of the arrangements we have made for the both of you." Agent Andrews said. Agent Jacobs was sitting in a chair with large earphones on his ears and some wires in his hands. He didn't say anything. He just fiddled with the wires he was holding.

I took a step forward and said to Agent Andrews, "I would love to get something to eat, but  we have some new and very serious information that I think you should know about." I explained about the email I received from Megan. How it mentioned Kaleb. I was so proud to tell the agents that Kaleb left on his own to help Megan. (More than they planned to do) I should have known that Agent Andrews would have something negative to say. "You received this information threw an email?" He said in a condescending tone. "How can you be certain that it was Megan who actually sent it to you? Right now all of this is just speculation until we have positive proof as to what your saying."

I was trying to be so strong and brave through all this but the remark he gave was like someone jamming a sword through my heart. I felt tears swell in my eyes and I had to leave the room. Josh followed me into the hall. I saw Carmen from the corner of my eye. She was trying to follow me out to the hall, as well. Diego was pleading for her to stay seated and rest. I turned to her and gave her a wink. I wanted her to know that I was fine. Besides, Josh was here and somehow his presence was more than enough to make the tears go away. I explained to Josh that Megan was a friend of mine. I told him that I realize that I haven't known her as long as Carmen, and for sure we were not as close. But, she still was my friend and it hurt that the agents seemed unwilling to help. "Why is it that every time I mention Megan, the agents seem so unresponsive?" I asked him. "Does it have something to do with who her father is?"
"I don't know Baby, but, we'll find a way to get her some help." Josh said as he put his arms around me. "Right now you are the only priority"
"They showed concern for Kaleb though..." I continued, Maybe because he is one of their own. You know, obligations to the CIA code of honor."

Josh smiled and gave me a short but sweet kiss. It was enough to throw off my train of thought. I stopped blabbering and agreed to get something to eat. Maybe some food will give me some well needed energy.
