Indeed many people of the press arrived. The local radio recorded some interviews and I saw at least six or seven people writing down in their note-books. Dr. Tachikawa gave a speech. First he explained thoroughly about the old devices and processes. Then he showed us the new M-Stores. From the outlook they all resembled the older ones. The new ones were decorated with a relief on the surface. Some stripes and dots. From the inner self only the new M-Stores are compatible with the new type server. I was flabbergasted when Dr. Tachikawa told me that the new server contains all necessary parts for an upload. I immediately understood the idea. No need to be dependant on the clumsy big devices! If these M-Stores with the new server are all you need for an upload, it means that you can recall memories at any desirable location and any suitable time! These devices easily fit into a cigar-box!

While I realized that Dr.Tachikawa had become successful with this development, I felt proud as a peacock. This man is capable in performing miracles! The press was at least half as impressed by his story as I was. Perhaps they also discovered the new possibilities. If this product will be for sale in the retail market, everybody who can afford it can experience anything he likes at home! Unless they have this implant of course, but nowadays it is just a matter of a poly-clinical surgery. This, undoubtedly, will cause an enormous revolution in the entertainment-branch-industry. “Professor Tachikawa!” a journalist caught his attention. “Did you try out this renewed technique already successfully?” Dr. Tachikawa winked at Adelle to come forward. “This woman here has experienced her first own memory according to a M-Store type B in the combined server this morning!”  Everybody started applauding spontaneously. “My experience was absolutely fantastic!” Adelle’s enthusiasm attracted others. Everybody seemed to be satisfied.         

Some technical problems and questions were posed, some photos were made and the devices were examined closely. In the meantime people were enjoying the buffet. Everybody was in a very good mood. Same with Dr. Tachikawa for he had reached his goal, the press for dealing with a good story and Adelle still fully enjoying her upload. The only person not looking so happy was Dr. Hamilton. He looked a bit worried. Maybe he disliked the feeling that this time he was not in the centre getting fully attention. Or perhaps something else was going on.
