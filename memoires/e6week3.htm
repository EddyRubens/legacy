Today’s re-experience exceeded all previous experiences. They already told me that from today we’d be going for the ‘real thing’, but I never thought that a re-experience could be so complex. It seemed like some sort of film, so lifelike that I wasn’t viewing it but actually went through it myself. I sat in the chair, and got the thingy’s taped to my head and neck again. The room was darkened and after the relaxation exercises the upload started. I went right in from the word go.

Warm air, not even the slightest wind. A dusty smell and a magnificent view over an environment which had to be to the Grand Canyon. I’ve never seen the Grand Canyon for real but I recognized it from documentaries and pictures. Besides, I could feel that that’s where it was. Splendid! I felt satisfied and had tired legs, as if I’d walked for miles. The atmosphere was reddish and the images forming before my eyes were sharp and clear. There were two other people of whom I knew that they had also been walking. I felt attracted to the woman and admired her. For the man I felt comradeship. A good friend. For just a moment the image of a car came up, I knew it belonged to the friend. I saw some more images of the beautiful environment, that dusty smell came up again and then it was all gone. The light came on immediately, and I wished I could have stayed in the dark a bit longer. Suddenly I knew the names of the two people: “Matthew and Nancy!” I said cheerfully. “I’ve been walking for several hours with them at the Grand Canyon National Park!”
