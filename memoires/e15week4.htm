My trip to Holland has been good for a reason. We discussed the registrations with Dr. Hamilton, Jessica and Sheila. A few of them were cancelled right away, especially the ones of that evening in the Now & Wow. So no William with his laughing gas. I will write those people a thank-you-letter for their interest very soon. Then there are at least twenty registrations in which we cannot decide yet. This afternoon Sheila will give them a call inviting them for an interview. Six people I agreed upon from the start due to my descriptions and also the others. It’s up to me to call them this afternoon to set a date. Dr. Hamilton was satisfied and made me compliments for the results I have made.

So far my service package concerning the registrations of new test subjects. The assistance of test subjects is obviously also part of my job. During my stay in Holland three new test subjects have been submitted. Not that many. Today we were quite busy in the left wing of the building. Four test subjects needed an implant and from five subjects the implant will be removed. All were MSI-subjects except one of them.

That lucky one is Adelle. She seemed to be very happy lying in her hospital bed when she told me that she would obtain a MESI. She really hoped so. “Soon you’ll be able to know how it is to be me!” she forecasted with a smiling face. “I cannot wait until then just like you!” I replied to her. And I really meant it. After my stay in Holland during two weeks my irritations for Adelle were gone. I like having her around me again. She is a very strange person but in a pleasant way. I just had to get used to the idea. I told her so and she started laughing. “Many people have the same problem.” she admitted. “But you know what I thought of you when I first met you?” Adelle made me curious. “I thought: what sort of smart mouse is walking around being such a misery. Must be somebody again trying to find somebody’s help in order to complete herself while she is already perfect.”  What did she mean by that? I did not get a chance to ask her for an explanation ‘coss she got a visitor. Her niece lives close to Seattle and came by with her young daughter. Adelle could communicate very well with that kid.
