A memory originally one of Rosanne’s, was the worst experience I ever had. I was totally not myself. That’s usually the case with an upload, but this time it was so much worse and in a scary way. My heart was pumping too fast and I believe I have never had so much adrenaline. I saw something, an ufo. Normally I would run away under those circumstances or maybe hide myself and carefully look. But now I ran towards it. I was called back by a weird punk rocker who was my boyfriend but I ran further. I had no control over my legs. They didn’t belong to me.

Fortunately it wasn’t a real ufo. It was made of plastic. But my heart was still pumping too fast and the atmosphere pushed hard on me. That was the most frightening of all. The clouds, the air, my breath, it all wanted to push me dead. That was so scary. And I had so much energy. My eyes felt stretched and my jaw, my tooth, they hurt. And I had a strange taste.

Fortunately such an upload takes only some seconds, because it was really frightening me. I wanted to forget this memory as soon as possible. But I can still taste it.

I used to watch horror movies with my brother. The ones I was yet too young for. Those movies were sometimes too scary but I continued watching. I used to stay afraid after those movies. That’s how it is right now. It is still scary and I still feel the threat. That you know something is going to be totally wrong but you can’t have any influence on it. I try to realize this is all not for real. Aren’t we supposed to experience only good things around here?
