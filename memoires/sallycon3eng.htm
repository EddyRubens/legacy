I have been home for two days because of Christmas. That’s the advantage of living close to braintec. My father picked me up with his car and Eric was waiting for me at home. Eric is my friend for three months already. My mother made turkey, traditional and very tasteful. It was nice that Eric was there as well. My brothers came as well but my sister didn’t. Last summer she moved to Florida and that’s too far to travel back and forth for only two days. I could see on my mummies face that she didn’t like that, that Lynn wasn’t among us.

Daddy was a bit worried and he wanted to know everything about braintec, how it works precisely, those re-memories. I found it difficult to explain and i think he still doesn’t understand. But i don’t understand either, technologically. But if it works, it works.

After Christmas dinner Eric had to go home because daddy doesn’t want him to stay overnight. But the next morning he came back for me. We have driven to Maine and took the water bus. That was very romantic. During the evening we had dinner again, but then at Eric’s home.

I am glad that i was home for a while and it was nice to see Eric.
