Those new M-Stores are terrific! They feel so much more authentic and the intensity is absolute. I had more the feeling of becoming someone else as ever before. This morning I became Lucas when he was approximately fourteen years old. I remember how it was to discover the secrets of life and being a stranger in your parent's house. It was nice. I think Lucas had a better adolescence because his father wasn't an alcoholic. Mine was. He started to drink as soon as he woke up and ruined the whole day for all of us. My sister ran away with a thirty year old boyfriend when she was still at high school. She couldn't take it any longer. I had to stay to help my mum.

Living through Lucas' experiences at that age, I felt released. My bad memories about my own youth seem to be vanished, washed away. It really gives me a clean feeling and I'm suddenly aware of the heaviness of my own memories I used to carry with me. I feel lighter, almost enlightened. Great!
