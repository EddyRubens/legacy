It would be entirely too easy to just assume that someone else's memories have been poured into my body, by virtue of some cosmogenically altered gene soup of a mixture.  That's not what science is all about.  Science is about selection, choices, making and isolating.  I'm pretty sure that what has happened is something more complicated.  It has caused in me a bellyache of a time, really.

I went for walk over the weekend, on Sunday.  It was the first brilliant day I had seen in this dark northern wasteland.  I walked past the rock garden, nobody there.  I walked through the stand of elms, barren without their leaves.  I took a leak against a dark rotting tree.  What type it was was of no concern to me.  Then I sat down and had an experience.

I am calling these moments "seizures of past lives" because I can only see them as that.  they are so unusual.  In real life I was sitting down on the cold ground, the winter light was radiatiing through the dead pussy willow stems, and some blackbirds were calling in the field behind me.  But in my head, as soon as I closed my eyes I was transported.   I was never in the field.  I was never given to believe that I had ever been in any field.

This whole time I have been brainwashed, it seems, and the cold of morning, and the walk in the field on Sunday was some kind of medicine induced hypnotism.  I feel like I"m breaking apart.  Someone is brainwashing me to keep me from realizing that where I really am is a Caribbean island, far away from the United States.
