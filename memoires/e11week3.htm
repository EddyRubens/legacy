Adelle has been irritating me enormously. Though I must confess that my first opinion of her was perhaps a bit too negative. Okay, Adelle is an arrogant bitch with a disgusting smell of perfume around her and I get gooseflesh every time I hear her talk. But she can’t help it that she has such an irritating voice. I assume she can’t get plastic surgery on that one. The things she says are less annoying than I expected. At one time we discussed the development of mankind. How we are able to have that in our own hands. The rebuilding of mankind. That is a subject we discuss quite often. But I have never heard someone talking about in such a funny and dry way. Unfortunately I can’t remember what she said. If good humor is a sign of intelligence, this lady is less stupid than I thought. She seems to adapt herself a bit. Today she was wearing jeans, the most common uniform on earth. Does she want to be one of us?

Even though Adelle isn’t such a disaster as I first thought, I prefer not to have her around me. I have to sneeze every time, because of her perfume. “Oh, dear. Are you still having that cold?” she asks every time. Dr. Hamilton doesn’t mind her odor. It’s almost as if they’re in love, the way I see them lately. At least they seem to have a lot to talk about. It would be very welcome, actually. That way Dr. Tachikawa and I would no longer have the full attention of the grapevine.
