Dr. Hamilton, Steward and Dr. Tachikawa were applauding enthusiastically. Big smiles from ear to ear. I was given a glass of water to drink because this recall had made me thirsty. I couldn’t wait to give an extensive account of my experience, which turned out to be Dave’s memory. The upload had taken no more than three minutes but it took me ten minutes to recount all that I had smelled, felt and seen. The next three quarters of an hour, at least, I shared everything I knew about Matthew and Nancy and how I related to these people. If Matthew wasn’t such a good friend I would certainly try to seduce her. Now it was just embraces and the odd wink. What I could tell about Matthew is that he is an estate agent and that we’ve been friends since he has sold me a house. The couple had brought me along for a long weekend and we had walked for quite a long time in the park. It was lovely, three friends enjoying the freedom of nature. I felt really happy and content.

Dr. Hamilton wanted to know more details, like the last names of the friends, the date of our outing and the color of Nancy’s eyes. There seemed to be no end to his questioning and I was amazed at how many questions I was able to answer. For once there was no standard list of questions. Answers I gave led to new questions. Everybody was cheerful and we were quite surprised to see it was already six in the evening. “We’ll continue tomorrow,” Dr. Hamilton suggested against all expectations and then he got up. Nobody probably would have minded if we had continued for another hour and Dr. Hamilton would usually want to go on himself. “I have a dinner appointment with Mrs. Duval,” he explained, sighing “and I need to get ready.” Another dinner with the lady from California! I wonder how far Dr. Hamilton is willing to go to get for some extra budget for his project!
