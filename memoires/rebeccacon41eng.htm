I don't know what Dr. Hamilton was thinking! I can't believe how far is he willing to let things go. Let me share what happened.

I went to his office as I was asked to do in the note. Nobody was there. There was however another note on the door instructing me to go to a part of the facility that I had never been to before. Test subjects were not allowed there without specific instruction. I went down the stairs at the end of the hall, which was in the right corridor of the Left Wing. At the Bottom of the stairwell was the very large security nurse which the test subject nicknamed 'Shrimp.' He asked to see my clearance. I only had the note Dr. Hamilton had left on his office door.

"Oh, so your Rebecca!" He said as he while chuckling. "Down the hall and make a left when you come to the green door. Then head on in!" His laugh made me nervous to begin with. I should have known to turn back, but that would have only created suspicion!

When I reached the green door I went in to find Dr. Hamilton, Sheila (who was wearing a specific diamond bracelet), and Dr. Tachikawa waiting for me. I could barley stand to even look at Dr. Tachikawa. Dr. Tachikawa said that he arranged an interesting experiment for me. He continued to say how excited he was about it and that I was an "enormous bonus" to him. I looked at Dr. Hamilton and Sheila. They both looked shocked at what Dr. Tachikawa had said.

Anyhow, Dr. Hamilton explained that Dr. Tachikawa was going to attach a special clip to my MESI implant. He said it was going to register happiness, joy, or any 'other' feeling I should have during the experiment. It all sounded simple, but I knew there would be more to it.

Then he walked me into a dark room. It was more of a chamber. The entire thing appeared to be made of metal and it had a locking door. As I walked in with Dr. Hamilton, all I could see was a chair. He told me to sit in the chair and relax. Dr. Tachikawa stuck his head in and said with a chuckle, "Now when the lights come on, don't be afraid to show your true emotions."

My stomach was turning upside down as Dr. Hamilton turned to walk out. He took one last glance at me before he closed and locked the door. When he did, he whispered the words "I'm Sorry!" Then the door was closed and I could hear it lock. I was so scared that I started to cry. Then I yelled for Dr. Hamilton to please let me out. I could hear Dr. Hamilton and Dr. Tachikawa yelling. It sounded like arguing. Then it happened. The lights came on.

The entire Chamber was packed with demon and monster looking marionettes. And there were scary wax statues of people. There was even some device which flung the small ones at me. Then the lights turned into strobe lights and I could only catch a half a second glimpse at them before the lights would flash again. I was screaming and crying for someone to let me out. I was even calling out Josh's name though I knew he would never hear me. The last thing I remember was falling to the ground in a state of panic. I couldn't move. This may sound funny to anyone who doesn't share the phobia that I have, but it was horrifying!
