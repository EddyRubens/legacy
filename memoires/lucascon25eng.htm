Early.

Still groggy I search for my glasses, feel them and then hear how they escape from my blind fingers and fall somewhere beneath the bed. The telephone keeps ringing and although I cannot see who’s calling I decide to answer it anyway. Sometimes my clients call me in the middle of the night. Demanding my attention or sometimes only my ears. I would like to ask them to watch the time, but you never know how Serious the problem is. So it has to be.

A strange voice, never heard it before, a slightly Asian accent. I cannot register the message. But it sounds urgent. If I have a passport? Of course I have. As a Belgium living in Ireland. But before I can confirm that, lightly irritated, he goes on. Research, quickly, plane. That are the three words I can remember. And something about a man from DHL. No I don’t work for DHL.

I break the connection and look uncertain at the clock, also being a thermograph. Is it 2 or 5 o’clock. What makes the different. I turn in again.

What was that nice dream about… oh yes, the classical warm day (what else can you dream about in this rainy Ireland) furthermore of the usual. Not that I try to analyse my dreams, but it is nice to follow it. And so I disappear again to somewhere in the ether…
