Contact

My curiosity wins it from the scepsis and I decide to send an email to Jessica Wright, which seems to be more logical than to send one to the director. I am sure he will consider it well. I receive a reply from Jesscia within thirty minutes, short and to-the-point including some very reprimand commends I can appreciate as a colleague. She also told me that they find my profile very interesting... Do I have to be proud or afraid. Her last note hit the mark, ‘See you soon’ 

Whatdoyoumean ????

A bigger problem is how to inform my girlfriend, who works as a nurse at the local hospital. First we have the categorical rejection, then the disbelief, and after that I get declared insane. It is only after long powwow that I can convince her that I will go, yes, but very objective and regarding it professionally before I let them put something inside my head. (and I’m not going to let them cut too much long strands of hair... )

My next email goes to Jessica again. Go on with it! I write pugnacious. Convince me and I’ll stand at your doorstep tomorrow morning. Let’s see how she responds to that.

When I put myself to sleep I wonder who is financing all this? Memories of old novels about espionage, megalomaniacal industrials play for ruler of the world. Images of Mel Gibson inside his barricaded apartment in Conspiracy theory lead me to Morpheus. But my sleep is deep and relaxes. And that is a good thing because day three is going to be a real roler-coaster… but that is for later.
