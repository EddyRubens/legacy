Kodjiro was not there! We had made an agreement that he would pick me up from the airport and I was anxious to meet him and could hardly wait to see him again. I had been waiting for him for at least more than twenty minutes at the arrival lounge and a few times I’d thought I saw him standing there. Whenever I walked over to him it seemed to be another Japanese or somebody with the same figure. I gave him another ten minutes. If he would not show up then I would leave for the Research Centre by taxi.

No need for that. A few minutes later McKenzie, (jack-of-all-trades), suddenly was standing in front of me. “Sorry I’m a bit late,” he apologized. “There was a traffic block”. Later in the car I asked him why Dr. Tachikawa did not collect me. “No time for that”, McKenzie explained. “His mind is set on a new type of M-Stores which he is developing now”. A new kind of M-Stores! The old types were perfect? Why he urgently had to develop a new type while he promised to pick me up from the airport? Of course such thoughts are not promising for the development of human kind, but I was very disappointed. Fortunately Kodjiro did not forget me at all. 'Coss he had asked McKenzie to pick me up. A new type of M-Stores is a competition I probably cannot cope with…..

Luckily the others had time for me. Adelle hugged me and for a moment I even appreciated her perfume. I was home again and Adelle was part of it all. Adrian too seemed to be pleased I had returned. He was anxious to know how my family was doing and he was glad I brought him Dutch shag. Immediately he prepared one. Also Jessica hugged me. You could see she was relieved when I told her that I hadn’t missed the uploads. The one and only person I missed most did not show up. It seemed not convenient asking anybody whether they knew where Kodjiro was. The fact that he couldn’t save time to pick me up from the airport I could still understand. But to come and pat me over my head does not last that long?
