Three o'clock came sooner than expected. I think it was because we were both so nervous. Together, Carmen and I headed towards the uploading rooms. On the way, we were confirming what we were going to say if they asked any questions as to why we wanted to be together. Carmen was planning on telling them that she had a horrible premonition last night. She was going to say that she had the feeling that something bad was going to happen. And, in her culture, (wink, wink) it was a 'sign' sent to her as a warning. She was going to finish by telling them she would just feel more comfortable if we could stay together. As we were walking down the hall, I whispered to her "I wonder which room they found Kasey in?" She whispered back that she didn't want to know. When we reached the uploading room, the usual people were there, Dr. Hamilton, Sheila, Stewart, and to my surprise, Dr. Tachikawa. I figured he had more important things to do than watch an upload. (Things like, torturing poor unknowing test subjects)

The first thing Dr. Hamilton said was, "I'm glad to see that you came." I wanted to say something rude back to him, but I just did a fake smile and answered, "You know I wouldn't miss the opportunity to get an upload!" "Good answer," Dr. Tachikawa blurted out. He had one of his evil smiles again. I started to feel sick to my stomach. I got a sudden feeling this was not going to be one of the exciting or calm uploads I had received before. I grabbed Carmen's hand and squeezed it. She gave my hand a reassuring squeeze before Steward told me to get into the chair.

"Your little friend can wait in the hall for her turn," Dr. Tachikawa said. I looked at Carmen. She cleared her throat then gave her 'It's a sign' story to them. Sheila looked at Dr. Hamilton and rolled her eyes. I thought it was rude of her to do that. She has been pretty cocky since Dr. Hamilton gave her the bracelet he originally gave me.  He shook his head in the 'Okay' motion and told Carmen to just stay quiet and out of the way. "Absolutely not!" Dr. Tachikawa yelled out. He pointed his finger towards the door and yelled at Carmen, "You go in the hall!"

I could tell Dr. Hamilton was upset with him. He took Dr. Tachikawa in the hall and closed the door. They were out there for about a minute or two then came back in. Dr. Hamilton's face looked as though he was mad, but he returned calmly to his seat. Dr. Tachikawa, on the other hand, came stomping in like a little kid who didn't get his way. He purposely bumped into Carmen on his way back over to his chair. Once Steward got everything in place, Carmen squeezed my hand one last time. She took a few steps back, then the lights went to dim.

Within a second, I was there. I was all alone in a dark room. I was little, about three or four. I could feel myself crying. There was also another little voice, in a room across the hall crying. My heart was aching because I knew the other person crying, was my brother.  Why were we apart? What did we do wrong? Mommy and Daddy never put us in different rooms, even when we were being punished. I was scared and confused. We were calling for each other, then for Mommy, and then Daddy. Then I remember a big voice. It told us to "Pipe down and go to sleep!" I grabbed my stuffed bear and squeezed him hard.  Why did we have to come here? I wanted to stay with Grandma and Grandpa! I was all alone. My brother, I want my brother! .....Tears...Sadness... That was it.

When the lights came on, I was crying. I looked at Dr. Tachikawa first. He was smiling of course. "Feeling sad and alone?" he said. "The world might turn on you. Get use to it now!" Carmen came over had put her arm around me. "You Okay?" she asked.  I shook my head 'Yes' while wiping away the tears. I had to explain what I saw and how I felt, as usual. Dr. Hamilton made a comment about how this was a different M-Store than what he had planned. I didn't have the energy to complain. We both knew who switched it.
