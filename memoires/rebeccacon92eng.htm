On the walk over to the 'Left Wing,' I was feeling so mad and betrayed by the 'Agents.' I started thinking about all the things they said and all the promises they made, and how they didn't follow through with one of them. They were obviously 'dirty' agents working with Dr. Tachikawa.

What is strange to me is the realization that they must have been working on getting rid of all evidence that could leak Dr. Tachikawa's business for quite some time now. I remember the man on my flight from Germany asking Rosanne all those questions, turned out to be Agent Andrews. Then he came to the facility searching for only three specific people. He must have known that Dr. Tachikawa had Rosanne in his grasps. He was in charge of getting rid of the other three who knew just a little too much! Then, poor Carmen gets dragged into the mix because of the mistake she made letting the doctors implants her baby with the minuscule MESI. I think what angers me the most was thinking how they completely tricked me when Carmen was losing the baby back at the Staybridge. They made me think she was being taken away in that helicopter. They're horrible! I can't believe I allowed myself to think they were actually here to help. How could I be so nieve! I should have known when I offered them information and they were not interested in hearing it, something was wrong.

When I reached the entrance to the "Left Wing,' the anger subsided and I felt a little nervous. I don't know what I would do if anything ever happened to Carmen. We were so close now, like sisters. I think the bond between us was tighter than the one Carrie and I formed. Maybe it is because Carmen and I have gone through so much together in such a short time. I pushed through the double doors and walked in. There were quite a few security nurses stationed in various places down the hall. Luckily, I didn't know any of them and they didn't know me. But I did notice how they all checked to see if I had a pass to be in that sector.

I walked down the hall a bit looking into the rooms as I passed them. I didn't see Carmen in any of them. When I reached the main counter I thought there would be a log identifying patients with room numbers, but I couldn't see one right off. Since the counter desk was empty, I walked around behind it and sat in the dark blue rolling chair. There were a few metal file folders lying on the desk. I lifted up the first one and read the name on it, 'Trent Weaver.' The next one I read said, 'Stella Kirkland.' I picked up one more and the name read, 'Max Montero.' None of them were Carmen.

I turned around to the computer which was on the back desk. I decided to type in Carmen's name and do a quick search in the medical program they had installed. (It was very similar to the one we used at the school when it was time to do grading.) When I typed in her name and pushed 'Search,' I had to wait for it to process. The computer was an older model and took longer for information to come through.

While the computer was searching, a Braintec neurosugeron assistant came to the front desk and asked for the files on 'Generation 3.' I looked down at the desk unsure what to hand him. "You must be one of the new people, right?" he said to me. "Well, new to this section." I said with a smile. I had to make him believe I was new and not snooping. I also didn't want him to notice to computer behind me doing a search. "Well, they must have given you some information on 'Generation 3,'...you know...the babies." He continued. "Oh yes, the babies!" I said. "I must have misunderstood you the first time. They never said anyone would be picking up those files today. They must still have them in the file vault."  "The vault?" he said. "I don't know of any file vault here." "Yes, there is one." I said, trying to sound like I knew what I was talking about. "Well, where else do you think they keep the covert and highly concealed cases?"

He stared at me for a minute. He was obviously in thought. "Well,..." He said. "I suppose you would know more about those things than I would. I didn't realize I needed to put in a request for the files. Look...I'm running late for a surgery, I'll have to come back and do this later." "OK," I said. "I'll see you later then." He walked quickly away, turned the corner and was gone. I had to take a deep sigh of relief. That was a close one.

Just then, the computer made a funny little sound and I turned around. On the screen it had Carmen's name and room number. I was so excited and wanted to hurry and go find her, until I noticed something strange typed under her name. It said: 'Generation 3'- Name: Max Montero- Status: infant #3, obtained. I knew I had heard that name before. It took me a moment, then I realized that Max Montero was one of the names in the metal file folders. When I finally put two and two together, I remembered Max was the name Carmen was telling me that she wanted to name her baby. And, Montero is Diego's last name. I swung the chair around to grab the metal file folder with Max's name in it. I took it out and read that he was born premature two nights ago, the night we were at the Staybridge Suites. It was also noted that an extraction was done on the baby and Carmen while she was giving birth. I grabbed the other two baby files and Max's, and made a quick photo copy of them. My heart was pounding in fear that I would be caught, but no one saw. I folded up the copies and put them in my bra. I didn't want to take any chances. I left from behind the counter and noticed it was 1:45pm. I had to hurry and find Carmen to see what was going on and then get back, unnoticed, for my upload.
