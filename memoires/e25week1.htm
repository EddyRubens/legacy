Japanese is really a very strange language. In this particular plane mostly Japanese passengers and I cannot understand one word. A pity, for after that last upload with Adrian I was hoping I would understand the Japanese language from now on. No way. Actually normal in one way. Memories are only registered in the short-term-memory and in that way you can never ever learn a foreign language.

The flight lasted not as long as I thought. Only 13 hours all together, changing flight in San Francisco. I thought Japan was situated on the other end of the world. Starting from Portland it is not that bad after all. I wanted to stay in San Francisco for a while, I almost did. Only forty minutes left between two flights and if I hadn’t found the right gate in time I would have booked a next flight at the end of the afternoon. That would have given me enough time to visit Jessica’s home. I wonder if she still lives there. Maybe she is at home right now! This idea came up too late otherwise I would have planned so. My rucksack with overnight-stuff would fly directly to Tokyo anyway. Those items wouldn’t guarantee a good-night sleep, but the idea of sleeping on the streets of San Francisco without my sleeping-bag was not my cup of tea. So I boarded the second plane and am listening to the loud conversations, not to be understood, of my neighbors. It amazes me that people can pronounce such amazing sounds.

Luxurious plane indeed. At the back of the seat in front of me there is a video-monitor. I am curious to know whether Japan is indeed the high-tech-country I’ve always been told. Everybody dealing with a mobile-phone contacting one another continuously. Must be expensive over there. For that matter I hope I can find Braintec’s office on the spot. I haven’t got more than a few dollars. Moreover I am anxious to know what I’m going through soon. At this moment I haven’t got the slightest idea what I’m intending to do when I arrive. Standing at Narita Airport, and then what? I might ask the man at the customer’s where I can find Dr. Tachikawa’s office? Wished I remembered the name of that company Kodjiro wanted to deal with. They should be able to help me there. The only guideline I have is Sonja. Although I don’t have a clue where she is now. Anyway I can email her.
