Braintec still has to find a solution for that stupid shaving. For a good upload it is important that the hair growth doesn’t interrupt the server. I’ve got a bold spot diagonal above my left ear and every day it has to be shaven to delete the new coming stubbles. That’s usually Jessica’s job. Until now she shaved me very carefully, but today she hit the scarves for at least three times. And that hurts, of course. I suspect her for doing that on purpose. The little wounds are now open again. And I was just so happy to be free of band-aid.  

I think I will adapt my haircut if I continue being a test subject. At this moment you can hardly see the bold spot because my long hair covers it. Actually I like it this way. But I find the shaving so annoying, especially when someone else has to do it for me. Some people might find it a pleasant thing, that fiddling about with your hair, but I really hate it. That’s why I never go to the hairdresser, yuck! If I stay here long enough and if the surgical wounds are completely healed, I might suggest shaving it myself. I might perhaps shave the whole left side of my head, for convenience. Like I used to do when I was an adolescent and tried to find a way to compare my inner feelings with my outside. A lovely feeling, I remember, when the wind blows over fresh shaven skin.
