The upload Kodjiro gave me this afternoon, took away all my doubts of morning concerning his integrity. I felt like a king during this upload. At least, a Very Important Person. I was taken care of in such a loving way that I started to see me that way. Only the best was good enough for me.

First of all, the surroundings. The room, interior and even the cutlery were all so sophisticated. Somebody was spending money like water. The food and the wine were absolutely delicious, cannot remember ever have eaten something before which satisfied my soul so deeply. People were laughing and drinking. I was with good company. There were Geisha’s entertaining me and some other men. The way they looked and behaved was so refined. The aim was perfection. Tonight was complete.

After the upload Kodjiro asked me what I had been eaten. “Nothing yet,” I answered honestly. By now, it was already afternoon and I just came out of bed. “So you’ve just finished a dinner costing 3.500.000 yen and then you argue that you haven’t had anything to eat!” I was flabbergasted. “Do you mean that’s what the party on that upload had cost?” Kodjiro looked at me while he was laughing. “For each person, yes.” Immediately I made a calculation. That must be $30.000!  “What you were having for dinner during that upload,” Kodjiro explained, “is ‘Wagyû’, a traditional menu served with beer-fed cows. Those cows get beer for ‘food’ and get a daily massage so that their flesh taste extraordinary. And that wine, one bottle of those is enough to nourish an African village for more than one year. Not even mentioning the price of those Geisha’s...”

“Ridiculous!” I shouted. How on earth people can waste money just like that. They should have given it to those people in Africa! “Indeed,” Kodjiro acknowledged. “And that’s why it’s a good thing people experience it through an upload now. Such an upload will only cost at least one out of thousand parts of the real experience and from the money not spent, we could save the world. Although I expect they’d rather spend it on something else.” I lost the fight. I liked the beef, absolutely, and I really had a good time. I wonder if my experience would have been different if I knew the price beforehand. I suppose so. Strange upload. Every upload is somewhat unusual, but this one is more difficult to get rid of. It got me to start thinking about the consumer, the final result. Shall the future market for M-Stores be the same as the group of swine enjoying the beef produced by beer fed cows or shall the baker next-door also be able to taste it?

My back was shivering when I remembered what Adrian wrote me the other day. Steward would make a living in the porn-industry. In what way that market would compromise Dr. Tachikawa’s customers? I stopped worrying because we decided to go for a bite. You cannot fill your stomach with virtual beer-cow.
