I read the letter Josh's Mom sent next. I was curious as to why she was writing me. I have known her for years and she was like a mother to me, but never in my wildest dreams would I imagine her writing me. Not unless something was gravely wrong.

"Hi Rebecca Honey,
You are probably wondering why I am writing to you. Well, a few weeks ago Josh came over for dinner and I asked him where you were. He said that you were in Oregon doing some research for something. He never really said what it was, but I suppose it's really none of my business. Anyhow, he spoke a lot about you and told me that you mailed him and told him that you loved him. Oh, I was so happy when he said that. You know, he has always had a big crush on you. I was so upset when he dated that Darcy girl. Now, you know I don't like to say bad things about people, but she is a no good tramp. Her mother must cry herself to sleep every night thinking about how her daughter turned out. That's why I am so glad he is with you now. Joshy told me that he was going to fly out to see you. That was about a week ago. I hope the reason he has not returned any of my messages is because he has asked you to be a permanent part of our family, and the two of you are in a loving bliss. I still remember when my Danny proposed to me. It was so romantic. Well, I suppose I have taken up enough of your time. Please have Joshy call me as soon as he gets a chance.

Love to both of you,
Mama Mary"

Well, I had to admit, it was a letter I completely didn't expect.  It made me smile, something I hadn't done in a long time. Mama Mary was a true reflection home and everything that went with it. Just her words made me miss everything I left to come here. My house, my job, my dog, my Josh...my life! I didn't want to respond because I didn't know what to say. 'Yes, Josh was here but, he's different now.' Or how about, 'No...no ring, just lies.' I couldn't do it to her.

I would read Josh's letter and try to make sense of what he's doing...of what he's thinking. My only fear is that he and Diego might still be influenced by those bad Agents. What then? I suppose I would be hearing the 'Tachikawa truth.' Yes, the truth according to Dr. Tachikawa.
