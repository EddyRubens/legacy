After half an hour I suddenly became very curious. Should there be animal memories on the other M-stores as well? I resembled the codes. That couldn't help me. The numbers and letters had no connections at all. I had two other M-stores with me and I decided to take another upload.

High in the tree, at a branch. If I looked around me I saw fences everywhere. I couldn't go far. Behind the fence I saw animals walking on two feet. Big and small ones. They looked a bit like me, but they had less hair on their bodies. But they where packed inside things, through which I couldn't see how they looked like. Suddenly a door opened and a big bowl with fruit was placed. I started to eat with my face away from the fence.                               

These weren't memories from test subjects but from laboratory animals. Of course, before they experimented on human beings they did some tests on animals. It was fun being a monkey for a while. That would say, that first time I was enjoying myself. During the second upload I felled trapped by that cage surrounding me.

When I stood up I suddenly felt very weird. My body wasn't in conjunction to what I experienced. I seemed to be much too tall, too straight and too high. I looked down to check the kind of body I was in. A human body, my body. I stretched a couple of times and did some exercises. Then the strange feeling disappeared. I was myself again.
