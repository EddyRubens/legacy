Earlier, while standing in the hall waiting for Rosanne to continue our tour, I noticed a pretty, young, Latin woman with long black hair heading towards the hall where my room is. She looked very tired. I wondered if that was Carmen. Then I started to wonder, if it was her, would she get the same urges I had and think to shuffle through my things. I couldn't help but laugh to myself.  

It turns out that indeed the woman I saw earlier was Carmen. She had arrived yesterday and was eager for a roommate to talk to. She is one year older than I am and we seem to have a lot in common. She has a man waiting for her at her home in New Jersey. His name is Diego. The only big difference between Carmen and I is, she and Diego are engaged. I only dreamed about being engaged to Josh. They made a pact that they could each do one crazy thing before they got married. Diego skydived. She said laughing that he though she was going to bungee jump. Boy was he wrong. I think Carmen and I will get along just fine. 

When I was brought back to my room, before meeting Carmen, Rosanne said that they would be by early to pick me up for testing tomorrow. Wow! That seems so soon. Since Carmen was gone for such a long time, I have a feeling I should go to bed early tonight. I will email Josh then go to bed.
