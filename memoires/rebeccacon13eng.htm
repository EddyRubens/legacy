Well, a day has passed since my last entry. I have done three days of various testing. All of it was very strenuous to the mind. Some made sense, but what is up with that room with the colors and shapes? I felt like I was in Kindergarten. The one I found most interesting was the test where I was blindfolded and had to guess what things were by using my other senses. It reminded me of one of those games you play at a slumber party. There were lots of other tests, but the one I liked least was the physical test, it was grueling! 

Sheila stopped by my room last night and said I could sleep in a bit if I wanted. Apparently Dr. Hamilton was going to review my tests and decide if I will work out. I sure would like to meet Dr. Tachikawa. I wonder why he is in Japan so much when the facility is here? I guess business is business no matter where you are! 

Carmen was accepted as a test subject yesterday. She is very excited and I am happy for her. She has the choice of getting either of the two implants. She has not decided which one she wants, but she had until tomorrow to make her choice. I am going to miss her when she is moved to the 'Left Wing.' She will go in two days. I am unsure how long she will be there. I guess however long it takes to recover. She told me that after she gets her implant she has a choice as to whether or not she wants her own room. She asked me if I am accepted as a test subject, would I be her roommate still? She said she doesn't really know anyone else to well, and would just feel more comfortable. I said yes, of course! 

Carmen and I took advantage of the time this morning by writing emails to our guys. We get along great. I suppose the fact that both of us leaving it all back home to try something exciting has left us feeling alone and in need of a good friend. Instantly we have become like two peas in a pod. She has started telling people we were twins separated at birth, even though we look nothing alike. She told me when this is all over, her and Diego were thinking about finding a house in a nice, quiet area. I told her there were a few houses in my area and I could have Josh get info on them. I was suprised when she said she was interested! I already lost my best friend Carrie to Germany, I don't think I could stand to lose another one to New Jersey. 

After our sending our emails and getting dolled up, we went into town. While we were walking around, this guy from Braintec, Davel was walking towards us. Carmen told me not to talk to him because he was strange. After he walked by us I asked how she knew him. She told me that he stopped her in the hall the other day and told her he was a pirate who just anchored his ship. He said he took seven prisoners because they were laughing at his wooden leg. He continued saying he was looking for a wench to feed his mind and other hungers. Carmen said she thought he was playing a joke on her at first, but then it was like he snapped out of it and in a completely normal voice he asked her what she wanted, like she had stopped him for something. I was laughing at the time when she told me but now I am worried that maybe they messed up on him.  

I hate to admit that I am having second thoughts now. I would like to tell Josh, but I know he would tell me to just come home. This is something I am going to have to really think about, especially since I find out tomorrow if they want me as a test subject.
