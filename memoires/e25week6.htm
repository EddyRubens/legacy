Kodjiro is a fantastic man. We drank tea in his apartment, a beautiful place in the middle of town. I can stay there as long as I want to. Tokyo seems much more friendly now. I just cannot imagine I would have regretted traveling all the way to this place.

Dr. Tachikawa was surprised I managed to find him. “You should have become a detective!” he smiled. Braintec has no official office yet here. I was dead lucky I ran into that doctor at the blood bank. “There are hardly any people in Tokyo who know what we’re really doing,” Kodjiro explained. “He might be one out of ten.”  It looks as if I will meet him more often.

When I had finished my tea, I stood up in order to look out of the window. The apartment was overlooking the town. Kodjiro was standing behind me. Softly he caressed my neck. “I’m glad you came,” he whispered. “Is there something you would like to have?” I turned around suddenly. “An upload!” I called out. I didn’t have an upload for days. After a day or two I only long for nothing else. Kodjiro started laughing. “You will get that for sure!” he said. “Walk with me…” I followed Kodjiro to his bedroom. He had this very low bed, just like a mattress on the floor. He made clear to me I should take place on the bed. He took some equipment out of his bag. A portable charger and a small box of M-Stores. These were decorated with funny pictures. So that’s the way Japanese like it. “An upload for Otemba,” he muttered and before I knew the memory was shooting through my head.

I was in a big carousel. Everything was moving and turning around me and still I didn’t feel nauseous. It was different from the feeling I felt in my stomach. I was in love! Oh yes, I felt so happy and my love was sitting next to me. So close to me, hidden as if she was scared of all turning movements. I felt so proud. This was my girlfriend! Yuki was her name, yes Yuki. I called out her name and she looked at me. How wonderful eyes! Her straight black hair fit in with her fragile face. Her lips opened halfway then followed by my mouth pressing hers. It reminded me of cherries and a pasture full of spring flowers. We kissed passionately. I was longing for more. It all went too fast. The carousel was turning into all directions and meanwhile my right hand did the same. I pressed the warm body close to mine. The carousel stopped slowly and the memory was over. I was laying spread out on my bed. I felt as if I had an orgasm. Heavenly!
