Hugo called me today. He was sweet and kind, and when I heard his voice I felt a lot of love for him, despite everything. I asked him where he was, and he said that he was in Paris for some conference, and that he had a fight with Sylvia. I felt hope instantly, that all could have a happy ending, and suggested that I would fly to Paris immediately. But he suddenly became cold and said that he didn't want me in his way right now because he needed some time to think and work things out for himself.

So I remained on my toes. I asked him, as if I was being spontaneous, where he got that Braintec leaflet. He said he couldn't remember, that he usually receives so many advertisement leaflets. I told him innocently how things were at Braintec, including some technical details, but he didn't seem to be interested. "And indeed", I added, "there is a very good doctor around here, maybe you know him, Tachikawa is his name." "Tachikawa? No, never heard of him," he answered. And there I had my evidence: Soesasji would never lie to me about this.

That night I dreamt about Hugo. We were boating, on some kind of cruise ship. It was a warm night, and everybody from Braintec was there. Suddenly strange music began to play, and Hugo took my hand, and together we started to float above the ship, and flew away from it, across the water. Suddenly it got cold, and the water turned black, with white crests,: a storm came over us. Hugo smiled and told me not to be afraid, but I tried to free myself from his grasp, which was getting stronger. Finally I broke free, and started to fall. Just before I was swallowed by the waves I woke up.
