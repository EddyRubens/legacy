After Sheila left, I decided to head over to the uploading room. I wasn't clear if Sheila's conference was replacing the upload or just delaying it.

When I got there, the door was closed. This was usually a sign that an upload or extraction was taking place. I waited for a few minutes outside the door. I was however, a bit suprised to see Adrian coming out of the room...alone. He looked at me then started walking quickly down the hall. He was holding his denim jacket closed as though he was hiding something under it. He was acting rather strange, especially since he didn't even say 'Hello' to me. This reminded me of how strangely he and Rosanne were acting the other night when I went to his room to try to find out where Carmen was. They were talking about Dr. Tachikawa before I entered the room. That is what made me stop. But, while I was waiting and listening in the doorway they were talking to people who were not in the room and calling out to Elisa (The woman who owned the bar in town) to serve them up another round. At the time, I figured they were just really drunk or maybe it was an inside joke, but now I wonder if they both believed they were actually at the bar!

I figured I would head to Dr. Hamilton's office to see if he was there. I wasn't sure if I was still scheduled for and upload. I didn't want to find Steward and ask him. I still think he is creepy and do my best to avoid him. And, I already had my fill of Sheila for the day.

When I arrived at Dr. Hamilton's office he was sitting at his desk with the door open. His reclining desk seat was leaned back a bit and he had his glasses off and was holding them in his left hand. His head was tilted back as he gently pinched pressure between his eyes with his right fingers. I could tell he was stressed. I hated to interrupt him but I needed to know what I was supposed to do. "Excuse me, Dr. Hamilton?" I said as I gently knocked on the open door. He quickly sat up and put on his glasses, answering, "Yes?..." Although he did not know who it was, he quickly erased the stress and worry from his face. He was, after all, the man in charge around here. He clearly was trying to make it seem as though things were still fine.

"Oh, Rebecca..." He said after he realized it was me. "I was dozing off there for a minute. I was working late last night and I think it's catching up to me."  I just smiled at him and said, "Don't worry about it. I just came here because I was unclear if I was still scheduled for that upload."
"You mean Sheila didn't come and speak with you?" He said in a suprised tone. "Oh she did." I said, "I was just wondering if that meant that the upload was canceled. Sheila never specified one way or the other."
"Oh..." He said. Yes, I canceled it. I hope that was OK. I just thought you might be a little upset with you friend gone and everything." I just shook my head and agreed with him. My stomach was boiling with anger. I really wanted to know where he learned to lie. He looked me straight in the eye without even the slightest flinch, while saying that. "I'm sure I'll manage." Was all I was able to get out.

Just as I was turning to leave, I changed my direction and went completely into Dr. Hamilton's office. I closed the door and sat down in one of the chairs he had in front of his desk. Now, I know it was wrong of me, but I was so angry from all the lies that everyone was telling me that I wanted to see someone else get a bit of the bad medicine. "Dr. Hamilton..." I started. "I want to be completely honest with you. (That was a lie) I really have made some good friends here and if that isn't reason enough to be here...well,... I don't  know what is." He looked at me sort of confused. He opened his mouth as though he was getting ready to say something, but I interrupted him. "Now, I consider you one of my friends Dr. Hamilton. You have always been kind to me and helped me out. So, I am going to help you out. Last night, I went to visit Adrian and Rosanne. They were acting really strange towards me so I decided to just leave. That was no big deal...but today, I found Adrian in one of the uploading rooms, alone. He obviously was hiding something under his jacket when he left. I feel really torn about going behind Adrian's back and telling you about all of this...But I think for his sake it's the right thing to do."

Dr. Hamilton was distinctly angry. His face turned a reddish color and his fists were clenched so tight that his knuckles were white. He whispered, "Damn her!" while shaking his head in disapproval. "Her?" I asked him. "Yes!...Her!...Rosanne!...She's been......" Then he stopped. He knew at that moment that he crossed the line and was about to divulge information that was not to be shared. "Thank you for bringing this to my attention." He said standing up and walking towards me. "I will have Steward check the room for any missing items. Your name won't be mentioned to anyone." He used the same technique I had with Sheila and opened the door proposing that it was time to leave. As I walked past him he said he would let me know in advance when the next upload would be, then he closed the door behind me. I glanced back and saw him pick up the phone. I wondered if he was calling Steward, telling him to go over to the uploading room to see if anything was missing.

As I headed back to my room, I felt bad for snitching on Adrian. But then again, it also felt good to have all eyes off me, if only for a short time.
