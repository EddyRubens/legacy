My recovery is progressing fast. The team is satisfied and so am I. I hate being so handicapped even if it is temporary. The more bored I become the more restless. So it’s just as well I am almost fully recovered. That annoying bandage can stay off now and besides my daily tests and exercises I’m free to do my own thing.

In the recreational room there’s a cupboard full of games and other stuff to help kill the time. I found some jig-saws there and for lack of people to play the other games with I have started on one of them. Never thought that laying a jig-saw could be so relaxing. As I am working on completing the picture my head is emptied and time flies. Doctor Hamilton encourages it because it’s supposed to help me recover. So I’m killing two birds with one stone. The first jig-saw however was way too complicated so after a few frustrated attempts I gave up. Now I’m doing the ones with fewer pieces. The pictures are lame, like Donald Duck on a bike, stuff like that. I look at the separate pieces and the colors and how they fit together more than the complete picture. When this one’s finished I will start on a 500 piece jig-saw with a picture of an old farmhouse. Maybe a bit complicated but if I raise the standard. It might speed my recovery.
