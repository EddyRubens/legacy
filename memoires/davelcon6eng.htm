I think that after my talk with Roseanne, i am less afraid of dealing with my "two minds" and eager to see a more communal reception in myself to the stories of others, even others I have never met. 

Which brings me to this memory retrieval, which seems to be the fullest one yet.   I had just listend to the last of the B side of a Velvet Underground tape, and I was shutting my eyes, when I began to remember that I was on a boat in Congo a couple of years ago. 

We were taking bend after bend in the river, at night.  The red eyes of crocodiles were blinking and shining in the dusky middle distance.  I was swinging a huge arc lantern through the mangrove roots, looking for the ripples around the backs of river dolphin.  Suddenly we spotted on, a pinkish pearline river dolphin, a female, hunting for prawns and yellow snapper in the estuary section of the river.  

A man with a gun, a tranquilizer dart fit into the barrel, took aim, and carefully, just as we got close enough to shoot it and far enough away that we didn't disturb it, he shot.  We watched her flash and flare in the shallows, and then I jumped into the black river.  My goggles didn't help, my bare chest icy cold after the retreat from the hot humid night.  I kicked blindly and headed in the direction I knew her to be in, trailing a rope and a holding net with me. 

I found her, just meagerly flapping, and then draped a cotton face veil over her eyes, wetted h er down, and slipped the net under her flippers, to drag her slowly and carefully back to the paddle boat. 

And then the night ended, and i was awake, staring out of my window at the gardener pruning the ash trees back.  Spring was coming in a couple of months, and he must have been preparing the old branches for the new buds.
