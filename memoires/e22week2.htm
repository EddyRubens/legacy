Dr. Tachikawa has left during the night. He took a few expensive things with him. Also his personal belongings are gone, even his laptop. It looks like he will not return anymore.

His sudden departure led to complete tumult. When Dr. Hamilton discovered that important equipment were gone, like an extraction-set, he called the police. Immediately after that Steward seriously tried talking to him. He warned about the power of the press and how this departure can end up in a huge scandal. The papers will over-exposure the whole rumor, resulting in negative consequences. Instability in a so-called controversial company would not do any good to the little confidence sponsors already have. Dr. Hamilton listened very carefully. He shook his head every now and then. I think he appreciated the intensity of Steward’s words.

A few minutes later police arrived. Dr. Hamilton apologized. An indoor miscommunication was caused by the disappearance of the equipment, he told us without moving a muscle. He reassured the policemen that everything’s under control again and he thanked them for their fast arrival. He walked with them to the police-car. I saw for myself that they did make notes of the whole affair.

Steward asked me to help him with making up a list of missing stuff. Together we walked through the whole building. Dr. Tachikawa didn’t take away a lot, but the equipment he took was very precious. Some M-Stores were missing too, among them the one with my trip with the dog-sledge. “What do you think Dr. Tachikawa is up to with all the equipment?” I asked Steward. “You think he will start his own business in Tokyo?” Steward looked at me in a penetrating look. “It is better you don’t know anything,” he answered. “For your own sake.”
