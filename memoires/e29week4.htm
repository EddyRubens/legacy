Kodjiro’s departure was very sudden. He had given us an upload of a Japanese girl celebrating her day of maturity. He received a message on his cell-phone and suddenly threw all equipment into his small suitcase. Then he was gone.

This time Adrian was much brighter than I was in no time. I still felt like a Japanese in a pleasant way while Adrian started Kodjiro’s desktop computer. This was the first time Dr. Tachikawa left us alone and he took advantage of that moment. In no time he had broken into the most important codes and knew how to enter the internal network. “Come and look here fast!” he called out excited. The whole secret information was rolling over the screen. Explanations about testing chimpanzees. We were focused on three webcams in which we clearly saw visions of large metal wired-cages in which monkeys were getting bored. I didn’t feel comfortable at all seeing so much suffering.

The network also gave us information about Hans and Heather. Something I think it had to do with following-schedules. We read about certain locations where they had been, mentioned with dates and periods. And a report. In that way we discovered that they wanted to avoid any risk concerning the two people on the run. It seemed to be a rather vague text but we could figure out the fact that they were planning to liquidate both of them. Them as well!

Nervously we were searching a bit further into the computer-files. I wondered what they had written about us. We didn’t find anything. I really don’t know whether that’s good or wrong. While we were searching, a small window opened. It was an urgent call stating time and place. “Urgent. Final call.” Is that why Kodjiro left in a hurry? “Come let’s go,” Adrian said and he shut down the computer.
