Last time it was peanuts. That’s why I didn’t feel ashamed to ask for another upload from Adrian. “You are lucky,” he laughed, “Some new memories from Japan just arrived.” I stood there staring with my mouth open. Did he make a joke? “You shouldn’t make a fuss about it,” he whispered within a second. He really meant what he was saying. “If you return within one hour, everybody is fast asleep. Then you get your upload and I’ll try to register it on film.”

I couldn’t wish for any better deal. A memory from Japan! I went for a walk round the research centre and made sure I was back in time. Adrian was hesitating to start with the upload right away. “Let’s wait for a moment,” he said. “I think I just heard somebody walking through the corridor.” We both drank a large cup of tea and I told him what I heard from Adelle. Adelle is an adorable person, we decided, but she is too much devoted to Frank and Dr. Hamilton.

We finished tea and then it was time for the upload. “What’s written on it?” I asked. “Have no idea,” Adrian said. “There is no description enclosed. But if I’m not mistaken, we will find out soon!” In no time we had prepared everything. This memory seemed to enter much faster than usual. Everything was flashing. I was located on a huge crossing, probably in Tokyo, and was fascinated by all sorts of impressions. Behind, in front and beside of me, Japanese were busy talking to each other. Neon-adds were flickering on buildings surrounding the crossing. Happy sounds, beeps and the sound of running engines of cars enforced the chaos. While everything appeared very structured and according to an arranged plan. Then it was done! A deep sigh and later I discovered the impossible. I did understand the Japanese conversation of the people in the street! How come? I heard parts of conversations about work, about a new computer-game and about the exchange market.

Happily I walked through the studio and talked about my fantastic experience. I was able to understand Japanese! Adrian pushed me back into my chair. “Calm down a bit, will you,” he hissed. “Careful they can hear you!” Of course he was right although this upload gave me so much energy I could hardly sit still. I tried my very best to stay calm. I noticed Adrian was irritated. He stared at the small monitor producing calculations and fully neglected me. Couldn’t wait any longer. I was just intending to leave, ‘coss Adrian was obviously a bit angry at me, when the film was ready. Visions were much clearer than before and exactly registered what I had seen. “Yes!” Adrian shouted and he immediately got frightened of his own voice . We looked at each other smiling. You simply cannot stop so much joy.
