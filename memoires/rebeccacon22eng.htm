It seems not a day goes by without something interesting happening. This morning, Carmen had an appointment to get her first upload. I was walking with her because she wanted to talk to Dr. Hamilton about me being in the room with her. She seemed so nervous and excited at the same time. In fact we ended up arriving at the room a half hour early! I offered to go get some mini muffins from the vending machine but she insisted she was to excited to eat. So we decided to just stand out in the hall and talk until it was time to go inside.  

After being there for not even two minutes, we heard a scream from down the hall. It wasn't a 'pain' sort of scream it was more of a 'fear' scream. We ran down the hall to see what was going on. A few minutes passed and that's when we saw Sheila walk out of a room with an odd look on her face. Carmen asked, "Is everything OK?" Sheila said is a very mellow tone, "Everything is under control. Nothing to worry about, just......a misunderstanding." She gave me a look like she wanted to say something to me about the letter I wrote her, but I think Carmen's presence restrained her from continuing. (Thank God!)  

She asked us to go back down the hall. She said she would be there soon to get things started for Carmen. "Is Dr. Hamilton going to come out here soon?" Carmen asked. "I have something I'd like to ask him," she continued. "If you have any questions you can ask me." Sheila said. Carmen looked at me with a look I understood. It was the look verifying that she had one nerve left, and Sheila was bouncing on it!
"Sheila," I said, "if it's all the same to you, I think it might be something personal and......I wanted to talk to you anyway." By the look on Carmen's face I could see she was happy that I jumped in and said something. However, I was mad at myself for saying I wanted to talk to her! That was the last thing I wanted to do!

"Very well," she said "Let me just get Carmen positioned comfortably in the chair, run through the procedures with her and then we'll go to my office. You do understand we are going to have to make it short. I have to be back her for Carmen's upload." "Oh... I understand!" I said smiling. The shorter the better is what I was really thinking.
