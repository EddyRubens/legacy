I’ve been reading Sonja’s mail all over, three times. What was going on with her? She describes me as being her lifeline. Her connection, like a navel string. Maybe I ought to help her. I could take Kodjiro’s plane ticket to visit her in Tokyo. But, for God’s sake, how am I going to find her?

Such kidnapping definitely impressed her in a terrible way. But still I cannot believe they are going to kill her. A while ago Hugo was still so much worried about her. He wouldn’t like the idea that they murder his own wife, does he? No, I don’t believe in that. Maybe her paranoia behavior exists out of her former memories. Sometimes I also experience bad moods through it. Disgusting, what she’s going through. I wished I knew how I could help her.

Adelle doesn’t believe it, no way. She does worry about Sonja but she argues that she has not been kidnapped. Although she underlines the fact that Dr. Tachikawa is capable in doing weird things. She also worries about Sonja because she still has an implant in her head. One day or another they have to take it out. And she’s got this unpleasant feeling that she has to make up her mind. I asked her about her choice she desperately had made. “Sonja kindly requested in her email not to talk about it with the group. Don’t you think it is for her sake that I still discuss it? At least she has gotten a huge psychosis and can be of danger to herself." I hope I was able to hush Adelle. I came to a point where I was worried myself, now I trivialized the whole matter. “I think, everything is going smooth, Adelle,” I tried. “Don’t talk about it with anybody. Just wait, at least, another couple of days.”

Although I can get along with Adelle very well, I shouldn’t trust her too much for that matter. Fortunately I didn’t tell her about the plane ticket. By changing subject as soon as possible, I therefore asked her about the latest developments with Frank. That hit a good score. Out of the abundance of the heart the mouth spoke. She explained about the discussions between Braintec and the army. About Dr. Hamilton getting along very well with the officers. At the beginning of this week they showed up with a cash advance and later this week the total budget will be known. It made my flesh creep while Adelle was talking gladly about the recent developments. Didn’t Sonja write in her email about the Ministry of Defense who would support Braintec as an important investor? How well the inventor’s intentions of gunpowder were, the results were a catastrophe!
