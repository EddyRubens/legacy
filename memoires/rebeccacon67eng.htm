While Carmen and I waited for a response, she put a French braid in my hair. We talked about where Dr. Tachikawa could have went. About half an hour passed when I decided to check my emails. There was an answer from both Agent Andrews and Megan's family. I read the letter from Agent Andrews first. It said;

Miss Reynolds,
First, I would like to apologize for our phone not working. The existing problems are being looked into as I write this letter. I would rather not discuss our business via Internet, so if you would, give us an hour and try the phone number again. If it does not work, we will have to schedule another place as to where we can meet. I hope to speak to you within the hour.
Agent Andrews

I told Carmen what he said in the email. She decided that she would go talk to Jerry while we were waiting for the hour to pass. "Any extra information couldn't hurt!" She said. I waited until she left, then I read the email from Megan's family. It read as follows;

To Rebecca Reynolds
Thank you for writing to my family. We have not heard from Megan for many weeks. We have been very worried. You mentioned trouble for Megan. That she is missing. Thank you for trying to help. Arrangements have been made to find her location. Please keep in contact. Any information will help.
Mr. Nikolai Isakova

I responded back;
To Mr. Isakova,
I am writing to let you know that the terrible doctor I mentioned in my first letter, has left the company. There is talk that he could have gone to Japan. The name he was using in this facility was Dr. Kodjiro Tachikawa. Apparently it was a false name. His real name is Shinicho Matsushita. This is private information the CIA revealed to me. I don't know if this is of any help to you, but I am willing to let you know everything I know, if it will find Megan. I will write again when I know more.
Rebecca

After I finished the letter I decided to go find Carmen. At the rate people have been disappearing around here, I was worried I might lose her as well.
