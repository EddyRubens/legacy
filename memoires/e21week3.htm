Frank, Adelle’s ex, came by visiting us today. He was accompanied by a dog. A little grey monster wagging his tail and barking out loud, jumping into her arms. Adelle told me later that she thought she owned this animal being hers. But her ex possessed this dog already before they fell in love. The dog is called Dinky because her ex is a collector of Dinky Toys. What a nuisance, I thought for a moment, but I remembered the toddler-contest and immediately forgot this whole idea. When Frank and Adelle got divorced, he took the dog with him and she could stay in the house. 

How happy she was to see Dinky again.  “Dinky, oh Dinky, Dinky,” Adelle said hugging the animal, that managed licking her head all over. She was sorry Frank could only stay for just a moment. He was in the neighborhood visiting some relatives and couldn’t stay any longer. He promised to come again very soon. He would take her out for dinner then. He hardly left ten minutes ago when there was this need for chocolate again. I wonder if Adelle can compensate all difficult moments by eating chocolate.
