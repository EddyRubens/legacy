About one-and-a-half hour later it all became clear to me. I met Kodjiro in the corridor. This time he had an eye on me. I could easily see his joy from a distance. He was in good shape just like Adelle was. As he reached me, he suddenly grabbed both my upper arms. “I made it, Rosanne!” his eyes were glittering because of all this. “My new M-Store is a great success!” Kodjiro pinched me just a little too hard. I think it’s great he feels happy, but no need that I should suffer through him? I tried to avoid attention on my painful upper arms and concentrated on his shining eyes. “Good for you! You have worked hard on it!” Maybe I reminded him that still a lot of work had to be done, for suddenly he let go off me. The magic seemed to be interrupted. The only thing I could do was rubbing my upper arms. Luckily, they were still there. “This afternoon some people of the press will arrive. I would like you to take care of something to eat for them. Could you take care of snacks and drinks together with Adelle? Of course, at the expenses of Braintec.”  I think I was looking in such a way that I agreed for he thanked me and left right away. Incredible how much energy that man was surrounded by! I had to shake off his enthusiasm to make myself conscious of what happened. Obviously I had agreed on taking care of a buffet. Where can I find it all? For how many people?
