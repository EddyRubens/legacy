Adrian reached me a glass of water and I tried to stabilize my mind. “Can I use your phone?” I asked after some silent minutes. Adrian gave me his portable phone. I picked the ticket to Tokyo out of my pocket. One reason or another it felt good to carry it along with me. I called the reservation number and managed to get a flight at four o’clock in the morning. Perfect. I was relieved taking a breath. I can keep my implant and in the long run I would have gone to Tokyo anyway. For Sonja, for Kodjiro or simply for the fact I still had this ticket.

Adrian insisted in bringing me to the airport. That’s very kind. The only thing I had with me was my rucksack, some clothes, my laptop, small camera and some rubbish.
