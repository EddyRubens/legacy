When I left my room, I decided to head to the Communion-Room, since that is where a lot of the test subject hang out when they are not scheduled for an upload. When I walked in, I saw a face that was strangely familiar but hard to place.

I went in and sat on one of the chairs across the way, but still facing her. I picked up a three month old issue of 'People Magazine' and flipped through it. I was paying more attention to her and the slightly older gentleman she was sitting with that the magazine I was holding. Luckily, they seemed too involved with each other to notice my stares. It wasn't until Hans said her name that I realized who she was. (Hans was the name she called him while showcasing a nonstop smile and twirling her platinum blonde hair in her fingers.) "You know Heather..." He said. "I am so glad we met."

Heather! Oh my God! She was the lady at the front desk of The Staybridge Suites.  Was she undercover with Dr. Tachikawa's crew? Was she working with Agent Andrews and Agent Jacobs? What was she doing here, and what was she doing with this poor older fool who obviously was falling for her? I decided to leave the Communion-Room before she noticed me. My mind was like a box of puzzle pieces. I had so much to figure out.
