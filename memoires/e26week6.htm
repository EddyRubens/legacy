I was at the country side. Somewhere in Japan. Behind the house there were fields of rice. The sun was shining brightly. I almost had to close my eyes entirely in order to see something. I have lived here all my life. I knew the hills in the distance like the back of my hand. Years ago my father had planted those willow-trees beside the house. I was staring into the distance. There, in the shadow of the hill, there was a path. A man was walking across that path. He was approaching me slowly. I loved that man, with all my heart. I desperately hoped he would love me too, but at the same time I knew it was impossible. He was busy doing other things. For months he had been gone and left me in pain. The pain had gone, now that he was approaching me. He came closer and closer. I was getting excited. It was him, I was sure, I could figure from the way he was walking. When he finally had come closer, I could see for myself. It was Kodjiro and he was laughing at me!

Jessica and Kodjiro were not there when I recovered from the upload. I was confused. I couldn’t manage sorting out my emotions. I had no idea how I felt or what I should think. It made me go crazy. Out of frustration I tore my blouse into pieces with my teeth.
