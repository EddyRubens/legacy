I just got back from receiving my first upload. Dr. Hamilton, Sheila, and Mark Steward were all there. Sheila stood over by the door most of the time. I felt sort of bad going off the way I did. I think I will write her an apology letter tonight and stick it under her office door. That way I can explain myself without having to interpret how I feel about it! (Rolling my eyes!) 

Dr. Hamilton explained that in the beginning, they use to start the test subjects out with an image, such as a glass, a vase, or a flower. Now that they have done lots of tests, they know these images worked fine, but were rather dull. He asked if I would like to start with an image or a memory upload. I of course, choose the upload. 

I was told to lay back in the chair and relax my mind. The lights were dimmed and I closed my eyes and waited. I felt the M-Store server being put in place and then heard a small click.Moments after that, I suddenly appeared on the side of an airplane runway. I remember feeling my heart pounding in my chest. It was the feeling of excitement! I was watching these huge jets fly across the sky. They came so low to the ground that I felt like I could grab the wing of one of them and fly along with it! "Here it comes again!" I thought excitedly. The ground started to rumble beneath my feet, then an extremely loud noise and finally a huge gust of wind and then the jet was gone. After it flew way up into the air, my eyes lost sight of it. No matter, I like when it zooms by where I am standing. I will wait for it again!Then it was over.
