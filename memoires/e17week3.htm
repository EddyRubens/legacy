Maybe I am too much stressed indeed. Nevertheless it had been a quiet day, an extraction and so on, but before I fell asleep last night I experienced something very strange. I heard voices. Not just voices like hearing them when you find out something or passing some words through your head. Those were strange voices and they seemed to come from deep down inside. If I was to point at a spot, it would be right at the top of the back of my neck. The voices were not talking loud. It rather seemed like buzzing. I could not hear what they were talking about but they were talking clearly about me. They were audience. I got very much afraid. What kind of voices were they? They weren’t mine and I could not stop them or influence them.

Fortunately they disappeared after a while. Also my fear became less. Unfortunately my nerves remained, that’s why I could not sleep. Was I going mad? If the doctors knew this, they would get out my MESI the same day. They took away Steward’s MESI as well. Despite of his request they refused to put in a new one. I would never ever like to miss any uploads. Hopefully these voices will not spoil the party. I decided not to talk about this with somebody. That thought made me feel lonely. Why I always had to decide by myself?

Restless I turned over into circles in my bed. This should not last any longer. It was almost four o’clock already and I still had not closed my eyes. Alarm was set on half past seven. That still resulted in only another three and a half hours of sleep during that night. And I had to make use of it. I did not manage to become calmer. That’s why I took two pills Sheila had given me. It helped. I felt asleep into a deep dreamless one.
