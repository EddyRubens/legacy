It is fabulous getting along with Dr. Tachikawa. He is so sweet and very kind. I’m afraid that I’m totally in love again. I shouldn’t imagine things of course. He isn’t the kind of man to commit to someone. But that doesn’t matter. I fully enjoy the times with him. What else can I wish for?

We made a boat-trip together. Sailing across the ocean. It was so romantic! There were many seagulls joining the small boat. Kodjiro had bought a special cookie for gulls and together we tried to feed the birds from our hands. We offered the food stretching our arms. Some naughty seagulls managed to snatch away the food. It was super. The whole afternoon we spent together and I can tell for sure that I didn’t long for an upload. Not just for one second. I just didn’t think about it. This event was even better. This was real. And besides, an upload would never give the same expectations. Apart from relishing the boat-trip afterwards, I can easily speculate for hours about any possible consequences of this trip today. The way Kodjiro laughed at me. He definitely enjoyed himself a lot. Could he possibly be in love with me?

After the boat-trip we had dinner together. Going out for dinner in Japan is very common, sometimes even three times a day. But this time was special to me. I think Kodjiro knows exactly what I like best, for he had ordered a few delicious dishes. The eggplant stuffed with miso was absolutely the best. After dinner we went to some kind of bar. If I had not followed Kodjiro, I could have never found the place. The small street didn’t give the impression that some kind of bar was situated in here and from the outside it looked like any of the neighboring houses. The only difference was that more shoes and slippers were stored in front of the sliding entrance.

Kodjiro ordered some Tempura and a few cocktails. He ordered three at the same time, only for me! I think he wanted me to get drunk! He drank sake himself. I like sake prepared into food, but I don’t like it straight. He also knew. The cocktails tasted just like lemonade and I finished them in no time. Still there wasn’t less alcohol in it.
