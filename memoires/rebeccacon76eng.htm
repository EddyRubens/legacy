I pulled from Josh's arms and ran as fast as I could down the hall. I watched Diego run back into the room. My heart suddenly ached, my stomach churned and my legs felt like rubber as I ran into the room. Diego was on his knees beside the bed crying and saying, "No...No...No!" over and over. Carmen was lying in the bed, shaking, shivering, and crying. There was blood all over. I climbed on the bed and propped her up in my arms. I tried to stay calm. "Carm, what's happening?" She looked at me and whispered, "My baby...don't let anything happen to my baby." 

It seemed like an eternity until the Agents came. They ran in the room behind Josh.  Agent Andrews pulled out some device resembling a cross between a walkie-talkie and a cell phone. "We need emergency medical assistance immediately!" he yelled into it. I stroked Carmen's hair out of her face and told her everything was going to be okay. Her eyes were glassy and she was short breathed as she tried to speak. "What was I thinking Becka? What did I do? This is my baby...my baby." She cried. "We're going to make it all okay." I said to her. "The Agents are getting you the best help."

About 5 to 10 minutes passed as I sat with Carmen in my arms, when finally medical help arrived. Josh took Diego into the other room and the Agents made numerous calls to people 'confirming arrangements.' I wasn't sure what exactly was happening since I was with Carmen. Once Carmen was placed on the gurney, the emergency medic asked who was going to accompany them in the helicopter taking Carmen to the hospital. I jumped up and started to say that I would, but Agent Andrews interrupted me. He said that Diego and Agent Jacobs would be going. I told Carmen that everything was going to be okay and then they pushed her out of the room. Diego and Agent Jacobs followed.

The room was nervously quiet. Josh came back into the room and put his arms around me.  A few moments passed and I could hear the helicopter flying off. I was so upset about Carmen I wasn't sure what to think. All I knew was I was extremely angry with Agent Andrews for not letting me go.
