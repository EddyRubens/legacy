There has been an extended review of my activities at Braintec. The team seemed to be very satisfied. They appreciated my engagement with the company very much and they want to keep me here as long as possible. I am too valuable as the third human and the first woman with a MESI-implant, to be sent home after the usual sessions. Therefore they offered me full employment. Besides being a regular test subject I will also become an assistant. The recruitment and guidance of new test subjects will be some of my new tasks. Explaining the procedures, answering questions and being a buddy for the newcomers. That sounds like fun. I have taken this opportunity with both hands.

Starting next week new test subjects will arrive regularly. To get a reliable report, it is very important that a lot of people recall the same memories. Every week approximately five new test subjects will arrive, they will stay in the Research Center for one month unless they’re not suitable for the program. To make room for twenty till thirty test subjects, they’ll have to share rooms. These new test subjects shall have less freedom than I had. Dr. Hamilton is afraid that he cannot overview their do’s and don’t when they are with so many. Therefore these new test subjects won’t be allowed to leave the Center without guidance. There is been made a tidying schedule to get the maximum out of the test subjects. Almost every day is fully booked. These new test subjects will have conspicuously less time off. I am glad that my MESI-implant ordeals me a special treatment. I can keep my room for myself and I can schedule my extractions and uploads in consultation. 

I am curious what kind of people I am going to guide next Monday. Will they have nothing better to do than becoming a test subject, just like me some weeks ago? Are they also thrilled to experience something extraordinary while earning a nice amount of money in the meantime?
