If I want to, I could skip everything between my fingers. No responsibilities anymore, no connection with anything happening around me. Surely the world will go on without me. Hardly anybody will notice it when I’m not there anymore. Only thing I have to do is to let loose. I’m absolutely not suicidal, getting rid of this all has nothing to do with dying. If dying is the issue here, then I’m already in paradise for quite a while.

I don’t wanna die. I simply don’t want this life. It’s too much for me. Deciding on all those choices, time and again. I am dealing with it all by myself. I want to prove that I can manage to be somebody. I want to be loved and I want to love somebody myself too. It takes too much energy and time for me. I cannot control it anymore. Enough is enough. I could lay down a bit, right here now, and relax, and let it slip away. Try and keep all worries, all tension. Won’t be needed anymore. I give up. Let the good times roll. I don’t care who I am or what I am. I have been so uncertain lately that I hardly recognize myself anymore. One moment I am a bad witch, the other a femme fatale, telling lies obsessively and the next time I’m a scared girl needing love so desperately. They take over from me. Together they choose the easiest way out. Choose the line of least resistance.

Then those voices reappear. Whenever I loose control of the grip, I immediately hear the buzzing. I’m not so much afraid anymore than before. Let them do the talking. Now and again I can hear bits and pieces of conversations. There is somebody who has wrong intentions. A troublemaker. He tells me not to accept anything anymore and that I have been mislead more than enough. I do am afraid of him. The others are not so much present. Except for that particular one, one left-over who misses a lot of attentions. Thinks herself very pitiful. 

If ever I decide to get rid of everything, they will take over. Sometimes they force upon me, a single one or many of them at the same time. The more often they reach their goal, the lesser it matters to me. I don’t have to live anymore, let they do it for me. For the time being I am their manager. I can control them very well and sometimes I do recall on them. Maybe it is caused by tensions. Being in stress already too long. Maybe it is caused through the uploads. Hopefully they will disappear just as calm as they had came into existence.
