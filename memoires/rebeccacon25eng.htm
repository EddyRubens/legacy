When I arrived at Dr. Hamilton's office he was eating inside. I apologized for interrupting and started to walk out. He stopped me and asked me to please come in. His deli sandwich smelled wonderful! I didn't realized that I hadn't eaten until I smelled the freshly cut turkey slices on his sandwich. I sat down opposite from him and did my best not to stare at his lunch. He pulled out another sandwich wrapped in white paper from a bag on his desk. He placed it in front of me with a bag of Ruffles and a Diet Coke.  

He said, "I hope you like turkey!"  I smiled and proceeded to unwrap the sandwich. I was exactly how I would have ordered it, Swiss cheese, no mustard, no onions, add some avocado! It was fabulous! I asked him how he knew my favorite deli sandwich. He just smiled and said, "Remember Honey, I specialize in working with the brain! I know more about you than you probably do!" 

He apologized for starting to eat without me. He said he was starving. I said that was fine, I wasn't aware it was a lunch meeting. He just smiled and said, "Meeting?" I told him that there was a note on the door of my room that said he wanted to talk to me about something. "Oh, right! right!" He said. "Let's just have lunch first. I don't like to eat and do business at the same time."

He was acting kinda strange. Not like I had ever seen him before. I don't want to sound conceited or forward in anyway but, I get the feeling Dr. Hamilton has a bit of a crush on me. I have never heard him call anyone else 'Honey' before. And how often does he find out other test subject favorite sandwiches and treat them to lunch? It all seemed too uncomfortable to me. Maybe since his colleague Dr. Tachikawa is involved with Rosanne (it's so obvious) Dr. Hamilton wants a young fling of his own. Well, if he really knows more about me that I do, he'll know that I have Josh! 

I figured I would just eat, find out what he wanted to talk about and get out. I was starving and figured what would be the point of passing up a free lunch! I also was curious as to why Dr. Hamilton wanted to see me. Maybe I was going to get another upload. That would be so great. I would like to experience the cabin and the wild flowers. I wondered if I should ask for that? After lunch I figured, I would ask after lunch.
