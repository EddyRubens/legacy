I don’t like it that kathy is leaving. With her i could chat about everything and sometimes i had the feeling that she might be a real friend. We promised to write but i am afraid we won’t. Usually such a promise doesn’t stand and the writing stops soon.

But i can understand why kathy wants to leave. I think about leaving as well. I have been here for quite a long time and the kick from the beginning is over. Not that there’re no surprises, there are, but seldom nice ones. For example those fights. I don’t want to experience such situations. It doesn’t feel good. Then i think about going home. I am rather unstable about this, because i like those memories. And sometimes it is exciting, like the ear dropping on Steward.

Perhaps i must go home for a couple of days. That might be possible. I went home during christmas and new years eve and that wasn’t a problem either. And rosanne got her permission and Adrian went away for a couple of days to a crater lake. I would go home then to think about my future. I might be going back to the university. That is perhaps the good influence of a memory they gave me yesterday. I don’t know whose it was but it was about college. There was a lot to laugh about and i felt great. Now I want to go to college again. Therefore it was a good memory and perhaps the whole Braintec thing was good because of that. I was studying tourism but I could go through with that. I never went to school with joy. It wasn’t too difficult but i had difficulties with concentration and got bad remarks. My parents found being happy more important and suggested to raise a family. How could i be happy if i didn’t know what i wanted? I still don’t know, but braintec helps me with that. Now i know many things are possible. I think i am really into adventures. Therefore i will go back to college and finish my education. Working in tourism is better for me than the boutique.
