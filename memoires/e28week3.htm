Today I met Sonja again! My heart skipped three heartbeats when I saw her. I was strolling along the streets close to the blood bank when suddenly I saw her crossing the street. She was heading my direction! I rushed over to her and called her name. When she recognized me, she turned round and started running away from me. She disappeared into the crowd. I haven’t found her since.

It all happened so fast that I find it hard understanding what’s going on with me. Of course I was happy to see her, surprised and excited at the same. When she started running, I had most strange feelings. I think I was even screaming. I recognized her as being a sort of object meant for me. I had to get her and should possess her. The way she looked at me in such a scary way, was arousing me. Yacht instinct appeared. I didn’t fancy her as a prey but as a sexual object. I had to mate with her! 

I collapsed in the street, defeated. I couldn’t care less that I was in the middle of the street. The hell with all Japanese good manners. Sonja had escaped! For days I was hoping to meet her and now she ran away from me! Did I scare her through my ridiculous behavior? Did she actually see that my behavior suddenly was fundamental? I think I was running on both my legs. Glad I did. Imagine if I would follow her jumping on my hands and my feet!

I was fed up with everything and was dealing with my disappointment. I’m sure there would be another chance. Hopefully I didn’t scare Sonja too much and we could have a great time together next time. All my thoughts were interrupted by a man who was sitting on his heels. It was the doctor! The physician who took blood from me the other day and took me to the meeting! Again he passed me over me a city-plan. “Come to this location tomorrow morning at ten. Then many questions will be answered.” He continued walking. Surprisingly I was looking at this small piece of paper. Immediately I put it in my pocket. Answer to my questions. Sounds interesting.
