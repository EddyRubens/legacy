I have made a long walk with Sonja. We wanted to visit the Japanese garden and decided to go there on foot. Therefore we were already tired when we arrived at the garden and we dropped ourselves on one of the first benches. I guess I like Sonja, not only because she speaks Dutch. She has some sort of restful feeling which comforts me. Maybe because she is a bit older. Or perhaps because she is from Belgium. Her choice of words and her accent are warm and soft. That is nice. 

Sonja has some difficulties settling down at Braintec. I think she still doesn’t understand why she is here. She came in contact with Braintec through her husband. She didn’t say it with so many words, but I guess things were at a deadlock at home. Within a couple of weeks at Braintec she hopes to get her thoughts back on the rails. Perhaps only my interpretations; I sincerely hope so. ‘Coss Braintec is not a nursing home. The uploads can confuse you even more if you’re mentally not strong enough. But I’m probably completely mistaken and project my own uncertainties on her.

It was caused by her words about the old year. I asked her if she didn’t prefer to come one week later, so she would be able to celebrate New Year’s Eve with her husband at home. “No,” she answered frankly. “If I have something on my mind, I wish to go for it immediately. A new year, a new beginning.”  That was all she wanted to say about it. She wanted to know a lot about my time at Braintec. I have softened the sharpness of the facts a bit but I did tell her about the epilepsy. That seems to be fair. It didn’t shock her at all. After that I told her about Greenland and then she wanted to know if I miss the Netherlands. My confirmation came out very abrupt. Yes, I might miss the Netherlands more than I thought. The language, that’s for sure, but also the people. Family, friends and even people I hardly know. Sometimes I dream about them. How are they today? Did my sister find a new boyfriend yet? Did my friends get the recognition for their art, which they worked so hard for? It was time to change subject, ‘coss I started to feel weak. Let’s talk about Steward and Jessica. If you gossip, you can get yourself out of the picture.
