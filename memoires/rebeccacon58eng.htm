When I got back to my room, I found Carmen still asleep. Seeing her still there, asleep, was like a weight had been lifted from my shoulders. I woke her up and told her she should get ready to go. She looked at the clock and jumped up when she noticed the time was 5:10pm. "We're going to be late to dinner with the agents!" She said.

I decided to wait to tell her about Kaleb and Megan. She already had a rough day and I didn't want to stress her out any more than she already was. I waited until she went into the bathroom then I pulled out the business card that Agent Andrews had given me. I turned it over and read the phone number he had written on the back.  I wondered if I should call to inform him about Kaleb and Megan missing. I thought about it for a few minutes. Just when I decided that I would call, Carmen came out of the bathroom and said, "You ready to go? I'm starved!"

We left the room and went down the hall to the front desk. Adrian was standing there when we signed out to leave for dinner. He commented on how he was hungry as well, and he should find some place to have dinner. "...Some place quiet and romantic with a cute girl." Is what he said. I smiled and told him he shouldn't have a hard time finding a cute girl to have dinner with. Carmen elbowed me in the ribs. It was her cue for me to shut up and get going. I told him to enjoy his dinner and that we'd see him later. He just smiled as we walked out.
