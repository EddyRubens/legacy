After I put away all my things I was feeling a little better. Those butterflies finally went away. It must have been the feeling of being in a new place, away from familiarity.  

I am already missing Bruce and Josh. When I napped on the plane I had a dream that Josh proposed to me. I almost got right back on another plane to go home, but that would have been a wasted trip here to Oregon. Besides, if went home, I would most likely spend the rest of my life wondering about Braintec, the implants, and the memory recalls. Nope, first things first. I am here and I will follow through, then I will go home to my men. 

I touched up my makeup and put my hair in a ponytail. It has been an hour and a half since I arrived and I am waiting for the tour. I wonder if I am supposed to go back out to the room I was in earlier or if someone will come for me. I was hoping to ask Carmen. She still has not returned. How much testing can you do in one day? I'm sure they will come for me soon.  

In the mean time I might play solitaire on my laptop. There is a computer in here. It's small and looks kinda old. I'm not sure if it belongs to Carmen or Braintec. Good thing I have mine. I hate to depend on other people for things! 

This might sound horrible, but sitting in this room all alone with nothing to do, kinda makes me want to snoop. I have always been the kind of person who wants to know everything about everything and everything about everyone! When I was a kid I must have said, "Who?, What?, When?, Where?, Why?, and How?" about a million times! But I think I should be adult about it and let Carmen tell me about herself when I finally get to meet her. I think I better just stick to solitaire. 

I hear voices in the hall. It must be Rosanne and the rest of the group picking me up for the tour. I will add another entry when I return.
