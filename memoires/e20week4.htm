Kathy couldn’t be stopped. She was ready for a new upload and suddenly became hysterical. She shouted she didn’t want it any longer while pushing away almost everybody. Steward gave her a shot. I wasn’t there at the time but he told me. He thought I ought to know and asked me to stay with her when she woke up. It was nasty to see how they had tied her down. They didn’t know where her anger came from and perhaps it would return after the anesthesia.

She had been sleeping for more than two hours. I finished reading my magazine and I started feeling hungry. While getting a sandwich, she woke up. She was sitting up straight and luckily she didn’t need to be tied up again. She had called her dad. He should be here within just a bit more than one hour. That seemed to be a good idea. Kathy really looked so worried and I didn’t manage to comfort her. I sometimes have the impression that she is afraid of me.

Indeed mister Ferguson came later that afternoon. He wanted to take Kathy with him right away and asked me where to find her possessions. I took him to Dr. Hamilton. Steward was there too and together they explained that it shouldn’t be wise to take Kathy home for the time being. They couldn’t find the reason for her fear and on the other hand she still has her implant. Leaving now would be very foolish. “Why don’t you get out that thing!” mister Ferguson shouted. He posed in such a way that he was ready for fighting. Dr. Hamilton explained that Dr. Tachikawa is not here at the moment and that he prefers to delay an eventual operation simply for that reason. Steward promised that he would make a brain-scan the same day, while he didn’t expect something was wrong with Kathy physically. He trivialized the whole event. Mister Ferguson calmed down a bit but looked at us insistently. “If anything is wrong with my Kathy, even if you touch just one hair. You are not finished with me!” Steward acknowledged and hushed up the man even a bit more. After that he walked with us towards Kathy. 

Obviously Kathy was very happy to see her father. She seemed to be in much better condition than before. I suggested that mister Ferguson would stay for dinner, but he refused my offer. He returned to the rest of his family and promised to be back tomorrow.
