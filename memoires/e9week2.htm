It wasn’t difficult to choose a memory for today’s extraction session. I had passionately spoken about my journey in Greenland before and the team asked unanimously for a memory of that time. Dr. Tachikawa proposed to extract a memory of a trip with the dogsledge. Simple but yet complete. A sore throat blocked any verbal response.

The dogslegde trip! It’s obvious that Dr. Tachikawa remembered my love-experience on the sledge very well! It’s absolutely a fabulous memory but so private that I would never wish to share it with all the others. I wish I had never talked about it during that drunken night. My head felt like a huge red balloon while Dr. Tachikawa’s eyes glittered attractively. I wouldn’t mind to share my most intimate moments with Dr. Tachikawa, but definitely not with the rest of the crew.  What if Steward or Steve recalls my sexual splurges?

I could see at their sober poses that Dr. Hamilton and the other doctors had no idea what was going on between Dr. Tachikawa and me. Otherwise they would never have asked for this memory. Or would they? No matter what their reason was; they kept on asking for a memory of the dogsledge and had even started to prepare for the extraction. I desperately searched for an alternative, a memory or experience that would even outrank the dogslegde. But I couldn’t find any. “Let’s see how good you can screen this memory!”  Dr. Tachikawa smiled while he pushed me gently in the leather chair. Whatever. I’ve done so many trips on a dogslegde while I was in Greenland. If I had fully understood the procedure of the extractions, my most intimate moments wouldn’t be in any danger if I could concentrate on any other trip with a dogslegde. I couldn’t resist participating in Dr. Tachikawa’s little game and I smiled as sensual at him as I could. “Yes, let’s do just that. I know for sure the test subjects would love this.”

Why am I so attracted to this man? It seemed to be wise to keep this question unanswered before Dr. Tachikawa would get a role play in my dogslegde-memory. I tried to concentrate as much as possible and to focus all my thoughts at a certain trip in Tasiilaq. I had a lot of help from the hypnotizer in this. His relaxing words brought me back to the snow and the ice within a few moments. The cries of the dogs and the icy cold weren’t difficult to remind. The extracting moment is never experienced consciously. I still find that a pity. I was able to watch the videotape as compensation, but there wasn’t much to see on it. I was clearly under a deep hypnosis and my expressions revealed that I was in a different world. My eyes were slightly narrowed to protect them from icy wind while the corners of my mouth were stretched from joy. And now I sincerely hope that no unprotected thoughts were extracted.
