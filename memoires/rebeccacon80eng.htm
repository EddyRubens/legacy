As, I was getting ready to leave the suite, I noticed that Josh was gone. I thought nothing of it until I open the front door. Both Agent Andrews and Agent Jacobs were standing there. They both had a look on their face like I was some horrible criminal, like Dr. Tachikawa. They were positioned in a way as though they were ready to grab me if I was to try and run. Josh was standing behind them. "I'm sorry, Rebecca." He said.

Seconds After Josh's apology, both Agent Andrews and Agent Jacobs grabbed me. They started dragging me back into the Suite. I did my best to try to fight them off but the two of them were too strong. I started to scream for them to let me go. Agent Jacobs, who is physically the bigger of the two, pushed me to the floor and straddled me while holding my arms down. Josh was yelling at him to let me up and to stop being so rough. I heard Agent Andrews telling Josh that he was going to give me something to calm me down.

That's when I saw the needle. I was screaming and crying for Josh to help me. He was pacing back and forth with his hair clenched in his hands. Agent Andrews knelt down on the floor and inserted the needle into my arm. He smiled and said, "Nighty, night princess," in a condescending tone. The pain was fierce but only for a moment. I turned and looked at Josh one last time before the darkness. Tears were welling up in his eyes and again he said, "I'm sorry!"
