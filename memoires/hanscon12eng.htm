In the morning stand up early, we go to the Fuji! The train takes us out of town through monotonies suburbs and monotonies industrial space. After that is becomes beautiful. Through farmers grounds we drive into the mountains. After the final station there is a bus heading for Fuji. Even at this common week day several Japanese and some white persons go with the busses. A lot of trees in bloom on our way. The Japanese spring is marvelous. We may not enter the mountain. At the feet are temples from which there is a good view at the mountain. Japanese are performing rituals in front of the temples. An impressive sight. Here they sell several religious articles and souvenirs as well. We don’t make a nice price this time. The same truck twice is very unpredictable.

In the train back a fluently speaking Japanese tells us about the meaning of mount Fuji for Japan. He invites us to go home with him. With Paul’s warning in our mind we make an excuse. A pity, it might be a total innocent and nice contact. The same is happening in the guesthouse. The artist who arrived yesterday asks us to take a look inside his atelier. We refuse that as well, it is not to be trusted.
