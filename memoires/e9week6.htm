I have had a very terrifying experience. Suddenly all the colors disappeared. I was in the garden with Sally and Adrian, two new test subjects, and suddenly Sally's red jacket became grey and all the other colors from the trees, the grass and everything else, disappeared as well. As if someone filtered them out.

I totally panicked. The adrenaline stiffed my body. I had an unreal feeling; all of a sudden I realized that my whole life was just a movie. The heads of that VCR definitely needed to be changed! Sally and Adrian brought me to Dr. Hamilton. He immediately tried to make an EEG registration.  I flipped even more, ‘coss in my paranoid thoughts it was Braintec who had put me in this movie. I screamed and lashed out, shouting that I wanted out. They had to knock me out with an injection. The colors were back to normal when I woke up. It is still unknown why they had disappeared. My whole brain has been extensively screened and it seems to be working perfectly. The eyes were fine as well. Because no reason was found for this malfunction, the diagnosis is a temporary drop out or some kind of hallucination.  Whatever it was, I hope it won’t come back. Especially that paranoia scared me shitless.
