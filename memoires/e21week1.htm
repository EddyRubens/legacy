Kathy’s implant has been removed without Dr. Tachikawa’s presence. At this moment she is recovering from the operation. This morning when I visited her she was feeling alright. Obviously, she needs a couple of days to recover but she told me she felt fine already. I think she is mostly relieved. Glad that her implant is no longer in her head. “I’m satisfied with my own memories for the time being,” she told me. Nothing wrong with the idea staying in bed for a couple of days. Not only to recover from the operation but most of all from the time spent here at Braintec. She will miss a lot of things but at least just as many things she would want to get rid of. Kathy has never been so straightforward with me before. Maybe she feels free now.

It doesn’t matter to Kathy if she can never get an upload again. She is really fed up. I wouldn’t miss it, not for love or money. I’m almost dying to get another one. Today I will receive a memory of Lucas, tomorrow I get an extraction myself and the day after tomorrow I get one from Adelle. As an assistant test subject I know beforehand what sort of memories they are. Actually I like that. Enough surprises. ‘Coss when you go to the cinema you also would like to know what’s showing. It would be even better whenever I could decide which upload I can get on which moment. “Hmm, today I’m a bit melancholic. Let me upload Adelle’s memory.”  

The day after tomorrow Dr. Tachikawa will be back from Tokyo. I’m anxious to know what he thinks of Kathy’s developments. I wouldn’t be surprised if it all didn’t matter to him. It seems that he is only interested in his own stuff. All attention goes to his developments. Just the way it has always been.

I hope he brings good memories, straight from Japan. I have never been in Japan. Tokyo is one of the places I would really like to visit. I think it will be great getting another upload in a surrounding such as the Japanese garden very soon. Maybe I should suggest it to him when he returns. If he is too busy or he is not in the mood, he will surely deny again that we have been there before. And he will definitely tell me that I’m imaging things. I’m sure that illegal M-Stores are easy to get. I’m afraid so. Even Steward would have some. Sally saw it all. She told me he received a mysterious parcel.
