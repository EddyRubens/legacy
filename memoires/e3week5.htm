Last night I had such a strange dream. I dreamt there were young hamsters everywhere, my hamsters. A while ago I had been somewhat irresponsible and had caused way too many hamsters to be born. I needed to find care takers for these animals. But I had no time and forgotten them. Now I had lost them. I knew there were many and that they needed to eat. But I couldn’t find them anywhere. I searched the entire Braintec building. Even an old dusty attic that Braintec definately doesn’t have. Some employees asked me what I was looking for but I didn’t want to tell them. I was ashamed of causing so many hamsters to be born and now I had even lost them. So might already be dead, because I hadn’t fed them for a long time. Before I found even one hamster I woke up. Wide awake. I was all sweaty, and the sheets were all sticky because of it. For a long time I sat upright in bed. What an odd dream. I couldn’t get it out of my head. Hamsters! What business do I have with hamsters? I grabbed the rabbit and cuddled it until I calmed down a bit. Still it took me another hour to fall back to sleep.
