Today it was such a busy one. In the morning we have extracted a memory of my fourth birthday and I’ve got it uploaded in the afternoon. Such sessions are tiresome. Physically I am a wreck by now –headache, sickness and my muscles feel as if I run a marathon- but that doesn’t matter at all. ‘Coss such an upload is really great. I am still enjoying the memory.

An extraction is very tiresome because you have to focus your mind so long and so intensively. I’m glad the hypnotizer helps with that, but afterwards I’ve always the feeling that it’s the better to stay in my bed for the rest of the day. Other test subjects with a MESI don’t think it’s that tiring as I do, so I have to be careful not to play the idiot. Perhaps I was just more tired because we had to dig deeper to get this memory. I liked the Disney-memory of Robbert so much, that I asked to recall a memory from my own childhood. I couldn’t remember much and it was even harder to know in which year it happened. “That doesn’t matter.” the hypnotizer said, called Lennard Victoroff  “If we both concentrate on that, it will all come back.” That was an exiting prospect. We did need some kind of guidelines and therefore we chose my fourth birthday.

During the hypnosis and right afterwards I suddenly remembered how it was to be four years old. But only during the upload I really experienced being four. Fantastic. It is a memory I love to keep in mind as long as possible. I was a little queen, the shining center of all the excitement. I don’t remember a cake. I thought I would blow four candles on a cake, very common, but there wasn’t any. The odd perspective was perhaps the most fascinating thing. The table was so high that I couldn’t clearly observe what was on it. And I had to pull at people’s legs to get their attention. That wasn’t a problem with the children from my neighborhood. Ronald, Jenny en Annemieke visited us and my nephew Henk as well. They just did what they wanted while it was my birthday. Suddenly this queen became a dictator, because they were not allowed to touch the new toys. Unbelievable, how small and well-organized the world is for a four-year-old child. Except for the perspective.

I have a good feeling about my childhood. Such a recall isn’t easy to let go. It opens a lot of doors and I was totally forgotten what was behind them. The memories come back in my mind as a chain reaction. How I had a fight with Annemieke, how it felt when daddy was angry and above all my cuddly-toys. I have been thinking about my teddies during the whole evening. I used to find dolls boring, but I have spent many years with little animal friends. Sometimes I was the vet and then they would all get a bandage. And I played monopoly with them quite often. But I was older then. I almost think that I miss my cuddly-toys. Fortunately I have my grey rabbit now.
