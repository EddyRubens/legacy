That was an impressive memory! I can understand way this is Rosanne’s favorite. For her, this was all about the discovering of a UFO but for me it was something else. But definitely as good. I was the alien. The laws of gravitation were different than usual. My legs were swinging enormously while walking. And I seemed to see the world completely different, as if I saw it for the first time. Everything was so strange and impressive. An apple, the clouds, an unfamiliar piece of equipment. I felt breezily and excited. 

Once, when I was in Amsterdam with a couple of friends, I felt the same. We had smoked a joint in a coffee shop and walked in the red-light district. On air I walked and it weren’t only the women who looked attractive. I could even fall in love with the streetlight!

When I told this to Rosanne, she felt caught. I wonder if she was under the influence of something. That’s why I asked her if she ever smoked a joint. Without batting an eyelid she answered that in Holland everyone above sixteen has smokes one. Shall it have been weed or something else? A boozer can see them fly as well of course, but I think that’s different.
