After I ate my lunch with Dr. Hamilton, he really did have something he wanted to talk about. He said he wanted me to experience two more uploads to make a total of three. Then he wanted to do an extraction. I was excited for this. Now maybe Carmen could experience something exciting from me! I had all these ideas for good extraction's. Like the time Carrie and I went to New Orleans "to research the old plantations." At least that was the excuse we told the professors as to why we were going to miss a week and a half of school. Of course we spent two days researching, completely hung over after a week long Mardi Gras celebration! Carrie swears we were flashing everyone for beads, but I don't remember doing it!

Or there was the time Josh and I flew to Florida and pretended we were newlyweds. He got this promotion through his work for newlywed couples. They got to stay for a week in an Alternative Resort in Key West at half price. It was an adult resort called the Atlantic Shores Resort. (Adult meaning that in certain areas clothing was optional! We only walked by those areas getting small glimpses of people and laughing about it later. It was crazy!) The plane tickets were half price as well, and the food was all complimentary! We had so much fun! 

As I rambled on, Dr. Hamilton sat and listened. He told me those were all good ideas for extraction's, but what he had in mind was different. He wanted to extract the first time I can remember being afraid of bees. He wanted "...to get the worst over first..."  I wasn't too happy about this but I told him I would do it under one condition, that the day after the extraction I wanted the cabin and wild flowers upload. He said that wouldn't be a problem.  

I watched him write on a calendar in my file. He wrote on tomorrow's date, "Ocean Walk," the day after that; "Grand Canyon" next day; "First Exraction-Bee Memory," then; "Hillside Cabin." After he was done writing he looked up at me. I turned my head in time that he didn't know I saw what he was writing. I pretended to be reading this framed poster behind the desk in his personal office. I have a feeling someone had it made for him because it read, " The mind is a terrible thing to waste, let Braintec extract it all first." I smiled to myself and then asked Dr. Hamilton if we were done here. He said yes, but he wanted me to arrive at his office about half hour before my upload tomorrow. There was something he wanted to discuss with me. I asked if he wanted me to stay now, but he said it would be best to talk about it in the morning.  

I thanked him for the lunch and left. I was kind of relieved to be out of there. As I was walking to my room I passed Carmen in the hall. She was staring out a window. When I approached her I asked her if everything was okay. I could tell she had been crying. I motioned her back to our room. I told her we could talk alone there. She said to me, "All you ever want to do is talk! You don't know me like you think you do. I don't always tell you everything! Besides, I don't feel like talking right now! So just leave me alone!" 

I was shocked that she would talk to me like that. I just walked away not sure what to say or do. I wondered if her conversation with Diego had gone sour and she was taking it out on me. Or could she really mad at me for some reason? I decided I wasn't going to let her remarks hurt my feelings. She was obviously stressed and just needed to be alone for a while to clear her mind. I'm sure thing will be back to normal tomorrow.
