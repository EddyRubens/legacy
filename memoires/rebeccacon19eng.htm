I woke up to a big surprise this morning. Carmen was back. She said the doctors saw she was only getting better and bored as the days passed so they decided to release her. Yea! I felt so happy! Kinda like the feeling you get when you lose something important to you, then find it again!  

I told her everything that was going on. She was laughing hysterically about the whole Sheila thing. "That's my girl!" she said still laughing! After that she asked how thing were with me and Josh. That's when I decided to tell her about the letter I received from him the day before surgery.  She said that maybe It was time I let him know how I really felt. So with that suggestion, I decided that I would write Josh an honest email. It read:

Dear Josh,

I have decided to write you this letter hoping that you will understand how I feel about you and why I need to continue my stay at Braintec. I think you know how I feel about you. I think you have known for a long time. If saying it will make you believe it, than that's what I will do. I like you Josh, more than just friends. When people ask how I feel about you, I tell them I love you. Yes,...I love you!  Now you know it's true. If you do not feel the same, I will understand and only hope it will not ruin the friendship we have.

As, for Darcy, I feel the letter you wrote me concerning her was entirely inappropriate. You know I don't care for her, and your last letter hurt my feelings. If you choose to spend your time with her while I am away, that is up to you. I would just prefer you don't write to me about it.

As for my stay here...I admit at first it was just one of those things where I wanted to know what it was all about. But now that I am here, I believe there is so much more to it. Dr. Hamilton has made his career here believing that this is something that could inevitably cure Alzheimers and Dementia. He even believes that it may cure my phobias! Wouldn't that be great! Well, Josh, I must get going. I hope all is well. Give Bruce my love.

Always,
Rebecca

Ten minutes later, I received a reply to my letter. It was short and to the point. It read:

Rebecca,

I love you, too!  I will wait for you no matter how long it takes. 

Josh

P.S. Darcy is gone. Bruce bit her.

I was in shock after I read his letter! He feels the same! Oh, I was so happy! Still am! Of course I had to run and tell Carmen. She was happy for me, too. Things seem to be changing for the better around here!  

I need to make this short, Dr. Hamilton has promised me my first upload today. His morning was busy, so he scheduled me for a 5:00 appointment. I am sure Sheila will be there. I hope she doesn't ruin things for me! I will write when I return.
