Today I saw Sonja! After going through those vague situations earlier this week I simply intended to spend a peaceful day. Some shopping in town. So many things to see over there! While I was strolling about the streets, she suddenly pulled me into an alley. It scared me out! Until I saw her standing there. I hugged her for a moment, for I was very glad to see her. Then she pulled me along, to a stand of ramen.

So many things I wanted to ask her and tell her. Patiently she let me do the talking. But she ignored my questions carefully by questioning me more. After that she showed me a few photos on the display of a small digital camera. Did I know that guy? Although it was such a small camera, the picture was clear. I immediately recognized the man. It was that boring man organizing our ‘meeting’ at the blood bank yesterday! I told Sonja about it and she seemed pleased with my information. I also told her that I ended up there only by coincidence when I just had arrived in Japan. “Putting your head straight into the lion’s mouth,” Sonja argued.

She was rather skinny. From time to time her eyes seemed to point at all directions. She didn’t feel quite comfortable. What could I do to help her? She didn’t know for sure. “Watch that Canadian,” she warned me. “He is the man who kidnapped me a long time ago. He is a member of the PNGO, a secret organization dealing with bad intentions.” I remembered her talking about that earlier. I understood while I nodded my head. Might she be exaggerating? Was she paranoia or was the truth an over-simplified opinion? In one movement I showed her a few large banknotes. I wanted to give them to her. “Otherwise you end up sooner or later in a blood bank yourself,” I fooled while I passed on the money to her. She refused it. Did she think it was polite refusing money or was she afraid to take money from me? If she even doesn’t trust me anymore…

She had enough money, she said, and wanted to leave. “Wait just another minute,” I tried. “I want to see you again soon. Shall we set a date?” She was taken by surprise through my question. She was thinking seriously and then she nodded her head. “No, I know where to find you when I need it. Remember very well that you cannot trust that man from the West. The whole of Braintec is an organization better staying out of it, even today and don’t wait until tomorrow. But I realize it’s asking too much of you. See you later, Rosanne. Take care!” I muttered the same kind of words and saw her walking away quickly. Strange girl. It was good to see her again. I like her very much.
