I cannot help looking at this huge bouquet which I got for Valentine every now and again. I have already read this poem more than a hundred times. Who could it be? It is a beautiful poem although I do not like poems very much. Somebody who cannot sleep at night and thinks of me. Mmmmh. But who? I still hope it’s Kodjiro. Would he be able to write such a poem? ‘Coss Japanese rather prefer to write an haiku. In this poem also comparisons with nature can be read, so who knows…I think it must be a romantic person anyway. And a shy type of person who is afraid of talking. Dammit, why all of this should be so thrilling?

Sonja really likes this tension. Together we wrote down all people we know through Braintec on a list. “Anybody who is here shorter than two weeks is off the list.” she decided and crossed out a few names. “Why?” I asked. “It can be love at first sight?” Sonja did not share my naive vision and called the concept a mythical invention. “In this poem you can clearly read that the writer has experienced your memories.” She declared. If we conclude on this, many persons will be left out. For instance Dr. Tachikawa.

“Steward!” Sonja screamed suddenly. “I think it is Mark Steward!” We could not stop laughing. He is the last person I would think of to give me flowers. And Sonja knew it. I got frightened by the idea of getting to know him better. But I have to admit that he had been looking at me already a few times. And as far as we know he is not married. It would explain the complicated relation between him and Dr. Tachikawa. “No, Sonja,” I said when my last convulsion of laughter stopped. “I don’t want to think of that, out of the question. Cross out!”

After some more speculations we came to the conclusion than there was no other way out than hunting for handwritings. Actually the poem had been written by hand. It is most likely that the sender would be found through this way. But how could we manage to collect samples of handwritings from the people mentioned on our list? Everything here is automated in every detail. We decided to investigate all files secretly. You never know if we could find some handwritten notes in them. But no, apart from a few they would all refer to Jessica’s and Sheila’s. Then Sonja came up with a splendid idea. “We will buy a card for Robbert who tends to be in a depression and will ask everybody to write something on it!” Smart girl. Everybody knows Robbert is divorcing his wife. Very convincing!
