After our night on the futon we started taking a lot of uploads. Adrian had roughly about thirty carrying with him and I’m afraid we have experienced all of them at least ten times. We couldn’t get enough of it.

Just for one short moment I left the apartment to purchase a few large bottles of coke and a large stock of noodle-dishes in a small shop just round the corner. We only had to add some boiling water to get something to eat. Because the room had been darkened, we had no idea about time. We ate when we were hungry, slept when we got tired and took an upload whenever we wanted. Especially that last thing we almost did constantly.

In the beginning I was afraid of the monkey-uploads. However, they were no scaring experiences but the fact they were dealing with animals I found a bit weird. And afterwards it gave me such a strange feeling. Moreover I was worried about the punishment of those animals. According to the memories it was quite clear that they lived in free nature. Apart from a few that probably came from the zoo. Where are they now, those monkeys? Are their lives still worthwhile? I didn’t quite understand the need of these experiments. Maybe they give those animals human memories as uploads. What shall happen to them if they do so? Obviously that curiosity motivated their self-interest.

Anyway, fortunately Adrian also carried human memories with him. When I got fed up with that, and when I saw how happily Adrian was jumping through the room, I also dared trying one from his favorite small box. Indeed it was fantastic. I believe I’ve never felt so much freedom before. I was carrying a baby on my tummy and felt proud as a peacock. Together we jumped across the trees. I didn’t feel any stress and nothing to worry about. As far as I’m concerned this gorgeous moment would last forever.
