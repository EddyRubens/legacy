It is not only Adelle who can remember so intensively! This morning I got an upload of the memory of Lucas’ parent’s home and I felt very confused all morning.

I remembered a decent interior of a home. A protected environment. I was brought up here with my father, mother and older brother. It is that particular brother I cannot let go. I never had a brother. Two sisters alright, but that is completely different. My brother Tom was some kind of a hero to me. Actually his name was Thomas, but we always called him Tom. Sometimes it is boring to have a brother. He has a larger room, was allowed to stay up late and had more football-pictures than I had. Whenever I bought a package containing a rare picture, my luck only lasted for a very short time. In one way or another he managed to manipulate me in such a way that I exchanged my picture for a pile of doubles which he said were worth double as much. However, I love Tom. If somebody teased me or if I had a fight with mom, he always helped me out. Everything I knew about the girls, I knew from him. He even sometimes helped me with my homework! And whenever he had nobody to play with, we played soccer together in the garden. There we had this huge goal. Of course Tom could play soccer better than I did. He was good in everything and wanted everybody to know. I suppose I have lived years and years in Tom’s shadow. 

Once I could really kill him. Although I had horrible acne I finally found a girl-friend. Her name was Linda. I was mad about Linda and especially about her long hair. Linda was dressed in the latest fashion and was a star in tennis. I thought I was gonna marry Linda. Together we would buy this large house with a dog. But my brother liked Linda as well. That bad, that he joined us to the movies. He insisted bringing her home that night. Since then Linda was no longer my girl-friend anymore. The worst thing was that he gave up on her after only a couple of weeks. Short afterwards Tom went to university and left home to live on his own.

Time heals all wounds and now I love Tom again, just as much as before I started going out with Linda. I really miss him. Since I moved to Ireland a few years ago due to my work, I haven’t seen him that often. He was not there at Christmas Eve! Now that I’m here in Portland, Tom feels so far away. Shall I call him? Immediately I shake off this whole idea. Who the hell is Tom?
