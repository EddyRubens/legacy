I cannot remember how I got into bed. Was it a dream? Could it be real, the experience with Kodjiro in the Japanese garden? Kodjiro gave me an upload while sitting on this bench. Jessica’s memory! I can clearly remember how it was. Usually dreams are not so clear. Jessica was in love with me. I was looking at myself and I was in love with myself. That is very narcissistic!

If that upload was real, it meant that meanwhile Jessica also got an implant. How did she get use of that? And where is she now? And why Kodjiro wanted me to experience this memory again? So many questions, so many clues. The most mysterious clue is the question how the hell I got into my bed. Even if the whole upload is only but a dream. How can I end up in bed? Last thing I remember is that Zen-period. Dr. Tachikawa and I were sitting on that bench talking about the strength of simplicity. And now I’m lying here with a complete chaos in my head! I cannot deal with that. 

It was much too early in the morning, but still I had to find him. Chaos was unbearable. He was not in his office. At last I found him behind his computer in the laboratory. He turned around scared. “Ah, Rosanne! It’s you.” Was he hiding something? He behaved in such a strange way. “Talking about yesterday,” I started. “Did we really sit on that bench together and did I really get Jessica’s upload from you?” Dr. Tachikawa laughed at me in a pleasant way. “No, not at all.” He waved his hand like trying to push away a fly. “I think you imagine the whole thing?” Dr. Tachikawa opened his eyes widely and looked at me intensively. “Yes, I thought so,” I answered and stared at the floor. Nobody should know. Strange things are happening here and I do not have a clue. Dr. Tachikawa’s big eyes seem to acknowledge my experience. But what happened after that upload? When Dr. Tachikawa and I were still in the garden, it was half past five in the evening. I woke up at four in the night. What happened meanwhile? I really don’t like it when I cannot understand the situation. It frightens me.
