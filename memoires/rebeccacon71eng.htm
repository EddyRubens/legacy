For some reason, I couldn't get it out of my head as to why Kaleb just left us here. Did he have a plan, or did he just have enough of this place? Even though he was with the CIA, he had no personal obligations to any of us. Would he do that? Would he just turn his back on us when he promised to help?

Carmen said, "It's too bad that we can't go down to where they stored his stuff. I wonder if anything he left here could help us ... you know, like a clue." I started to laugh. "Jinkies, Scooby Doo,...you think we should investigate?" I said to her. "I guess that did sound pretty stupid, huh!" Carmen said while laughing.

A while later, Carmen and I decided to walk around the facility to kill some time. We wanted to look in Kaleb's room. Was it still empty? Did they put someone else in there? We wanted to know. Also, Braintec welcomed some new test subject a few days ago. If all their testing was done, they should be getting ready for their surgeries. We wanted to go see what they were like.

When we reached Kaleb's old room, I knocked on the door. There was no answer. I knocked again. Since there was still no answer I reached out to the doorknob to see if it would open. Just when I was trying to turn the obviously locked knob, a voice shouted at Carmen and I. "Hey! What are you two ladies doing here?" We both turned to our right to see one of the security nurses coming our way. I looked at Carmen and quickly whispered for her to go along with what I was saying. She nodded. "I said...What are you doing here?" He repeated in a sarcastic tone.
