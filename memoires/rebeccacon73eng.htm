We pulled into the parking lot of the Staybridge Suites in no time at all. The place was beautiful. A large brick building surrounded with greenery and lights. It slightly resembled a castle. I thought that was a bit ironic...A princess in distress (me), a prince waiting in a castle (Josh), ready to rescue me from the evil dragon. (Dr. Tachikawa) Sort of like a fairytale.

Anyhow, we got out of the car and walked up the brick paved path. Just before the entrance to the front doors, I couldn't help but notice the covered oning held up with pillars. The lights shining off the pillars and down from the covered top gave it an enchanting effect. When we walked into the lobby, the atmosphere gave off nothing but a welcoming feeling. I felt a bit guilty letting myself feel relaxed and calm, when Megan must be fearing for her life and Kaleb clinging tight to his. There were many amenities here. The Agents were obviously use to traveling in comfort. I wasn't sure how to absorb all that was surrounding me. I myself, was a little more use to the 'Motel 6' or one of the other well known low rate motels. Josh and I did travel to a few nice hotels when he got the promotions through work, but that was only because we stayed for free or half price.

Carmen and I walked in silence to the front desk. I think we both felt exhausted with everything that has been happening recently. We waited for a moment at the desk when Carmen finally broke our mutual silence saying, "Do you think we should have called or something? I mean, they're not expecting us until another hour."  I told her it was probably better that we just showed up. "It won't hurt to keep them on their toes." 

When the woman arrived to the front desk, she gave an obvious fake smile and asked, "How can I help you ladies this beautiful evening?" Carmen circled to face the lobby. I knew she turned around so that 'Heather' (Which is what the woman's name tag read) wouldn't see her laughing. Heather ruined the beauty of the place. Though she must have been close to my age, if not younger, her skin looked worn and leathery. Her platinum blonde hair was in huge curls billowing on her shoulders and her make-up was way over done. Everything I have read in the magazines that were labeled as, Fashion No-No's, Heather mistook as Fashion Know-how's.

I told Heather that we were suprising some friends who had come to visit. "Their names are David Andrews and Marcus Jacobs. They have two other friends with them, do you need their names as well?" Heather continued her fake smile and said, "Please hold on for one moment, Thank you!" Her false fingernails were all I could hear clicking against the keyboard of her computer. She must have been typing in their names to locate them. She stared at the screen of her computer and said, "Hmmmmm....Could I ask you ladies to make yourselves comfortable in our 'Great Room?' I need a moment to make one quick phone call before I can proceed." Carmen turned back around to face Heather. She said, "But if you call them, it won't be a suprise." Heather never lost her phony smile since we arrived. Her cheeks must have been killing her. She replied to Carmen, "I'm truly sorry ma'am. But here at the Staybridge Suites, we are obligated to the privacy of our guests. Especially those whom occupy more that one suite on our 'Excutive-Club Floor'."

Our plan to suprise them at their suite doors was apparently not going to happen. I told Heather when she makes the call to tell Andrews or Jacobs to send down Josh and Diego. "Josh and Diego?" Heather repeated in a confused tone. "Trust me," I said. "They'll know what It means" She stared at Carmen and I for a moment. Then we walked into the huge room with the fireplace.  I could only assume this was the 'Great Room.' We didn't sit. We stood with our backsides to the fireplace warming our rears. There was one other person in the room, an older gentleman, maybe in his 60's. He was sitting to our left, lost in a novel. I watched Heather nodding her head up and down, and glancing over at us while she was on the phone. She still wore her phony smile. After a brief conversation on the phone, she hung up the receiver and walk away.

I ran over and gave Josh a big hug. It felt like I hadn't seen him in years. "How's my girl?" he said to me. "Better now that your in my arms." I said back to him. Diego had his arm around Carmen's waist. "We should get back upstairs, there is a good plan waiting to happen." Diego said motioning for us all to follow. Josh and I held hands as we headed for the Grand Staircase. I was incredibly eager now to share with the Agents, Kaleb and Megan's whereabouts. Hopefully they will be included in 'the plan.' If not, I can only hope Mr. Isakova will find them.
