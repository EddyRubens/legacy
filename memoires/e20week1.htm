I could not help laughing very loud when I realized that I openly admitted to the impulse to attend keep-fit exercises. Standing beside my bed I was stretching and bending. Actually it felt good. I think I must have been twelve when I attended such exercises last.

My parents had a voluminous medical encyclopedia which I had gone through now and again. Diseases and illnesses with illustrative photos attached to it. At the end of the book some twenty exercises with drawings how to keep healthy and fit. By bending the spinal column I could avoid getting the most horrible skin-diseases, open wounds on legs and other awful disorders.

Why was I again fascinated in making so much effort with my body? For a long time I have not seen disgusting pictures and meanwhile I do not believe anymore in the protective power of all those exercises. Surprisingly I choose low-fat cheese for breakfast. What is wrong with me? Why did I go for the healthy way?

The pounding of my heart skipped a few times when I saw that Kathy was dealing with the same healthy-maniac-method. As long as I know her she takes four spoonfuls of sugar into her tea. Now she drank without! She got a bit frightened when I told her that. She had almost emptied her glass but when hearing my remark she suddenly realized she had forgotten to take sugar. “Oh well, I also like it this way,” she discovered in a calm way. You could see she did not agree at all. Frightened and scared she looked at me. It would be wiser not to mention anything about it.

I asked Kathy if she had more uploads after my UFO-experience. She got excited when she told me that she saw Adelle’s niece on stage yesterday. The quality of this upload also took her by surprise. Actually I wanted to know if she knew anything about the miscarriages but I did not dare asking her. It would be wrong. If I were Adelle I wouldn’t like anybody to know about it. We were chatting about details of Helen’s dress and so on, until Kathy left for her appointment. I decided to pay a visit to Adrian. Maybe he could make use of my help. Yesterday it appeared he had a brainwave when he tried to explain how his DOSS-system works. But I couldn’t let go the fact that both Kathy and I are worrying about our diet. Especially after we experienced Adelle’s memory again!
