Before and during the Christmas days I have completely worked my guts out. Dream on script didn’t work, but the sounds of a dream into a computer file.......THAT HAS WORKED.

One day after my download of Rosanne’s dog-sledge adventure I started to work on this. It was a tremendously clear download. I saw the dogs, I saw Rosanne on the sledge, in the snow, everything came through my mind very clear. And above all, I heard sound. The barking of the dogs, the grating upon the gliding of the sledge on the snow. It was freezing cold. The pole-wind in my face and inside, within my body, very warm. But especially the sound. I could even decide which way the dog-sledges came from. It all sounded very clear. I also heard the Eskimo’s, they whistled for the dogs ordering a specific kind of code. They stimulated the dogs to go faster and faster. It was ecstasy. I have really enjoyed this download. It was completely done. And afterwards I’ve got the refreshing idea, to split the memory into image, sound, smell and feeling. The analyzing of the memories brings some kind of simplification. Analyze of all the ingredients. I thought, I must start at the basic. No DOSS (Dream On Script Scriber) but dreams in sound, let’s start with that.
