To whom it may concern:

This is most probably the last page of my diary. It is no longer safe for me to stay here. I’ll tell you what happened: Yesterday evening I came ‘home’ late. I had been to the pub with Lucas. When we walked to our rooms, we saw Steward in the kitchen. He spoke to someone we both didn’t met before. That made us curious. There are so many things going on here that we didn’t understand. We spoke about that in the pub and now we had the possibility to reveal some of the secrets. We thought. Well, to make a long story short: we found out that Steward is indeed dealing with the pornographic industry. This mate of him, the fellow we hadn’t met before, came to pick up some new memories. Steward sold them for $5000 each! The bastard!

We tried to stay calm but we couldn’t. Maybe it was the alcohol who let us leave too noisy. Steward heard us leaving the kitchen entrance and grabbed us. What do you think you were doing? he asked. I told him that we only wanted to have some water after the alcohol we drank. It was the first thing on my mind. It was ridiculous. We could of course get some water in our rooms as well. Steward made something very clear. That we had all the reason for being afraid of him. That our future was in his hands. He frightened me. Suddenly I believed all the rumors. About Sonja, Hans and Heather and I’ve also heard that even Rosanne has to fear for her life now. I have never felt so endangered in my whole life.

That’s why I’ve left. If there’s one thing I can’t deal with, it is fear. You might think I’m a coward but I’m not. This is just my way to survive. This is just my way of taking my life in my own hands again. I have learned more during my time at Braintec’s than I could ever dream of. Staying longer just gives me too much stress and I have to learn for my exams. Those exams make it too difficult to come up with another escape or solution. I gave Braintec some weeks but I can’t give them my life, if you know what I mean. And I realy don't fancy Tokyo. I don’t know what Lucas will do. I’ve left the same evening. I think it’s no problem to keep the implant for a while. I’ll let Hamilton remove it after my exams. Perhaps it helps me learning...

I wish you all the best and I hope you find a way to survive this project as well. Good luck at take care,

Steve.
