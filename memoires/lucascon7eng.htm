I am almost entirely recovered, and Nursie’s message made me alert. I constantly feel the urge to scratch but despite my expectations the wound has healed very quickly. (they have to open it again of course). 

I also had my first upload. They told me it would be a UFO experience, but.... I only saw some vague spots and I noticed that the sound was really weird. As if they played a tape backwards. A bit like that Pink floyd track on Wish you were here. Rosanne seems to be disappointed that I can’t share her thoughts about the UFO. 

A nice conversation with Sonia. She thinks Rosanne’s search for the sender of the valentine bouquet is rather daft. I’ve got the impression she likes the romance more than the concrete part of knowing who. And I join her in that. What is more alluring than to fantasize about a secret lover. For someone who searches it is more valuable than to find out.

Then I have to decide which memory I want them to up-load... (giggle) there are some that would definitely be appreciated by the male, but I guess the ladies won’t be that charmed.... so I choose for my trip to the Mount Everest. Not the whole tour of course (took 26 days) but the moment that I reached the top of a mountain track and could see the famous mountain itself for the first time. The combination of deadly tiredness, hunger, out of breath and the overwhelming view at the same time. I hope this transfer can bear such emotions. Or should they be only images?
