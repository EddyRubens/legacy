Out of all the people it turned out to be Steward who would recall my dogslegde memory. I really don’t like that man. There is something in his behavior that makes me distrust him. I think he might be doing bad things behind people’s backs. And all of his memories I recalled this far, had a nasty aftertaste. Sneaky and dull. So I didn’t like him to recall my first complicated memory. The possibility that some private fragments were accidentally extracted as well was still on my mind. It made me fear and dislike Steward as the first to upload this memory even more. Dr. Tachikawa understood my fear and I think that was the reason why he wanted me to witness this upload.  Normally it is prohibited for test subjects to be present at the sessions. Dr. Hamilton had already told me that he didn’t appreciate my presence. But I could stay for the sake of becoming a good guide of the new-coming test subjects. Oh, yes, for the sake of Dr. Tachikawa’s kicks of course!

The upload took remarkably little time, but Steward could talk for ages. He obviously didn’t like the cold as much as I do. He spoke about Naoyak, the daughter of Aqqaluk and Klara, who taught me to sing Greenlandic songs. But he didn’t mention anything about Stig or Dr. Tachikawa. Thank God for that.
