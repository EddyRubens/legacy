I was almost drunk when I entered Elisa’s bar. Adrian was cuddling Elisa and to my great surprise Elisa looked at me in a friendly way. “Sorry, about last time,” I muttered. “I will behave from now on.” She nodded very quickly and walked over to the other side.

We were looking for a table and ordered another bottle of wine. I was just seated when Adrian shoot questions on me. “What do you know about Kodjiro’s activities? Do you have a clear picture what is going on?” In the first place I told him things he already knew. About fights Kodjiro had with Dr. Hamilton, about the strange situation Sally had witnessed and about a few suspicious remarks coming from Steward. It was quite clear to Adrian that Steward knew all about it. Although he doesn’t know the role he is playing. Adrian explained that, not long ago, he had asked him why he hadn’t gone to Japan together with Kodjiro. He answered that he wasn’t interested in that at all and that he would be better off with Dr. Hamilton. When Adrian was questioning again, Steward didn’t say a word. What a pity. I would like to know what that man is trying to invent in his head. He is not to be trusted.

After that we talked about Sonja. Yesterday all test subjects received her email, in which she mentioned that she had been kidnapped. I was shocked when I heard her story. According to her, Braintec is only part of a much larger organization called MemoCorp. They say they are inventing equipment for the Pentagon and the CIA which can allow us to shoot in our head a smaller version of our implants, at a distance of 200 meters. This was serious matter and I had problems concentrating on the conversation. I just cannot imagine that Dr. Tachikawa would cooperate in such a thing. And the idea she will be murdered is like going paranoia. Such things do not happen in reality, do they?

Adrian also didn’t know how to handle Sonja’s story. Since he received this email he doesn’t feel comfortable anymore. Imagine if it’s true, then are we also in danger? Sonja advised us to leave Braintec as soon as possible. Supporting a weapon of war is the last thing Adrian wanted to do. At this moment Sonja would be in Tokyo. We wonder what she is doing over there. She was rather behaving mysteriously about it. Anyway, together we agreed that things hadn’t turn out better since Dr. Tachikawa was gone. I asked Adrian which person he would vote for, Dr. Hamilton or Dr. Tachikawa, if he was to make a choice. “Kodjiro,” he answered without hesitation. “He pays more for the job and gives me more freedom. Same with you?” That was the easy way out. No doubt, I would vote for Kodjiro. “But what if Sonja’s story is true….” I began. I couldn’t finish my sentence. Things were turning through my head. Alcohol was working stronger. A serious discussion was out of the question.
