Last night, when I got back to my room, Carmen was already asleep in her bed. The gift I had gotten her was placed on my bed. There was a note attached to the back of it. It read:

Rebecca, You have been so nice to me. You are like the sister I never had and always wanted. But, I have done something very wrong! I want to tell you because I know you, of all people, can help me. But I am afraid you will judge me for my actions. I don't know what to do and I am very afraid! I can't tell Diego for I know he will leave me. If you choose to, meet me in the Japanese Gardens, tomorrow at 2:30 and I will explain everything to you. Please don't tell anyone where you are going. I could get very serious trouble with Braintec. If you don't come I will understand. You may want to think twice if you choose to be involved. Thank you for the monkeys. They did make me smile, but I don't feel I deserve them.
Carmen 

I can't imagine what this could be about. Is she going to tell me that she is pregnant? Is the baby not Diego's? So many things went racing through my mind. I wanted to wake her up and talk to her. But I knew the best thing would be to wait. 

She just sounded so desperate in her letter. I figured I would go see Carmen in the Japanese Gardens at 2:30 and hopefully make it back by 6:00 for the Grand Canyon Uplaod I was supposed to be getting next. If I missed it, Dr. Hamilton would surly want to know where I was. Then he would most likely get Sheila involved. Whatever Carmen is involved in, I will have to keep quiet about it. It sounds serious! So, for Carmen sake, I will have to be back on time.

It is 12:00 now. I will shower then go meet Carmen. What could this be about?
