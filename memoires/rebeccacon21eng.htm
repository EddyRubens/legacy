Last night, after dropping off my apology letter to Sheila's office, I walked over to the communion-room. Carmen seemed a bit depressed and turned down my offer so I was flying solo.  

When I walked in, I saw two girls talking. I asked if I could sit with them. They seemed hesitant, but gave in, It was like they didn't want me to pick up on their uneasiness. To late for that! They introduced themselves as Sally and Kathy. They seemed to have the same kind of friendship Carmen and I have. Kathy asked if I had any uploads yet. I shared that I had my first one that night. She told me to never let them do the UFO M-Store to me. She said it would mess me up! I smiled and shared the fact that Adrian told me it was one of the better ones! Then she sort of flipped out and yelled "He's in on it too! There all in on it! They're gonna get you, but their NOT going to get me!" My eyes shifted to Sally. She had a shocked look on her face. I just stood up and said, "I think it's time I get going to bed." I smiled and walked away. But being the nosey ninny that I can be, I hid around behind the door frame to see what Kathy was going to say next.  

I heard Sally ask, "What's gotten into you?"  Kathy said, "I keep telling them that, 'Yes, I would like another M-Store.'  But,...I don't.  I'm afraid! Sally, I'm so afraid...afraid of what they'll do or say if I refuse them! And, I even know don't know why! There's something strange about this place and I've got to get out of here! I wanna go home!" Sally reassured her that things would be fine. She encouraged Kathy to get more rest. Then I crept away. 

That one seems to have some problems! I wondered if Carmen and I should hook her and Davel up. That would make an interesting couple. Good ol' Captain Peg Leg, sailing the seven seas and Sybil. (You know, Sybil, the schizo girl in that 1970's movie starring Sally Field?) I went and told Carmen. I knew this would get her laughing again! She went on and on about how Kathy could be the wench Davel seemed to be looking for, and she could also be the cook, first mate, cabin boy and a lookout. "Who knows, she might have enough personalities for each!" Carmen said. 

Carmen seemed to be in a better mood. I though it was because I cheered her up with the Davel-Kathy story. Turns out that Sheila came by while I was gone and told her that she would get her first upload tomorrow. Thank God!  I was happy and relieved at the same time. I can't stand seeing Carmen depressed! She was asking all kinda of questions about what they did to me. I told her everything that happened, again. This time she was obviously interested. She asked if I would go stand in the room with her. I told her I would, if they let me. 

It's funny how the one person you think is so tough and so together, is so dependent. I don't mind, honestly! It's nice to know that she wants me around, not just tolerates my presence! We'll have to see what Dr. Hamilton says tomorrow! I wonder what she will experience. Will she get the jets too?
