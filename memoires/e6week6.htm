The doctors were very disappointed when I could only tell them some small details about Matthew and Nancy the next morning. All I remembered were the things I’d already told them right after the upload. There was no connection to the information left; I had no longer the feeling that I knew this Matthew and Nancy. They became characters from a book or a movie. That was a relief for me, ‘coss I find the idea of knowing people whom I don’t know at all quite confusing. What if I would ever meet them on the street? Dr. Hamilton had to deal with the biggest disappointment of all while Dr. Tachikawa had already suspected this.
