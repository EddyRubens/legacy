I’m getting along much better with Dr. Tachikawa. He makes me feel valued and I think not just because of the implant. As far as age is concerned he could have been my father but he doesn’t treat me as his daughter. More as a lady. He makes it clear that he likes me, and the feeling is mutual. And I’m not the only one; there are more women here who’ve got a bit of a crush on him. Every now and then curiosity turns to gossip. So I don’t know if it’s true, but he’s been married three times apparently. Each marriage stranded because of his ambitions. His first wife is said to have committed suicide because he was more interested in how her brains worked than her personality. It appears she didn’t speak for a year before she took an overdose of sleeping pills with a bottle of saké. His second wife took off with someone else, someone who did want to spend time with her. And his third wife is supposed to live in Tokyo, where Dr. Tachikawa has an apartment. He doesn’t have children and wouldn’t even have them if they paid him.  Which is a huge problem for his third wife because she would like to have a big family. I wonder where those gossipers got all this information. Dr. Tachikawa may be very friendly and polite, but he keeps to himself. He hardly ever lets people know how he feels and what he thinks and is much too reserved to talk so openly about his personal affairs. Others are more than willing to do that for him I guess.
