After reading Sonja's email, I felt bad that I was just sitting around. I needed to find Carmen. I left my room and headed to Adrian's. It was time to put the charm on and see what information he could give me. When I reached his room I could hear him talking to someone. It was Rosanne. They were talking about the letter that Sonja obviously sent to all the members of Braintec. I was wondering what poor Dr. Hamilton must have been thinking, knowing that he was screwed over by Dr. Tachikawa. I couldn't really understand what they were saying, but I did hear Rosanne ask Adrian who he would vote for, Dr. Hamilton or Dr. Tachikawa, if he had to choose between the two. Then to my suprise with no pause for thought I heard him say, "Kodjiro, He pays more for the job and gives me more freedom."

I was disappointed in him. Dr. Hamilton has given him a lot, too. Who exactly did he think allows him to work in that crummy little office. If he only realized what a bastard Dr. Tachikawa was maybe he would think twice. (Or at least take a moment to think!) I waited for a quiet moment to walk in. I didn't want to interrupt any conversations that might be useful to me. Unfortunately after that comment and an unfinished sentence of Rosanne's the room became quiet. I walked to the door, which was open and said "Knock, Knock!" while smiling. Rosanne turned and looked at me. She was clearly not happy to see me. She walked over to me and said, "Shouldn't you be laying down?" I politely responded to her saying, "Why would I be laying down, Rosanne? It's still early." I could smell the liquor in her voice when she responded, "Because I heard you and your little friend in the 'Left Wing' decided to do some landscaping." She started to laugh and Adrian chuckled at her landscaping joke. So, Carmen was in the 'Left Wing.' Rosanne just unknowingly gave me all the information I was interested in. I looked at her and Adrian and said, "You know... Your right. I think I do need to go lay down. I'm feeling a little light headed."

As I turned away from the door, I heard Rosanne say something which I'm sure wasn't polite, and her and Adrian both started laughing. I was laughing too, because I got what I wanted in less than five minutes.
