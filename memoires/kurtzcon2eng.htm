5. November

This is Portland! It is? I'm not sure as well. I am very confused. O yes, very confused am I since I had taken the plane to the US. I never been there before. I don't know, why I do this? Do this? What's do it? What's going on here ...? So confused. Jet-lag maybe. I hope so.

I'm sitting here in a breakfast room near by the research center, face to face with a glass of orange juice as thick as my finger and ask for myself: This is the life?

Yes it is. Some kind of. Sometimes. Somewhere.

And why not? Any life is worth to live it, isn't it?

I don't know. Better to go to bed now, I'm so confused about this journey.
