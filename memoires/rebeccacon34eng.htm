As I was leaving the facility to meet Carmen, I saw Adelle and some man visiting in the Communion-Room. She was petting and playing with a small gray dog. The poor thing looked as though it needed to be let outside. It was going crazy! So was Adelle. She was treating it like it was a child, saying things like 'My little Dinky-Winkie missed his Mama!'  'Where the Dinky-Poo?' And my personal favorite, 'Does Dinky-Dinky need to do the Dooty-Dooty for Mama? Yes, he does! Yes, he does!' I love Bruce to no end, but I hope someone would come over and kick me if I ever start talking like that! After seeing Adelle and that loud and annoying dog it makes me realize how much I miss Bruce. It has been 5 weeks since I have seen him. I wonder if he thinks I gave him away. But, that reminds me...Why does Adelle get to have her dog come visit? I was told absolutely no animals were allowed here.

She is the only one who can break the spell the old witch cast upon me! Have you see her?" The only thing I could think of was..'Gee, wonder what Disney movie this guy just watched.' Then I replied; "Yes Davel, I have seen the beauty! The old witch cast a spell on her as well, turning her into a gray hairy monster. She is being held captive. You must go rescue her. She is in the Communion-Room. Go!"
