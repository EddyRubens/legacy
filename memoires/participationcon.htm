Maybe you like to read about the experiences of test subjects and maybe you like to imagine how it is to recall their memories. In that case you might find it even more fun to become a test subject. That is possible. Almost everything is possible at Braintec. The borders lie behind the imagination. 

Apply and you'll receive all the necessary information for test subjects by e-mail. The products of your pen and the photographs or videos you made during your stay at the Braintec Research Center will be placed on this site as well. And if you want to, you can develop your own html-pages.
