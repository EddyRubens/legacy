"I swear!" Carmen said first. "Cross my heart!" I said while making a crossing motion on my chest.  Kaleb paused for a minute. He was obviously hesitating as to whether or not he was going to tell us something. Then finally he let it out, "I'm here undercover. I work for the CIA. "He went over to his desk and pulled out a black wallet from under some papers. He handed it to me. "Go ahead, open it," he said. When I opened it, there was a clear plastic casing that held an ID card. It was a CIA ID card.

Carmen was looking over my shoulder and then said, "So, maybe you can tell us what's going on! We need to get out of here! Can you help us?" He looked at her and said, "First let me explain why I'm here." He motioned for us to have a seat so Carmen and I sat on the bed. He sat at the chair by the desk then continued. "Before I became an agent, I had a twin brother. We went through all the basic training and took all the assigned courses. Then about two weeks before our final exam Kasey, my brother, came across a flier about this place." He explained how Kasey was immediately interested and decided to join. From what he was saying, Kasey looked into Braintec and came in contact with Dr. Hamilton. He was finally able to persuade Kaleb to join when he mentioned how they would be the first twins to do memory extraction's. The doctors (most likely Dr. Tachikawa) offered Kaleb and Kasey and ridiculous amount of money to come. They also made a huge deal about how they were finally going to get to do 'Twin Extraction's." In a 'Twin Extraction' the doctors took an extraction of the same memory from each of them. Then they reversed the M-stores and Kaleb and Kasey got each others upload. He continued to say that one night, a few days after the 'Twin Extraction,' Kasey had a scheduled upload. Apparently he never returned to their room that night. They found his lifeless body the next day, in a chair in one of the uploading rooms. No one was sure what happened or why he died.

He said the facility kept it all under wraps because they didn't want to scare any of the other test subjects away. Then, they made up some story for the 'Newbie's' (That was his term for new test subjects) about how his brother was murdered before Kaleb even came to Braintec. He mentioned how they were nice enough to grant him a few weeks of bereavement and let him leave the state to plan and attend the services. "When I was back home, in North Carolina, I was approached by the agency. They said that because I had the basic training and experience in a facility they wanted to know more about, they were interested in hiring me. Now I am here undercover trying to find out what happened to my brother and trying to nail Tachikawa."

I told him how in the first email I received, there was mention that the CIA was not even sure that Dr. Tachikawa or any of the other doctor or scientists here, were the man they were looking for. Kaleb said that when Dr. Tachikawa worked for the CIA, he was using his given birth name, Shinicho Matsushita. It was on his birth certificate and other legal documents in his CIA employment files. Once he was terminated, he quickly and legally changed his name to Kodjiro Tachikawa. It threw off the CIA for a few months. They were looking for him under his birth name. "Once we figured out his new name, we were sure that he was, and is, the guy we're after." "So, do you think that he might have something to do with your brother's death?" I asked. "If I didn't think the bastard was responsible, I wouldn't have come back!" he said.

He went on to tell us that if we wanted to help, the only thing we could do was pretend like nothing was going on. "Don't miss any uploads or they will know something's up," he said. "They know how addictive these uploads are, and if you refuse to have them they'll either send you to Sheila and Lucas to be hypnotized, or they'll lock you up in the East Wing until your ready to talk." Don't be alone with any of these doctors under any circumstance. Make up any excuse. I'll look into getting your friend out of there before her extraction."

We let him know what time our uploads were and when Megan's extraction was. He told us to do this one last time, then go have dinner as scheduled with the agents.
