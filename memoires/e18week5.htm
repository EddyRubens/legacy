There was an article on Dr. Tachikawa and his invention in today’s newspaper. Actually a decent article attached to a beautiful photo. “Plug and Replay your memories – Total Recall finally reality.” Good article. Written in a populistic way but still worthwhile reading. Dr. Tachikawa  should be a satisfied man.  

It is thrilling getting involved in something like this here. It is happening right here and right now. The inventions which are tested here will be world-wide news soon. Maybe in future every family has their own recall-kit at home. Doesn’t Johnny-boy understand what mamma is saying? Wait, then we will share my experience! Never talk about “when you grow up you will understand what is good for you”. New educational methods, new techniques for meetings, new politics but most of all, new forms of entertainment. “Have you tried the latest rodeo-experiment of Cowboy Jacky already? If you watch that ring on his right hand closely enough, you get even further!” No need traveling to New Zealand to enjoy the best bungee-jump.

I wonder what it shall mean to me whenever this castle in the air will win from the future. Will I be known as “first lady with a MESI” better than being registered in the Guinness Book of Records? Will my memories be for sale in shops in the near future? By that time movie-actors will not be famous anymore for their outlook and acting talents but known for their rich world of imagination. Who knows I shall win an Oscar for the most spectacular, however, honorable memory! My UFO-experience would be well recommended. I have not talked about it to anyone because of this panic. What a shame. I’m really curious to know how others have gone through that experience. Time will tell when this hectic period is over and done with. Unless that memory is already out of date ‘coss it is situated on an M-Store A type....

In the afternoon yesterday’s presentation was heard on the radio. Together with a bunch of youngsters we were gathered in the lounge. They invited a specialist who thought the whole idea as ethical irresponsible. A few test subjects expressed themselves by calling “Boo-boo!” Tonight’s guest in the TV talk-show is Dr. Tachikawa. Never a dull moment.
