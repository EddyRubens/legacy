And indeed, the lady from California arrived as the first of today’s new test subjects. Her private driver dropped her at eleven o’clock precisely. She brought halve of her household and she gave orders to everyone. I was surprised to find myself standing on a stool with Dr. Hamilton, fixing a curtain with a disgusting flowery design in front of her window. Her room was transformed into a chic hotel suite within a few moments. It isn’t difficult to give her the special treatment Dr. Hamilton had asked for. She commands it.

The other newcomers arrived one by one during the day. A young girl from Tacoma; Kathy. She makes a funny contrasts sharply with Matthew; a man that could well be her grandfather. Last but not least we have two students; Mark and Jeromy.

It is going to get overcrowded if we welcome five new test subjects every week. I am afraid that won’t improve the atmosphere. Especially not if more of them are like that lady from California. Adelle is her name. With a double L. I have never seen someone overreact this much when his or her name was misspelled. Jessica and I exchanged some knowing glances.

Adelle is definitely getting on my nerves. She figured that our towels wouldn’t be soft enough for her, so I could have them back. That didn’t surprise me. But I was quite shocked when it became clear she worries about her own towels as well. She wants to be sure that our laundry service is sufficient enough and even got me to check that for her! This lady is driving me crazy!
