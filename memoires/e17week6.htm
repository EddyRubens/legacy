Today’s upload threw into a commotion. It was my remembrance which had been extracted from my memory earlier this week. I was given the free choice to choose a memory I wanted. They asked me to choose a moment any test subject would love to experience again. With that choice they had a lot of faith in me. Maybe I had broken that confidence.

Who doesn’t want to believe that he saw a UFO for a moment? I thought it would be a great experience. Anyway a memory I would definitely like to experience again. It was somewhere in the country where apple trees disappeared into agriculture. That evening I was jumping at an open air concert, a festival for experimental music held in the neighborhood. It was already midnight or actually early in the morning and I didn’t want to go home yet. I wanted to go for a walk and my boy-friend was walking behind me. After we were taken by surprise through the beautiful round apples in the orchard, I saw this huge white mobile. The traditional forms of it exactly described that we were dealing with a UFO. Silly enough I was not astonished at all. But I was very much excited. Adventure! The terrestrial spaceship attracted me in a strong way. “A UFO!” I shouted at my boy-friend and rushed to it. “No, wait!” he answered frightened. “It might be dangerous!” The white metal glittered at me in the early morning sun. How I longed to be introduced to the drivers! When the ship was only at a distance of a few meters, I saw that the glittering metal was not metal but white agricultural plastic. And the round portholes were car tires holding the plastic in its place. I started to laugh out loud. I explained the misunderstanding to my boy-friend, and slowly he dared to come closer. It was such a funny situation! So extraordinary and so intensive!

It was Kathy who got this upload first and she did not feel well afterwards. Then later Adrian got the upload who was not afraid anymore for strange side effects after his experience with Steward’s memory. He really liked my memory and he asked me if he could have it again within a short time. We all understood that seeing a UFO does not need to be in favor of everybody. Only when I went through my own UFO-experience again I understood that something had gone wrong. That night I forgot I had taken a trip! After that experience I again remembered much more what I had gone through that night. How I wanted to eat one of the apples in the orchard and how the small stalk suddenly changed into a worm when I had a first bite. How my legs were moving mechanically when I was walking. How strange the morning dew felt on my eye-lashes and how I got afraid to be intoxicated when I saw a sprinkler. It was a strange night which I will always treasure as a good memory caused by that so-called UFO. The hallucinatory aspect I completely forgot because during that time I was so often under the influence of triptamines that it felt natural. I never mentioned it when I registered myself as a test subject. Do I have to mention it this time?
