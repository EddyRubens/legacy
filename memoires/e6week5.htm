It’s strange, this memory of a hike that I’ve never taken part in. And I felt real affection for two people that I have never met my entire life. That’s so bizarre. While reading a book or watching a movie you can sometimes get the feeling that you know the principal characters. But that feeling is by no means as intense as this. Suddenly, all at once I knew these people. From one moment to the next I knew quite a lot about them. That’s bizarre. It fascinates me no end. I’m terribly excited about all this and I’m really looking forward to the next upload.

At the same time I’m a bit worried about my privacy. I have a MESI-implant and I’m sure it won’t be long before my memories will be extracted. Other test subjects will have those uploaded and who knows how much they will get to know about me and my life. Maybe more than I would like them to know. Luckily there isn’t much that I’m ashamed of and that I absolutely wouldn’t want others to know about. But still, some things should not be shared with just anyone. In what way could I protect myself against this sort of invasion of privacy? How much will I be able to keep to myself?
