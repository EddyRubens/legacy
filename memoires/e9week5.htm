Very unreal. That is how I feel today. Even though I experience the Research Center and especially my personal room as my home, I walk around disorientated. Like a chicken that can’t find a spot to lay her egg. The recall of other persons’ memories has a bigger impact than I expected. These uploads might be out of my short term memory, it seems as if they truly left something behind. Sometimes I feel so confused, not at all myself. I spoke about this with Jessica. She didn’t find it alarming but still wanted to be kept informed. Every human being is confused every now and then and everybody has doubts about the meaning of life. The confusion doesn’t have to be caused by the uploads. She is probably right. I might be worried for nothing, again.

This evening we had some kind of a special event. It is Christmas Eve, so we all sat down at the fake Christmas tree, ate till we felt sick and sang some songs. It was awful. I never liked Christmas. Most of the people have one or two days off but I explicitly requested to work during these holy days. There is nothing sadder than being alone at Christmas. So there we were; a couple of lonesome people or workaholics. I spoke to Adrian, who lives too far away to go home for just two days. He misses his fiancée very much. I think her name is Elgin. She has to be a wonderful woman and Adrian loves her very much, the way he talks about her. And I had a good chat with Jessica, she was actually very kind to me. No hard feelings. Or is that an essential part of the whole Christmas idea?
