Where should we go this time? Every place can be dangerous and insecure. A lot of people seem to be interested in the information I’m carrying. We give the driver the card one of our guests, Yukio, gave us. We trust him the most. At that address he seems to be working at a big multimedia project. It is huge and quit interesting what Yukio’s building there. With the help of the available equipment we make a back-up from all the information stored on my laptop. This way Heather can have it on a disk in case anything happens to me. It should be in code, because a lot of strange figures appear on the screens. Heather and I don’t want to go back to the guesthouse but prefer to take our planes without catching the eye. We ask Yukio to pick up our belongings. He needs to go to the guesthouse for some tools anyway. If he sees that other people have been in our room, he only has to take what we stored in the storage room. We enjoy his flashy installation when he’s gone. My laptop images fit in so well. This way I become part of an artwork!  

After one hour Yukio is back. He only took our belongings from the storage room. It’s obvious that there has been some activity inside our room. Yukio describes the people he saw sitting in the room next to ours and one white skinned man in a house across the guesthouse. Yukio couldn’t see them clearly but it has to be the Brainteccers and Paul. An extra reason to be careful. 

It doesn’t matter that we miss our luggage from our room. We have enough clothes by now and the artworks we bought and other things that we care for were all in the storage room. But how to go on? Yukio sends us to the owner of an art institute, Mitsuki. She has often sent all kinds of things for exhibitions. We pack it like art and Mitsuki should ship it to our both home addresses today. Now we only have to sneak out of here. At our arrival in Japan I saw a separated entrance for business class. If we exchange our tickets we can use that one.

Then I think that the payment could be a problem. Paul could possibly follow us if we pay with the credit card and paying in cash is somehow suspicious. Mitsuki has a solution for this as well. She shall pay the extra costs electronically and we shall pay the costs to her with the credit card right before we leave. We can even sleep at her place. She lives above the institute. We have a nice evening. Great food and interesting conversations mostly about Japanese literature.
