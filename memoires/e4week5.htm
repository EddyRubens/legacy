Tomorrow I’m getting my first memory upload. I’ve just been given a detailed explanation of how that’s going to go. I will sit in a big chair in the middle of the room. A bit like at the dentist’s but only upright. I will have those black ribbons with electrodes on my head again, only now they have to be in exactly the right place. My brain activity must be registered perfectly during the upload. The bit of skin above my left ear will be shaven again, that spot will remain bold for the time being. That’s where they will place the server, a round disc where the M-stores are placed in. The hypnotist will go through some relaxation exercises with me and then the procedure starts. I’m terribly curious but also a little nervous. They can’t tell me what kind of memory it’s going to be because that may influence the experience. At any rate it’s going to be something simple. “We must start with easy things,” Dr. Tachikawa said. “All in good time.”
