As I sat in my room, my stomach ached from all that was happening. I felt as though I was looking through a window at my life, but with an 'Alice in Wonderland' effect. There were a bundle of unusual and shady characters, surreal surroundings, and nothing really seemed to make any sense. I was just the stupid girl who was too curious.

I packed my suitcase with only my most important belongings (including the Band-Aid box) and shoved the suitcase under my bed. I wanted to be ready to go as soon as Agent Davis and Mr. Isakova's friend's arrived. I planned on emailing them later to find out their exact location, and ask if there is anything I can do to help. By the time I got everything packed, I realized how restless I was. I was fuming with anger and was contemplating a confrontation with Dr. Hamilton.
I looked at the clock and noticed it was 12:45am. I knew Dr. Hamilton would most likely be awake, so I headed to his office.

Along the way I saw Rosanne in the Communion-Room. She was talking to one of the new test subjects. She was a petite Asian girl, named, Mei, who had porcelain looking skin. I had seen her around a few times. From what I heard, she was Sally's new roommate. When Rosanne was done talking to her, she turned towards me. She slowly walked up to me, obviously knowing what she was going to say, and gave me a snide look. "You know," she said, "If I'm not mistaken, pretty soon you're going to be left here...all alone." I asked her what she meant by her remark, and she said, "That just means you won't have your little Latin fireball to protect you. I know people...and I can have all sorts of things done to you. Remember your little puppet extraction? That was a lot of fun,...wasn't it!" She laughed and walked away after that comment.

Was she threatening me? What was her problem with me anyway? Just to make things clear, I love Carmen. She is my best friend. But Lord knows I don't need her for protection. Rosanne obviously didn't know who she was dealing with. She obviously didn't know I knew people too! I followed Rosanne in hopes to catch her with her hand in the cookie jar, so to speak. She ended up going to the little room Adrian called his office. I stood outside the door listening to her plead with him. She was asking Adrian to give her an upload.
An upload? That must have been what Adrian took the other day under his jacket...M-stores. I waited for a few more minutes then heard him putting things into place.
I quickly headed for Dr. Hamilton's office. If Rosanne wanted to play rough, she just got the bull!

I knocked on Dr. Hamilton's office door. He was sitting at his desk reading something. "Rebecca, what are you doing up so late?" he said as he opened the door.
I barged in without an offer and sat down on his leather couch. I cupped my hands over my face and turned on the fake tears. I was hoping that Dr. Hamilton still had some feelings for me and decided to play on them.
"Oh, Dr. Hamilton I feel silly for even coming here but I don't think Sheila likes me much, and I have no one else to talk to." He sat down beside me and put his hand on my shoulder. He grabbed my hand and assured me that if something was bothering me, he wanted to know so that he could take care of it. I was feeling a little guilty for playing with his feelings, but then reminded myself how he hasn't done much to help Carmen, Megan, Jenna or Simone.
