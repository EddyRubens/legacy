I’m afraid I became a bit daft. These silent days take too long! I wish Jóannis was here; he would understand loneliness strangles with silence.
Once, Jóannis gave me a little bear. This gift became more then just a talisman. It became a good friend and now we can talk about almost everything.
My ice bear told me about the danger of loneliness. Loneliness can make crazy. And the craziness can make you jump in the water. Your muscles will cramp because of the cold and you can easily drown. I hope I will strand somewhere before that happens.
