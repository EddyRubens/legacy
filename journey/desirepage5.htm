Let’s hope this was the best decision I could make. Burning all your ships behind you isn’t easy. It’s small a consolation that most of my memories were annoying me anyway. It’s better to leave them behind so I can start with a clean slate. But why do I have those phantom-limb pains as if a huge part of me is torn apart? Perhaps that’s the emptiness of missing Jóannis. Although it’s a tough situation I have to persist, I can already enjoy the freedom it gives me. I am totally free to go wherever I want, but there is only one way I wish to go: to the North. My desires for the North are so huge that they feel like instincts, as if an inner compass needle constantly points that direction.
