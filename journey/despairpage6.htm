I cherish the day Jóannis gave me the ice bear. We were sitting at the coastline to rest a bit, after walking for at least two hours. I was pulling out grass straws around me. I think I even tried to whistle between two blades, when he searched his pockets. “I’ve got something for you,” he mumbled, still searching. “Something very important.” Then he gave me the talisman. “It’s just a little bear, but it carries my deepest affection.” After he said that, we had the best fuck of all.
