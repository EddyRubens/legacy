It’s good to be alone again. Soon after the flight across the Inlandis I fully recovered. I’m in control of my thoughts again.

The sun is shining and it’s not too chilly; the perfect conditions for a long walk. Let’s hope this nice weather isn’t going to change, because I’ll be walking for at least two or three weeks.

It gives a good feeling to have everything under control. I’ve got plenty of food, warm clothes and my satellite connection to keep contact with the rest of the world. These modern technologies give me the possibility to choose whether I want to be lonely or not.
