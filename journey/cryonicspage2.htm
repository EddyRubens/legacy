I can’t get the person who lost his knife out of my mind. Where did he die? How long will it take me to find the body?

Actually, I think it’s quite romantic. I could just lie down like Little Snow White in a coffin of crystal ice. I will be dead for ages until my true prince or princess awakes me. And in the meanwhile I can be with Jóannis in the afterlife. What more can I wish for?
