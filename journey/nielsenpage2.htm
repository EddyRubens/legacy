It happened on the ferry to Ilulissat: I fell in love again. My heart is pumping as fast as it can when I see my object of desire.

Stig Nielsen is the name of this Danish scientist. He was the one who started first contact. He invited me to stay at his house within the first five sentences! But it wasn’t love at the first sight. On the contrary, I was quite suspicious about his offer. What does this man want from me? After we talked for hours about almost everything I couldn’t imagine being without him. He knows so much about the history and the wildlife of Greenland, the people and their destiny. It is nice to spend time with a person who understands my fascinations. It is even nicer to have someone around me who cares. If it depends upon me, this boat trip will last forever!
