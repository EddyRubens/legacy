The people here are so friendly! They became even more hospitable since I’m no longer the sight of the town anymore.

I have made good friends with an Inuit family: Aqqaluk, Klara and their daughter Naoyak, who is perhaps the sweetest Inuit girl I’ve ever seen. This kind family invited me to stay in their house. I am very grateful they did, because my bivouac sack isn’t that comfortable after all. And it’s getting considerably colder.

Aqqaluk really snores like a walrus! I guess all the Inuit are snoring, but Aqqaluk caps everything. When I finally fall asleep, Naoyak wakes me up again. Every morning she wants me to comb her hair and I have to make her some tea.
