Olafsfjörður 
 

We received a few messages from Iceland. Among which one from J.Olafsson, de director of  "The Department of Missing Persons" of Iceland. This is the letter we got from him:

"Rosanne van Klaveren was spotted recently walking from Dalvík to Olafsfjörður in Iceland. I have checked with the airlines and was told she had left the country in a hurry. The desk clerk remembered her for the hounted look in het eyes.

rgsd

J.Olafsson, Director of The Department of Missing Persons"

The information that we got from an old fisherman of Olafsfjörður, confirms her visit to this village. The fisherman couldn’t believe Rosanne had left the country in an aeroplane. On February 20th she supposedly bought a boat from him and sailed out of the fjord. It was an old wooden fisherman’s boat that only just was seaworthy.  The man did not want to reveal how much Rosanne gave him for it.
