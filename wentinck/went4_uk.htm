Rosanne lives!

 

We are very pleased to let everyone know that Rosanne van Klaveren, missing since march 27th 1998, was seen alive in the city of Rotterdam. Although bodily remains were never found, it was feared that Rosanne died an unnatural death.

The missing person was signaled at "Diergaarde Blijdorp" at the van Aerssenlaan in Rotterdam, thursday september 17th, as was made clear by the letter we received earlier this week.
This comforting information was send to us by Mrs. De Vries, who visited the zoo that day. Near the entrance her family was photographed by the zoo's professional photographer, a service provided to every interested visitor. Before leaving the zoo, Mrs. De Vries bought the picture. A female passer-by can be seen in the background. This person has been identified as Rosanne van Klaveren beyond any doubt.

 

Link to the received letter

The photograph makes clear that Rosanne is alive but leaves us with a lot of questions. Did Rosanne talk to anyone around 15.30 p.m. - the time the photo was taken? And if so, to whom, and what about? We can see that Rosanne klings tightly to a fake leather bag and looks rather distressed, or so it seems. What could be the contents of the bag? We can only guess at her motives for visiting this zoo.

If you have any information, be it about her presence at "Diergaarde Blijdorp"on thursday september 17th, be it about the possible contents of the bag, please let us know. Other hints, suggestions and motives that possibly led to her disapperance are more than welcome. We want to thank you for your attention.
