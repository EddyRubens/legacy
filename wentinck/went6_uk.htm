Interview Jeaninne T.
 

Hoping to gather more information about Rosanne van Klaveren, we contacted Jeaninne T. a second time. Jeaninne is a friend of Rosanne, and lives in Arnhem, Holland. The two women visited each other every week, in a period that seems long a go to Jeannine.

On the 10th of April Rosanne added a message to the answering machine of Jeaninne. This was also the last time that Jeannine -and many others- heard of Rosanne.

Link to answering machine

Following is an excerpt of the interview with Jeaninne, this was all the information Jeaninne could provide.

  • Did you hear from Rosanne since?
  • No, I'm afraid not.
  • Did you see the picture from the Zoo?
  • Yes! I saw it on your website on the internet! Rosanne held on to an ominous looking bag. Is that the picture you're refering at?
  • Indeed, that's the picture. Did you see the bag before?
  • Never. The bag suprises me, because it isn't her style. I don't like the ugly bag either.
  • Do you have any idea why Rosanne might visit a zoo?
  • No, absolutely not. That's why the message suprised me. Ofcourse, I am glad to see that's she's still alive! The only thing I can imagine, or that I can think of, are elephants. It struck me at the time, that every time that it was her turn to zap, she always kept looking at nature documentaries about elephants. I never understood why, maybe she likes elephants a lot.
  • Her turn to zap?
  • Yes, once a week we watched television, mostly on fridays. Every other week she came to me, the other week I went to her place. We took turns to hold the remote. But I don't know why she liked watching elephants, I think they're clumsy animals.
  • Were those the only moments you two saw each other?
  • Yes, we didn't have time to see each other more often.

Well then, elephants. I don't think we're getting anywhere with this information. I presume Rosanne didn't visit the zoo with a bag full of elephant food.
