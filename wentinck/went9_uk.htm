An employee of the PB-Internet Café in London has contacted us. Here his story:

Tuesday, November 17th, at noon there was a stange woman in our café. We agreed this was noteworthy. She probably is the woman you're after.

She attracted the attention of the whole café by making weird noises. The almost inhuman gurgling throat sounds resembling the moaning of a cat in heat, made a few customers leave early. Because I got curious, I monitored her surf-operations from our main computer. At first she only visited chatrooms and messageboards, where she left the text 'PLEASE, HELP ME FIND MYSELF!'.
After that I got a phonecall, so I could no longer spy on her. But I had to interrupt the phonecall because the woman laughed loud and indecently. She yelled so hard that for a moment I thought the windows would crack. While everyone was holding their fingers in their ears, the woman left our café screaming.  On the PC she was working on I found this avi-file.
