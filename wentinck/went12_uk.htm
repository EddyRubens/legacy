In love with Jóannis? 
 

We are back from our visit to the Faroe islands last week. The research we've done over there, makes us think that Rosanne van Klaveren is no longer hiding on one of this isles since September. Before that time she has been signalled several times. We spoke with Ms. E. Jákupsen, owner of the small fisherman's store in Leirvík where Rosanne bought groceries several times:

"Rosanne is a frightened woman that is easily scared. Sometimes she seemed to see or hear things. She then looked up to see if no one was looking. I got nervous in her presence."

 
J. Jorgensen, the local pastor, knew kende Rosanne:

"We have a small pier where I love to fish. The view over the fjord is really beautiful! Rosanne used to sit there, so we had the chance to talk to each other a few times. She was always happy and in a good mood. She talked about Jóannis Kristiansen al the time, what great man he was and so on. She always talked about him in the present tense. Jóannis is being seen by most islanders as the best Faroe artist ever, but the praise for him by Rosanne beat everything. When I pointed out to her that the artist had been dead since a long time, she reacted very indignated. She denied it and assured me that Jóannis took good care of her. I felt sorry for her, reality and fiction were probalbly difficult to tell apart."

Will Rosanne fall in love with a dead man? Have her fantasies got that much out of hand, that she wrote us the letter in the name of Jóannis? For the time being, we stick to that.
