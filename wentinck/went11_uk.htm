Bad joke 

 

We are currently on the Faeroe Islands. It was our intention to talk to Jóannis Kristiansen. As can be read in the previous update, this man informed us that Rosanne van Klaveren stayed with him last summer. We have been in his atelier in Leirvík, but we did not see himself in person.

It wasn't difficult to find the atelier of Jóannis Kristiansen. Adressing the first passer-by sufficed to walk straight to it. It was a very nice place, small but cosey through simplicity. The pictures, wich were attached to the correspondence, do not lie. The place is completely filled with oil paintings that spread their typical smell. It is quite clear the man was a painter.

Indeed was, because Jóannis Kristiansen has been dead for al least ten years! On March 3rd, 1988 Jóannis Kristiansen died in Copenhagen, probable of old age. Dissilusioned by this discovery we presume that sending us the letter in the name of the deceased artist was a bad joke. Because of the fact that the woman on the pictures seems to be Rosanne, we will stay here for a few more days to continue our investigations. We will keep you informed.
