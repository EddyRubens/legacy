Avatar Grand Hotel 
The receptionist of the Avatar Grand Hotel sent us the mail you can read here below. Rosanne supposedly mailed him several times and one of her letters can be found in the post room inside the Avatar Grand Hotel. We read it, but found it a bit silly. Rosanne seems to philosophise about magnetism and other rubbish.
But we will keep an eye on the Avatar Grand Hotel, you never know if she will walk in again. The mail:
Avatar Grand Hotel,                                                              January, 3rd 1999

Dear family members and/or searchers for Rosanne van Klaveren:

Because of the notice on your site, I'd like to tell you this: on the first of January this year, about four o'clock pm, the frontdoor of the Avatar Grand Hotel opened and someone came in. It was a tousled looking woman dressed in a long coat. (This is what I heared later from some witnesses - I haven't seen her myself that day.) First she went inside the dancepalace end ordered something to drink. Our barkeeper could not remember what she ordered was, although she was the only customer at that moment. But he knows for sure it was an alcoholic beverage. He was that day, because the day before was New Years Eve, even more silent then usually. She didn't say anything either. She emptied her glass in a quiet dark corner, after that she left the dancepalace. Then the towelgirl saw her going into the lobby, where she took the key from room 1 and opened the door of our post room with it. (Things like that are quite normal in our hotel, so she didn't pay much attention.) At that moment I was inside my own apartment, so I haven't noticed her appearances. It seems that she has asked for me inside the post room, but when she heared that I wasn't attainable at that moment, she deposited a letter and left the message that she would come back later to meet me. The letter she left made me suspect that this woman must have been Rosanne van Klaveren herself. You must know, I wrote her several times and in the last letter she said she wanted to meet me. I placed her letter in our post room collection, so you can read it if you want. LETTER NUMBER 9. Today it is January the 3rd 1999. I suppose Rosanne wil return to the hotel to meet me, or to bring other letters, but I'm not sure. I will wait. I hope you can use this information. The letter from Rosanne can be read inside our post room,(key no.1).

kind regards from the receptionist of the Avatar Grand Hotel.
