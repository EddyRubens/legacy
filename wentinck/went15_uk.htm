Art robbery 

 

Rosanne has committed a crime!  On Friday January 29, 1999 she robbed a
painting by Jóannis Kristiansen from the National Gallery of Iceland, Listsafn. The stolen painting is a selfportrait made in 1968, only recently acquired by the gallery.  

 

show avi-file 291 kilobytes
The robbery has been filmed by a security camera.  The tapes were only viewed long after Rosanne had left the gallery.They clearly show that Rosanne hides the painting under her coat. She probably left Listsafn like this. The National Gallery has given us the fragment of the tape where Rosanne steals the painting, thus hoping to find back the painting sooner.
