Stay at Faroe 

 

We received this message early this week:

Dear people of the detective agency Wentick and others concerned,

There is no reason to be worried. To my knowledge, Rosanne van Klaveren is doing well. She stayed in my atelier last summer, where she enjoyed herself a lot. I let her do whatever she wanted; it turned out that our intentions and desires matched well.

I am happy for her, because she could live a 'normal live' during her stay. The village Leirvík, a little harbour to the east of the isle Eysturoy, offered her all the rest she wanted. Rosanne gave me a new live through all the excitement and sensation she brought along with her. I am very delighted with that.

Jóannis Kristiansen.

We are very happy with these comforting words. We hope to fly to the Faroe Islands in two weeks time, to talk to mister Kristiansen. Maybe he can tell us where Rosanne is at the moment.
