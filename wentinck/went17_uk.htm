Westergasfabriek

 

Rosanne van Klaveren has been signaled the terrain of the Westergasfabriek in Amsterdam. It seems to be a park that she frequents. A Frenchman told us this already three month ago, but then we couldn’t take it seriously. He claimed that Rosanne played poker in the park with a rabbit named "Anton".

 

Because of the fact that the Frenchman -who lives round the corner of the terrain of the Westergasfabriek- couldn’t bare the idea that we didn’t believe him, he has secretly hid himself behind some bushes to get photographic evidence. He heard Rosanne and the rabbit talk about the plans that the city of Amsterdam has for the terrain. This wilderness, an ideal hideout for rabbits and other suspicious characters, has to make room for “Park of the Future”. During their poker game they made sinister plans to keep their hiding place.
