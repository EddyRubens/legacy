Arctic View provides alternative accommodation for travelers passing through or visiting Kugluktuk, Nunavut. We offer flexible rates by day, week or month.

Located next to the Arctic Ocean, there is a great view year round. We provide private or shared accommodation; shared bathroom, kitchen and living room; meals can be provided on request (traditional options); overnight laundry service; internet; free long distance to Canada and US (possibly overseas); winter access to snow machine; and guides can be arranged for land trips.

Arctic View is currently under renovations, though we may be able to accommodate you. Contact us for more information.
