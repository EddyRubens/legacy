About the humanupload

What is the Humanupload Project?
The Humanupload Project is a project of the Braintec Institute, a progressive technology company for research on memories. A humanupload is the transfer of the human thought and memory from the scanned brain tissue to an automated facsimile.

Isn't it dangerous to create a humanupload? Won't the original mind be destroyed?
Some posthumanistic philosophers believe a humanupload will destroy the original mind. This is a huge misconception. The definition of a humanupload Braintec is working on is just a huge range of MEMES (memories and thoughts) with whatever details of the test subject’s life available but without the human mentality or a personal genetic makeup. Identity remains unique and inviolate. Therefore it is not dangerous.

What is the side effect of a humanupload for the test subject?
We can state that in other research, a test subject might lose a thousand neurons from drug use and obviously remain the same person though the pattern of neurons has changed.  Actually, patterns that can be derived from our organic states shift constantly without effecting identity. We all change over the course of time, yet remain ourselves. The creation of a humanupload won't be different from that. The effect may not even be noticeable.
Beside that, a backup of your memories and thoughts will be very valuable in cases of trauma, mental illness, brain diseases, memory loss or other misfortune.

How can Braintec create a humanupload?
We have developed a prototype of an innovative bi-directional electroencephalogram (brainwave) machine that enables a computer to read and induce the human’s brainwaves.

Why do you want to astablish a humanupload for?
Like most medical research programs, we work for the sake of science. Our technology is already having a significant impact on bio-medical study. Many fields, including drug discovery and toxicological research, will certainly benefit from the creation of Braintec's first humanupload. Conservation of this instantaneous exposure might come in hand for new kinds of therapy as well.

How can I help your research?
If you join the BraintecGrid, your donation of spare computer cycles helps us to unravel the enormous amount of MEMES, to analyze this data and makes it possible to comprehend the first humanupload.

About the BraintecGrid

What is the BraintecGrid?
The BraintecGrid is a global grid that connects thousands of volunteers’ personal computers. The BraintecGrid saves and distributes the otherwise wasted power of these computers for our valuable scientific and medical research without disturbing the usual computer use of the volunteers.

Why did you build the BraintecGrid?
We require more computational power than is available in the largest existing supercomputer. With the BraintecGrid, we use thousands of small computers, all working simultaneously on different parts of the analysis. With this attempt to use large-scale distributed computing power we will analyze the most sensitive scans ever done.

My personal computer has only very little computing power compared to a supercomputer. How can it be at any help?
It can certainly be helpful! We break up the large datasets into small chunks that are essentially independent of one another. In this way, we can distribute the work to people willing to donate their spare CPU cycles. Even your personal computer can analyze these chunks or work-units comparatively quickly, no matter how slow or how fast it is.

Why should I join the BraintecGrid?
A great amount of computer power is necessary to comprehend the first humanupload. Without the help of thousands of volunteers’ personal computers worldwide we would never get the job done, which is why we need you (and your friends!) to become part of the BraintecGrid. If you join the BraintecGrid, you can start making a difference.

How do I join the BraintecGrid?
All you need to do is download the free software. Once that has been done, your computer is automatically put to work when you don't use it.

About the Humanupload Program

What is the Humanupload Program?
The Humanupload Program is a small program that works as a special kind of screensaver. We invite you to download this free software and become part of the BraintecGrid.

How does the Humanupload Program works?
 Like other screensavers it starts up when you leave your computer unattended, and it shuts down as soon as you return to work. What it does in the interim is unique. While you are getting coffee, having lunch or are asleep, your computer helps unravelling the memories and thoughts of our testsubjects. The Humanupload Program automatically downloads small pieces of data from our main server and performs calculations that analyze. After your computer processed the information, the results are sent back to the Braintec Institute. 

What do I see when the Humanupload Program is running?
The screensaver shows several pie charts and graphics showing the progress and history of the analyzed chunks.

How long does it takes to analyze a chunk of data?
Depending on the individual work unit's parameters, this processing loop requires 2.4 to 3.8 trillion floating-point operations (Tflop). It takes a typical (500 MHz) home computer 10 to 12 hours to complete a work unit.

What computers can run the Humanupload Program?
Currently, any Internet-connected Windows PC with a Pentium 133 or faster processor, and 64MB of RAM or more, and Internet Explorer 5 or higher installed. As we embark on Phase II, we will be adding other popular platforms like Mac OS X and Linux.

Will I need to do anything after I download and install the free software?
One of the beautiful things about the free desktop application is that it will run on its own.

Will the Humanupload Program interfere with my normal work?
No, it will not. As soon as you start using your computer again, the Humanupload Program shuts down. You won't even notice it's there waiting to solve one of the world's most complex problems.

Will I lose control of my dial-up?
You will never lose control of your dial-up or network connection or any other part of your system.

Do I need to leave my computer on all the time to run the Humanupload Program?
You are not required to leave your computer on. However, the more time your computer is running, the more time it spends working on the humanupload.

How do I set my default screen saver?
In the Control Panel, double click Display to bring up the Display Properties dialog. Select the Screen Saver tab. There you will see a list of available screensavers from which to select.

How do I completely get rid of Humanupload Program?
You can deactivate the "BraintecGrid" screensaver in the Control Panel "Display" applet. And you can completely remove the program by deleting the "BraintecGrid.scr" file in the Windows folder.

Privacy and safety

How do I know my privacy is guaranteed?
Your privacy is of paramount importance to us, and to that end the Humanupload Project will never gather any information from you without your consent. The Braintec Institute respects your privacy and will take reasonable measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of your personal information.

Can I use Windows screensaver password protection with the Humanupload Program?
Yes.

Do I need to change my firewall settings?
 No, you don't. Communications between clients and the server use the hypertext transfer protocol (HTTP). We chose this protocol because many Internet volunteers might be behind firewalls that prohibit most traffic but permit access to the World Wide Web.

Is it possible that the screensaver contains viruses that can harm my computer?
Braintec guarantees that the Humanupload Program is completely virus free. However, Braintec cannot be held responsible for any damage resulting from the installation or the use of our software. 

Are you using or selling my email address for other purposes?
We treat our members with the highest level of personal respect: we don't open members up to security risks, unwanted spam, or invasion of privacy.
As in any voluntary organization, it's important that Braintec is responsive to the desires of its volunteers, because the program's success depends entirely on the volunteers who provide the computing resources. Therefore we will continue to keep our volunteers informed of our progress.

What's in it for me?
Simply, the satisfaction of knowing you are harnessing the idle resources of your computer to assist us in our endeavours to analyze the first humanupload. We couldn't do it without you.
