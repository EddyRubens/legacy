What will happen if you become a test subject for Braintec?
The research program will take four weeks if you follow the MSI-program or up to six weeks if you are eligible for a MESI-implant. During this period of time you will undergo many physical and mental tests. A close team of specialists, nurses and medical assistants will often examine your blood, urine, vital functions and brain activity to spot your response to the implant and the memories. You will get a gross fee of $5240 for the MSI-program or $7860 for the MESI-program. Travel costs will be fully refunded.

We will pick you up from the airport or the train station; depending on your way of transport. It is also possible to drive to the Research Center by car.
Click here to find your way to the Braintec Research Center

Soon after your arrival, you will get a tour through the building together with our other new test subjects. At the end of this tour we will announce the assignment of the rooms. Most probably you will share your room with another test subject. In very exceptional cases we will place three participants in one room. Every room has a bathroom with toilet and shower. You can retreat to your room until evening dinner. This will be the only organized dinner. After that, you will have to arrange your own meals. We can provide you with a list of caterers, if you wish to have meals catered at the Center. But it is also possible to use our kitchen to cook yourself.
Click here to find a list of restaurants nearby  

To make your stay a pleasant one, we have organized several recreation rooms, some coffee corners and a small fitness hall. Inside the recreation room you can play table tennis or explore other activities. We have a living room as well, where you can watch television. An outdoor swimming pool is in the planning. You may only leave our Research Center under supervision of a team member. Unfortunately that strict rule is necessary to keep our research reliable.

You will be tested intensively during your first two days to be sure you’re suitable as a test subject. If the results of these tests are sufficient you will undergo a hypnotic session to test your suitability for a MESI-implant. If so, you will stay two extra weeks at Braintec concerning the extractions and a longer convalescence. You will leave your personal room for a couple of days to be admitted to the left wing of the Research Center. There your will be operated and stay for a few days to recover.

After a full recovery you will recall several memories. If you follow the MESI-program you will also have some of your memories extracted and re-implanted. During your last week at Braintec you will stay in the left wing again to have your implant surgically removed. You will leave the Research Center as healthy as you were on the day you checked in.

One month after your participation you will come back for one day to undergo the last tests.
